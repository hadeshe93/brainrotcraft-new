## What is Geometry Dash Fingerdash?

Fingerdash is the 21st official level in the rhythm-based action platformer, Geometry Dash, serving as the epic conclusion to the game's original progression. Available within the main Geometry Dash game on iOS, Android, and Steam, this level is a rite of passage for seasoned players. Your objective is to navigate a perilous, lava-themed course by tapping in sync with the high-energy electronic track "Fingerbang" by MDK. The level is infamous for introducing the Spider game mode, a challenging new mechanic that tests your reflexes in entirely new ways. With its stunning red-and-black visuals, pulsing hazards, and a relentless pace, Fingerdash is designed for players who have honed their skills and are ready for a definitive test. Completing it, and finding its three secret coins, is a badge of honor in the Geometry Dash community.

## How to Play Geometry Dash Fingerdash?

The core of Geometry Dash Fingerdash is timing. You control your icon with a single inputâ€”a tap on the screen or a click of the mouse. This action makes your icon jump, fly, or use a vehicle's specific ability. The level automatically scrolls, and your goal is to survive by avoiding spikes, saws, and other deadly hazards.

Fingerdash cycles you through various game modes, each with unique controls:
- **Cube/Robot:** Tap to jump.
- **Ship/UFO:** Tap and hold to fly up, release to descend.
- **Wave:** Tap and hold to move diagonally up, release to move diagonally down.
- **Spider:** This is the key new mechanic. A single tap instantly teleports the Spider to the opposite surface (ceiling or floor). Unlike the Ball, it's an instantaneous switch, requiring precise and quick taps rather than rolling momentum.

To succeed, use Practice Mode. This allows you to place checkpoints and master difficult sections without starting over. Focus on memorizing the layout, especially the portal transitions that change your vehicle, speed, or gravity. For the Spider sections, practice short, sharp taps to understand its instant teleportation and navigate tight corridors.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How hard is Geometry Dash Fingerdash?

Fingerdash is officially rated as an "Insane" level with a 12-star difficulty. It represents a significant challenge, requiring mastery of all previous game modes plus the brand-new Spider. Expect a steep learning curve, precise timing, and lots of practice to succeed.

### Where can I play Geometry Dash Fingerdash?

Fingerdash is the 21st official level within the full version of Geometry Dash. You can play it by purchasing the game on PC (via Steam), the App Store (iOS), or the Google Play Store (Android). It is not available in the free "Lite" versions of the game.

### What's the best tip for controlling the new Spider mode?

The key to the Spider is understanding its instant teleportation. A single tap immediately flips it to the opposite surface. Do not hold the button. Practice in short, controlled bursts in Practice Mode to get a feel for its quick, sharp movements, which are very different from the Ball's rolling gravity switch.

### How do I find the 3 secret coins in Fingerdash?

The secret coins are located on difficult alternate paths. The first (42%) and third (79%) coins are found in Spider sections, requiring you to tap into a hidden passage. The second coin (55%) is in a very tight corridor during a Ship sequence. Use Practice Mode to safely find and master these routes.
