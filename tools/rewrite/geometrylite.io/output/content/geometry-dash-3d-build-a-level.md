## What is Geometry Dash 3D: Build a level?

Geometry Dash 3D: Build a Level is a fan-made masterpiece that brilliantly reimagines the iconic rhythm-platformer in a fully three-dimensional space. Developed by Quix, this web-based game shifts the classic 2D side-scrolling action into a dynamic 3D world, offering a fresh perspective and a new layer of challenge. The game is a creative sandbox at its core, built around two main pillars: building and playing. Your primary objective is to use the intuitive level editor to design your own intricate, challenging, or visually stunning stages. Then, you can test your own creations or jump into an endless library of levels crafted by a global community of players. Designed for both veterans of the original series and creative newcomers, Geometry Dash 3D is perfect for anyone who has ever wanted to not just beat the level, but to be the architect behind the madness.

## How to Play Geometry Dash 3D: Build a level?

Mastering Geometry Dash 3D means getting comfortable in its two distinct modes: Play Mode and Build Mode.

**Play Mode - Conquer the Challenge**
This is where your reflexes are tested. The goal is to navigate your cube to the end of the level by avoiding obstacles.

- **Controls**: Use the **Spacebar** or a **Left Mouse Click** to jump. The timing is just as critical as in the original, but now you must also account for 3D space.
- **Mechanics**: Judge distances and alignments in a 3D environment. Use shadows and perspective to time your jumps over spikes, pits, and other hazards. The feeling is familiar, but the gameplay is entirely new.

**Build Mode - Unleash Your Creativity**
This is your canvas. Craft levels ranging from simple obstacle courses to complex, artistic worlds.

- **Camera Controls**: Move the camera with **WASD** or the **Arrow Keys**. Hold the **Right Mouse Button** and drag to rotate your view and build from any angle.
- **Building**: Select blocks, spikes, jump pads, and other elements from the menu. **Left-click** on the grid to place an object. You can easily switch, rotate, and delete objects to perfect your design.

**Beginner Tips**:

1.  **Start Small**: Build a short, simple level first to learn the editor's controls.
2.  **Test Constantly**: Use the instant 'Test' feature to play your level as you build. This helps you fine-tune difficulty and flow.
3.  **Study Others**: Play levels made by other players to get inspiration for new traps and design ideas.
4.  **Use Shadows**: In Play Mode, your cube's shadow is your best friend for judging your position above platforms.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Geometry Dash 3D an official game?

No, this is a free, fan-made game created by a developer named Quix. It is a tribute to the official Geometry Dash series by RobTop Games but is not affiliated with them. It's a passion project built for the community.

### How do I control the camera in Build Mode?

Navigating the 3D space is key to building. Use the **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to move your camera position horizontally. To change your viewing angle, **hold the Right Mouse Button** and drag your mouse to rotate the camera around the build area.

### Is this game harder than the original Geometry Dash?

For many players, yes. While the core jump mechanic is the same, the addition of the third dimension introduces a significant challenge in judging depth and distance. It requires a different kind of spatial awareness, which can be a steep learning curve even for seasoned players.

### Can I play Geometry Dash 3D on my phone?

Geometry Dash 3D is primarily designed as a web-based game to be played on a PC or Mac with a keyboard and mouse. While it might load on a mobile browser, the controls are not optimized for touchscreens, and performance may be poor. For the best experience, play on a desktop.

### How do I share my levels with friends?

After building and testing your level, there is typically an option to generate a unique level code or link. You can copy this code and share it with friends, who can then import it into their game to play your creation. This is the core multiplayer aspect of the game.
