## What is Geometry Dash Ashley Wave Trials?

Geometry Dash Ashley Wave Trials is not a standalone game, but a legendary user-created level within the rhythm-based platformer, Geometry Dash. Created by OddMod and verified by top player Zoink, it’s an Extreme Demon, placing it among the most difficult challenges ever made. The main objective is to navigate your icon through a nearly two-minute gauntlet composed almost entirely of the 'wave' gamemode. Players must survive incredibly tight corridors and rapid-fire speed changes, all synchronized to a high-energy electronic track. Despite its brutal difficulty, the level features a unique, light-hearted aesthetic with a pink and red color palette and anime-style character art. It’s designed for elite Geometry Dash players who want to test their wave skills against a top-tier challenge recognized on the official Demonlist.

## How to Play Geometry Dash Ashley Wave Trials?

Mastering Ashley Wave Trials boils down to perfecting one-button control with immense precision. The level is played almost exclusively in the wave gamemode.

**Basic Controls:**
- **PC:** Click and hold the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make your wave icon travel upwards at a 45-degree angle.
- **Mobile:** Tap and hold the screen to ascend.
- **Mechanic:** Releasing the button or your finger causes the wave to descend at a 45-degree angle. The entire level is a delicate dance of holding and releasing to navigate razor-thin passages.

**Game Mechanics & Progression:**
Progression is about learning and mastering the level section by section. The core mechanics involve surviving tight wave corridors, reacting to gravity portals that flip you upside down, and adapting instantly to speed changes (from normal speed to 4x speed and back again). Since this is a single, continuous level, there is no formal progression system other than your own percentage completion.

**Beginner Tips (for this level):**
1.  **Use Practice Mode:** This is essential. Practice Mode allows you to place automatic checkpoints to learn the patterns in small, manageable chunks.
2.  **Find a 'Copyable':** Search for a copyable version of the level online. This lets you place a start position anywhere, allowing you to drill the hardest sections repeatedly.
3.  **Master Micro-Clicks:** The level demands tiny, precise adjustments. Practice short, quick clicks and releases to maintain a steady path in narrow spaces.
4.  **Learn the Music:** The song, "Ashley" by t+pazolite, is perfectly synced to the gameplay. Learning the rhythm can help you internalize the click patterns.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difficulty of Ashley Wave Trials?

It is an Extreme Demon, one of the highest difficulty ratings in Geometry Dash. Upon its verification, it was placed #15 on the Pointercrate Demonlist, a ranked list of the hardest levels. It remains a formidable challenge requiring elite skill and thousands of attempts to beat.

### How can I play Ashley Wave Trials?

It is a user-created level within the official Geometry Dash game (on Steam for PC/Mac and on the App Store/Google Play for mobile). You can find it by searching for the level ID '91349125' in the online level browser. It can also be played on various fan-made web versions of the game.

### What's the best way to practice this level?

Use Practice Mode to learn the click patterns with checkpoints. For more focused training, find a 'copyable' version of the level by searching online. This allows you to place a start position anywhere, which is crucial for mastering the hardest wave sections without replaying the entire level.

### Who created and verified Ashley Wave Trials?

The level was created and designed by the talented creator OddMod. It was officially verified (beaten for the first time to prove it's possible) by the legendary top player Zoink, which helped solidify its status as an iconic Extreme Demon.
