## What is Obby With Friends: Draw and Jump?

Welcome to Obby With Friends: Draw and Jump, the ultimate test of your creativity and reflexes! This browser-based multiplayer game reinvents the classic obby (obstacle course) genre by putting the power of creation directly in your hands. Instead of just jumping on pre-set platforms, your main objective is to guide your character, who automatically runs forward, through treacherous levels filled with deadly gaps and traps. How? By using your mouse to draw platforms, ramps, and bridges in real-time. Designed for fans of creative challenges like Roblox obbies and chaotic party games, this game is perfect for playing solo to hone your skills or teaming up with friends for hilarious, unpredictable multiplayer action. Whether you're a seasoned parkour pro or a doodler at heart, Obby With Friends offers a fresh, engaging experience where every line you draw can lead to victory or a spectacular fall.

## How to Play Obby With Friends: Draw and Jump?

Getting started is simple, but mastery is a true challenge! Your character moves forward automatically, so your entire focus is on creating a safe path for them.

**Controls:**

- **Draw:** Click and hold the **Left Mouse Button** to draw lines on the screen. These instantly become solid platforms.
- **Camera:** In some versions, you can use the **Right Mouse Button** or **WASD/Arrow Keys** to move the camera and plan your path ahead.

**Gameplay Mechanics:**
The core of the game is its physics-based drawing. A line drawn too long or unevenly might tilt and collapse, sending you into the void! You must constantly draw new platforms and ramps to navigate over gaps, block hazards, and reach the finish line. Most versions feature a regenerating 'ink' meter, limiting how much you can draw at once and forcing you to be efficient with your lines.

**Beginner Tips:**

- **Draw Short & Stable:** Start with short, thick lines for better stability.
- **Think Ahead:** Always try to draw slightly ahead of your character to give yourself time to react.
- **Use Your Friends:** In multiplayer, you can use your friends' drawings to your advantage—or playfully sabotage them!
- **Embrace the Chaos:** Don't worry about falling. Each attempt helps you learn the level's layout and timing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I play Obby With Friends on my phone or tablet?

Yes! Obby With Friends is a browser-based game, making it accessible on most devices with a modern web browser, including PCs, tablets, and smartphones. However, for the best control and precision when drawing, playing with a mouse on a desktop computer is often recommended. The experience on a touchscreen is playable but can be more challenging.

### How does the multiplayer mode work?

In multiplayer, you and other players join the same level simultaneously. You can see each other's characters and, most importantly, all the lines everyone draws in real-time. This allows for both cooperation—like building a bridge together—and chaotic competition. Be careful, as a poorly placed line from another player can easily get in your way! It's a fun, frantic race to the finish.

### Is this game hard for beginners?

The game is easy to learn but can be surprisingly challenging to master! The concept of drawing is simple, but the auto-running character and physics require quick thinking. Early levels are designed to ease you in, but the difficulty ramps up. Don't worry if you fall a lot at first—it's part of the fun and the learning process!

### Are there different levels or is it an endless runner?

Obby With Friends is a level-based game. Your goal is to successfully navigate your character from the start to the finish flag of each distinct stage. After completing a level, you will automatically proceed to the next one, which typically introduces new challenges and a more complex layout to test your drawing skills.
