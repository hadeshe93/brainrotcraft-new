Ascend a haunted kingdom as the princess-protector, Hornet. Master dazzling acrobatic combat and craft deadly tools to unravel a mystery woven in silk and song.

## What is Hollow Knight: Silksong?

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the highly anticipated action-adventure sequel to the award-winning Hollow Knight. As a Metroidvania, the game challenges you to explore a vast, interconnected world, defeating enemies and acquiring new abilities to unlock previously inaccessible areas. You play as Hornet, a swift and graceful warrior, who finds herself captured and brought to the haunted kingdom of Pharloom. Your main objective is to ascend from the depths to the kingdom's shining citadel, uncovering why you were brought to this strange new land. Silksong is designed for fans of challenging combat, deep exploration, and rich atmospheric storytelling. While the game does not yet have a confirmed release date, it is planned for launch on PC (Windows, Mac, & Linux), Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X|S. It will also be available on Xbox Game Pass from day one.

## How to Play Hollow Knight: Silksong?

In Silksong, you'll master Hornet's unique and agile moveset, which is significantly faster and more acrobatic than the Knight's from the first game. Your primary weapon is your trusty Needle, used for quick slashes and lunges. Unlike the Knight, Hornet does not collect Soul to heal. Instead, she uses Silk gathered from enemies to Bind her wounds. This is a fast action that can heal multiple masks at once, but it consumes your entire spool of Silk, encouraging strategic use.

Progression is tied to exploration and combat. You will traverse a massive new world with over 150 new enemies and formidable bosses. Defeating them and discovering secrets will grant you new powers, such as a higher jump or a lightning-fast dash, allowing you to navigate Pharloom's diverse environments. A major new mechanic is crafting. You'll collect Shell Shards from fallen foes to craft a variety of tools, weapons, and traps—like Pimpillo Bombs and Sting Shards—to customize your playstyle and gain an edge in battle. Pay attention to quest boards in towns; these offer tasks from NPCs that grant rewards and flesh out the world's lore. For beginners, focus on mastering Hornet's mobility first—her speed and aerial prowess are your greatest assets.

## What Makes Hollow Knight: Silksong Special?

Hollow Knight: Silksong sets itself apart with Hornet's incredible agility. The gameplay is built around fast, acrobatic combat and fluid movement, creating a distinct feel from the more deliberate pace of the original. The all-new crafting system adds a deep layer of strategic customization, allowing you to adapt your tools to different enemies and situations. You're not just finding upgrades; you're building your arsenal. The setting, the kingdom of Pharloom, offers a new visual palette, trading the solemn gloom of Hallownest for a world of gilded cities, vibrant coral forests, and misty moors. Finally, Silksong introduces a more structured quest system, with tasks posted on notice boards. This gives players clearer goals to pursue alongside the main journey of ascension, making the world feel more alive and interactive. For veterans, a challenging 'Silk Soul Mode' awaits after completing the game, offering a remixed and tougher experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When will Hollow Knight: Silksong be released?

There is currently no official release date for Hollow Knight: Silksong. The developer, Team Cherry, has confirmed the game is in the final stages of development but has asked for patience to ensure the game meets their high-quality standards. Keep an eye on their official channels for the latest news.

### What platforms will Silksong be available on?

Hollow Knight: Silksong is confirmed for a wide release. It will be available on PC (Windows, Mac, Linux), Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X|S. It will also be a day-one title on Xbox Game Pass.

### Do I need to play the first Hollow Knight to understand Silksong?

While it's highly recommended to play Hollow Knight to appreciate the world's lore and the evolution of the gameplay, it is not strictly necessary. Silksong features a new kingdom, a new protagonist, and a self-contained story, making it accessible to newcomers.

### How difficult will Silksong be compared to the original?

Silksong is expected to be a challenging game, in line with the original. However, Hornet's increased mobility and the new crafting system may provide players with more options to tackle difficult bosses and platforming sections. The developers aim for a tough but fair experience for all players.
