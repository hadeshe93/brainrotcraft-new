## What is Geometry Dash End Of Line?

Geometry Dash End Of Line is a fan-favorite user-created level by PMK, celebrated for its unique style and satisfying gameplay. As a 3-star Normal difficulty level, it serves as the closing challenge in the official Beginner Pack. Your objective is to navigate a geometric icon through a series of synchronized obstacles, surviving until the very end. The level is set to the energetic track "End of Line" by F-777, and every jump, flight, and gravity switch is timed to the beat. Its iconic gray-scale aesthetic, inspired by the official level "Theory of Everything," creates a cool, atmospheric experience. Built for players who have mastered the basics and are ready for a more polished challenge, End Of Line introduces multiple game modes—including cube, ship, ball, and wave—in a smooth, non-frustrating way. It’s the perfect test of your growing skills before you move on to harder levels.

## How to Play Geometry Dash End Of Line?

Mastering End Of Line is all about rhythm and control. The game uses a simple one-touch input system: tap the screen or click your mouse to jump. Different game modes change how this control works.

- **Cube:** A simple tap makes you jump over spikes and obstacles.
- **Ship:** Tap and hold to fly upward; release to descend. Master short, controlled taps to maintain a steady altitude.
- **Ball:** A tap instantly switches your gravity, sending you from the floor to the ceiling and back again.
- **Wave:** Tap and hold to move diagonally up; release to move diagonally down, creating a zigzag pattern.

Progression is linear—your goal is to reach 100%. If you hit an obstacle, you restart from the beginning. To avoid frustration, use Practice Mode. This allows you to place checkpoints and master difficult sections without starting over.

**Beginner Tips:**

1.  **Listen to the Music:** The track by F-777 isn't just background noise; it provides audio cues for when to jump and transition.
2.  **Learn the Paths:** The level has a great flow. Play it a few times to memorize the layout and anticipate the changes between game modes.
3.  **Stay Calm in the Wave:** The wave section can be tricky for new players. Focus on small, smooth movements rather than sharp, sudden taps.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How difficult is Geometry Dash End Of Line?

End Of Line is officially rated as a Normal difficulty level with 3 stars. It's designed as an accessible challenge for players who have some experience with the game. The difficulty comes from learning the rhythm and paths, not from extreme precision or speed.

### How do I find the three secret coins?

The coins reward exploration. The first (31%) is in the ship section; drop down to a hidden lower path. The second (50%) is in the ball section; hit an orb late to fall onto a secret platform. The third (82%) is in the mini-wave section; navigate through a narrow passage above the main path.

### What platforms can I play End Of Line on?

As a user-created level, End Of Line is available within the full Geometry Dash game on PC (Steam), macOS, iOS, and Android. It is also a featured level on many free-to-play web browser versions of the game, making it easily accessible to everyone.

### What is the song used in the level?

The level is built around the high-energy track "End of Line" by the artist F-777. The music is a core part of the experience, and its beat is synchronized with the gameplay obstacles and transitions, serving as a guide for the player.
