## How to Play Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge 3?

Mastering Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge 3 is all about precision and rhythm. The controls are deceptively simple: **hold down the left mouse button or the spacebar to make your wave travel upwards diagonally, and release it to travel downwards diagonally.** Your wave is in constant forward motion, so your only job is to navigate the vertical space. The core mechanic is survival. You must guide the wave through incredibly tight and complex passages without touching any of the neon walls or spikes. A single collision means starting over from the beginning.

Progression in this single-level challenge is about memorization and muscle memory. Use the **Practice Mode** to your advantage. This mode allows you to place checkpoints, so you can break down the level into smaller, more manageable sections.

**Beginner Tips:**
*   **Small Inputs are Key:** Avoid holding or releasing for too long. The high speed demands tiny, precise adjustments to maintain control.
*   **Learn the Music:** The level is synced to the soundtrack. Pay attention to the beat drops and melody changes, as they often signal a shift in the obstacle pattern.
*   **Stay Centered:** Try to keep your wave near the center of the path whenever possible. This gives you more room to react to sudden vertical shifts.
*   **Don't Panic:** The fast pace can be overwhelming. Take deep breaths and focus on one section at a time. Getting frustrated will only lead to more mistakes.
