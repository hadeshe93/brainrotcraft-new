Mastering Speed Stars is about precision, not power. Forget frantic tapping—this game is a test of pure rhythm. Here’s how to become a track legend.

*   **Left / Right Arrow Keys:** Use these keys to run. The secret is to alternate them in a steady, consistent beat.

**Core Mechanics:**
1.  **The Perfect Start:** The race begins with a "Ready, Set..." signal. Time your first key press (either Left or Right) to coincide exactly with "GO!" to burst off the starting blocks with maximum acceleration.
2.  **Finding Your Cadence:** As you run, a **Rhythm Bar** will appear on screen. Your goal is to keep the indicator in the green "Perfect" zone by tapping the arrow keys at the optimal tempo. Tapping too fast or too slow will push the indicator into the red, causing your athlete to lose form, stumble, and slow down. Listen to the rhythmic footfall sound effects—they are your best guide.
3.  **Maintaining Momentum:** Longer races like the 200m and 400m test your ability to maintain this perfect rhythm over time. Stay focused and don't break your cadence, especially as fatigue sets in.

**Progression & Beginner Tips:**
Start with the "Quick Race" mode to practice the 100m dash. At first, ignore the other runners and focus solely on keeping your rhythm bar in the green. Once you can consistently win, enter the "Championship" mode to compete in a series of events against progressively faster AI. Your first goal should be to find a comfortable, steady rhythm, even if it feels slow. You can gradually increase your tapping speed while ensuring you never hit the red zone.
