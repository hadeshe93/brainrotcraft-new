## What is Geometry Platformer?

Geometry Platformer is a challenging 2D precision platformer that tests your reflexes and patience. Played directly in your web browser, this skill-based game places you in control of a simple cube with a monumental task: navigate treacherous levels filled with geometric traps. The main objective is to guide your cube from the start of each stage to the exit portal, surviving an onslaught of spikes, spinning saws, and moving obstacles. Combining a minimalist neon aesthetic with tough-as-nails gameplay, Geometry Platformer is designed for players who love a satisfying challenge. If you're a fan of hardcore platformers like Super Meat Boy or enjoy the visual style of Geometry Dash but crave more direct control over your character's movement, you'll feel right at home in this unforgiving but rewarding geometric world.

## How to Play Geometry Platformer?

Mastering Geometry Platformer is all about precise control and learning from your mistakes. Unlike auto-runners, you have complete freedom to move, stop, and strategize your next jump.

**Core Controls:**

- **Move Left/Right:** Use the `A` and `D` keys or the `Left` and `Right Arrow` keys.
- **Jump:** Press the `W` key or the `Up Arrow` key. The longer you hold the jump button, the higher your cube will go, so mastering short hops and full leaps is crucial.

**Gameplay Mechanics:**
Your goal is to reach the glowing green portal at the end of each level. Along the way, you'll encounter a variety of deadly hazards. Touching any red obstacle, like a spike or a blade, results in instant death and resets you to the beginning of the level. This 'one more try' loop is central to the experience, encouraging you to memorize patterns and perfect your timing. While your main goal is survival, you can also collect blue shapes scattered throughout the levels to increase your score.

**Beginner Tips:**

1.  **Patience Over Speed:** Don't rush. Stop and observe the patterns of moving obstacles before you commit to a jump.
2.  **Learn the Physics:** Get a feel for your cube's jump height and movement speed. Practice makes perfect.
3.  **Prioritize Survival:** Collecting blue shapes is optional. Focus on clearing the level first, then go back for a high score if you want an extra challenge.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Geometry Platformer the same as Geometry Dash?

No, they are different games. While they share a similar neon-geometric style, Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based auto-runner. Geometry Platformer gives you full control to move, stop, and jump freely, focusing on pure precision platforming skill.

### What devices can I play Geometry Platformer on?

Geometry Platformer is primarily a free-to-play browser game. You can play it on any device with a modern web browser and a keyboard, including Windows PCs, Macs, and Chromebooks. It is not officially available as a standalone mobile app.

### How difficult is this game?

The game is designed to be challenging! The controls are simple to learn, but the levels require precise timing and quick reflexes. Expect to fail often, but each attempt helps you learn the layout, making eventual success incredibly rewarding.

### Are there checkpoints in the levels?

Typically, no. If your cube hits an obstacle, you will instantly restart the current level from the beginning. This 'one more try' design is a core part of the challenge, pushing you to perfect your run from start to finish.
