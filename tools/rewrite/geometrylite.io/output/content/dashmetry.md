## What is Dashmetry?

Dashmetry is a free-to-play, browser-based rhythm platformer that takes heavy inspiration from the legendary Geometry Dash. It offers a thrilling, skill-based experience where precision and timing are everything. In this game, you control a geometric cube, navigating it through treacherous, obstacle-filled levels that are perfectly synchronized to electrifying music tracks. The main objective is simple to understand but difficult to master: survive from the start of the level to the end without crashing into spikes, saws, or other hazards. Built by fans for fans, Dashmetry is perfect for both hardened veterans of the genre seeking a new challenge and newcomers eager to test their reflexes. The game's core is its vibrant community, which constantly expands the experience by creating and sharing unique, imaginative levels, ensuring that you'll never run out of challenges to conquer.

## How to Play Dashmetry?

Mastering Dashmetry is all about muscle memory and rhythm. The controls are deceptively simple, relying on a single action to navigate complex environments.

**Core Controls:**
- **Jump:** Use the `Left Mouse Button`, `Spacebar`, or `Up Arrow Key` to make your cube jump over obstacles.

**Game Mechanics:**
Your journey will be punctuated by portals that transform your character, changing the controls and physics entirely:
- **Cube:** Standard single jumps.
- **Ship:** Hold the button to fly upwards, release to descend. Navigate tight corridors with controlled bursts.
- **Ball:** Tap the button to instantly switch gravity, sticking you to the ceiling or floor.
- **UFO:** Similar to the ship, but each tap results in a short, controlled hop, requiring rapid inputs for sustained flight.

**Progression & Tips for Beginners:**
Progression isn't about unlocking content, but personal skill. Create a free profile to save your progress, track your stats, and save your created levels. 

1.  **Start with Practice Mode:** This mode allows you to place checkpoints, letting you master difficult sections without starting over.
2.  **Feel the Beat:** The level's obstacles are synced to the music. Listen closely, as the rhythm often tells you exactly when to jump or act.
3.  **Browse Player Levels:** Don't jump into the hardest levels first. Sort by 'Easy' or 'Normal' difficulty and by 'Most Liked' to find well-designed, fun introductory courses.
4.  **Stay Calm:** Frustration is part of the process. Take breaks and come back to a tough section with a fresh perspective. Every crash is a learning opportunity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is Dashmetry different from the official Geometry Dash?

Dashmetry is a free, fan-made tribute that runs in your web browser, making it instantly accessible. Its key differentiators are a fully community-driven level library and the ability for players to upload their own music tracks for use in the level editor, offering unique creative freedom.

### Can I play Dashmetry on my phone or tablet?

While the game might load on a mobile browser, it is designed and optimized for desktop play with a mouse or keyboard. The precision and rapid inputs required are best experienced on a PC for optimal control and performance.

### Do I need an account to play or save my progress?

You can play guest levels without an account. However, creating a free private profile is highly recommended. It allows you to save your stats, keep track of completed levels, and most importantly, save and publish the levels you create in the editor.

### Is Dashmetry too difficult for a beginner?

It can be challenging, but it's very beginner-friendly! The game has a massive library of player-created levels with difficulty ratings. Start with levels marked 'Easy' or 'Normal' to learn the mechanics before tackling the 'Demon' rated challenges. Practice Mode is your best friend!
