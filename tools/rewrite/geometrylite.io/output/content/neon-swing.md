## What is Neon Swing?

Welcome to the vibrant, high-stakes world of Neon Swing! This is a fast-paced, 2D arcade skill game where reflexes and an understanding of physics are your keys to survival. You take control of a nimble stickman with one simple goal: swing from one anchor point to the next and reach the finish line. Played primarily in a web browser, it's the perfect game for quick, thrilling sessions. The core challenge lies in mastering your momentum as you navigate increasingly complex levels filled with static and moving obstacles. All the while, an ominous red wall constantly closes in from behind, adding a layer of urgency that will keep your heart pounding. Neon Swing is designed for players who love one-button challenges, physics-based puzzles, and addictive gameplay that constantly begs for 'just one more try'. If you enjoy titles like Stickman Hook, you'll feel right at home in this glowing acrobatic adventure.

## How to Play Neon Swing?

Getting into the swing of things is simple, but mastering the game takes practice. Your goal in each level is to get your stickman from the starting block to the checkered finish line.

**Basic Controls:**
- **PC/Mac:** Click and hold the left mouse button to shoot a rope to the nearest anchor point. Release the button to let go.
- **Mobile:** Tap and hold the screen to swing, and release your finger to detach.

**Core Mechanics:**
Your stickman automatically attaches to the closest anchor point in range when you click or tap. You don't control the direction, only the timing. Momentum is everything. To gain speed, let your stickman swing low in the arc. To gain height, release at the peak of your swing. Many levels feature special bouncy pads. Hitting these will launch your character, helping you cross large gaps or gain speed without needing to swing. As you progress, levels become more intricate, and the pursuing red wall gets faster, demanding quicker thinking and more precise maneuvers.

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Short Taps:** For small gaps, a quick tap-and-release is often better than a full swing.
2.  **Chain Your Swings:** Try to release and immediately attach to the next point to maintain a smooth, fast 'flow'.
3.  **Use the Bounce:** Don't ignore the bouncy pads! They are often strategically placed and essential for completing the level.

### How do I swing farther and get more speed?

Momentum is crucial! To swing farther and faster, let your stickman drop lower in the arc before releasing the rope. This builds up speed. To gain more height, release the rope at the very top of the swing's arc. Practice combining these techniques with bounces off pads to maintain high speed across the level.

### Is Neon Swing a difficult game?

Neon Swing features a well-balanced difficulty curve. The first few levels are very easy and act as a tutorial for the core mechanics. However, the challenge ramps up significantly in later stages, requiring precise timing, quick reflexes, and strategic planning. It's challenging but fair, encouraging players to learn from their mistakes and try again.

### Are there any special power-ups in the game?

The game focuses on pure skill, so there are no traditional collectible power-ups. However, levels incorporate special environmental features, most notably the bouncy pads. These pads act as trampolines, launching your stickman across the screen. Using them effectively is a key skill for clearing many of the game's toughest challenges.
