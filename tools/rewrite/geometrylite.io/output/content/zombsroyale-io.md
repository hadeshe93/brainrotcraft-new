## What is ZombsRoyale.io?

ZombsRoyale.io is an adrenaline-fueled 2D battle royale that throws you into a chaotic 100-player firefight. As a top-down shooter, it simplifies the genre to its core elements: looting, shooting, and survival. Available on web browsers, iOS, and Android, the game features full cross-platform play, so you can team up with friends no matter where they are. Your mission is simple: parachute onto a sprawling map, crack open chests for weapons and gear, and survive against 99 other players as a deadly gas cloud shrinks the battlefield. While some special modes feature zombies, the core experience is a pure, fast-paced player-vs-player showdown. Perfect for both casual players looking for quick matches and competitive veterans honing their skills, ZombsRoyale.io offers the intense thrill of a battle royale in a format that’s easy to learn but challenging to master.

## How to Play ZombsRoyale.io?

Mastering the battlefield is simple with intuitive controls. Use WASD to move, 'E' to interact with doors and chests, and your mouse to aim and shoot. Your game begins by parachuting from a plane. Pick a landing spot, then immediately start looting. Chests contain weapons, which are color-coded by rarity—from Common (gray) to Mythic (gold)—so always grab the upgrade! You'll also find Medkits to restore health and Potions to build up your shield, which acts as a crucial first line of defense against enemy fire. Stay ahead of the closing gas storm by checking your map ('M' or 'Tab') and planning your route. Unlike other battle royales, there's no building, so success depends entirely on your aim, movement, and tactical positioning. 

**Beginner Tips:**
- **Land Smart:** Avoid hot-drop locations at first. Land near the edge of the map to gear up in peace.
- **Shields First:** Prioritize shield potions. They can be used faster than medkits and absorb incoming damage.
- **Stay Mobile:** A moving target is hard to hit. Never stand still in the open and use trees, rocks, and buildings for cover.
