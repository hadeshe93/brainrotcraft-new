## How to Play Brainrot Animals Geometry Dash?

The goal in Brainrot Animals Geometry Dash is simple to understand but hard to master: survive the level from start to finish without crashing. Your character moves automatically from left to right, and your only control is the jump.

**Controls:**
- **PC:** Press the `Spacebar`, `Up Arrow`, or `Left-Click` your mouse to jump.
- **Mobile:** Simply `tap` anywhere on the screen to jump.

**Game Mechanics:**
Your task is to time your jumps perfectly to leap over spikes, platforms, and an onslaught of meme-animal obstacles. Hitting any object will instantly reset you to the beginning of the level. The key is to learn the level's layout and synchronize your jumps to the beat of the frantic, meme-filled soundtrack. The rhythm isn't just for fun—it’s a crucial clue for timing your moves.

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Use Practice Mode:** Most versions of the game include a 'Practice Mode'. This allows you to place checkpoints, so you can master difficult sections without restarting from 0% every time.
2.  **Listen to the Music:** The beat drops and musical cues often align perfectly with required jumps. Turn up the volume and let the rhythm guide you.
3.  **Stay Calm and Persistent:** You will fail—a lot. This is a trial-and-error game. Each failure is a lesson in the level's timing. Don't get frustrated; embrace the absurdity.
4.  **Look Ahead:** Focus on the obstacles coming up, not just your icon. Anticipating the next sequence is essential for survival.
