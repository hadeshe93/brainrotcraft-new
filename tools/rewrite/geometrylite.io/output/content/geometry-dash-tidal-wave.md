## How to Play Geometry Dash Tidal Wave?

The controls for Tidal Wave are the same as standard Geometry Dash: a single input (click, spacebar, or screen tap) is all you have. The challenge lies in the timing. The level is infamous for its wave gameplay, which dominates the first 70% of the experience. In wave mode, holding the input makes your icon travel diagonally up, while releasing makes it travel diagonally down. Tidal Wave demands inhumanly precise and sustained control to navigate razor-thin corridors at blinding speeds. 

There is no checkpoint or progression system in a normal run; you must complete the level from 0% to 100% in one attempt. Your real progression comes from using Practice Mode. Here are some essential tips for learning the level:

1.  **Master Practice Mode:** This is non-negotiable. Use it to learn the patterns for every section. Place checkpoints frequently to drill difficult timings.
2.  **Use a Copyable Version:** Search for a 'copyable' version of the level online. This allows you to use start positions to practice any part of the level in isolation, which is crucial for a level this long.
3.  **Study the Pros:** Watch the verification video by Zoink and other top players' completions. Observe their click patterns and the path they take through the tightest gaps.
4.  **Build Your Endurance:** A three-minute Extreme Demon is a marathon. Practice long runs (e.g., 40-80%) to build the mental stamina needed to stay focused under pressure.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How difficult is Geometry Dash Tidal Wave really?

It is officially the hardest rated level in the game, holding the #1 position on the Pointercrate Demonlist. It requires tens of thousands of attempts for even the world's most skilled players to complete. It represents the absolute peak of difficulty in Geometry Dash.

### Can I play Tidal Wave on my phone?

Yes. As a user-created level, Tidal Wave can be played on any platform with the full version of Geometry Dash, including iOS and Android. However, due to the extreme precision required, most top players strongly prefer playing on PC with a high refresh rate monitor.

### What is a 'Nine Circles' level and how is Tidal Wave related?

A 'Nine Circles' level is a style known for its intense, epilepsy-inducing wave sections with a signature flashing color effect. While Tidal Wave's heavy focus on wave gameplay is inspired by this style, its decoration, complexity, and gameplay variety are considered far more advanced and modern.

### Who are OniLink and Zoink?

OniLink is the brilliant creator who designed and built the level's layout and stunning visuals. Zoink is a legendary top player who was the first person to successfully beat the level from start to finish, thereby 'verifying' it and releasing it for the public to play.
