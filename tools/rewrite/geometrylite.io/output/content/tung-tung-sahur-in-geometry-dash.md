## What is Tung Tung Sahur in Geometry Dash?

Tung Tung Sahur in Geometry Dash is a viral fan-made level that has taken the community by storm, now playable as a standalone browser game. Inspired by the Indonesian meme of waking people for the pre-dawn meal (Sahur) during Ramadan, this game transforms the meme's rhythmic chant into an electrifying challenge. It's a rhythm-based platformer focused exclusively on the 'wave' game mode. Your objective is to guide the iconic Tung Tung Sahur character, a glowing figure with his drum, through a treacherous neon cityscape just before sunrise. You'll navigate tight, spike-filled corridors that pulse and shift to the beat. This game is perfect for Geometry Dash veterans who love a pure wave challenge and rhythm game enthusiasts looking for a test of reflex and timing. It's a brilliant fusion of internet culture and hardcore gameplay, accessible to anyone with a web browser.

## How to Play Tung Tung Sahur in Geometry Dash?

Mastering Tung Tung Sahur is all about precise control and rhythm. The gameplay is focused entirely on the wave mechanic, where your icon moves in a zigzag pattern.

**Controls:**

- **PC:** Hold the left mouse button or spacebar to make your wave ascend. Release to descend.
- **Mobile:** Tap and hold the screen to ascend. Release your finger to descend.

The controls are simple, but the level is unforgiving. Your goal is to survive from start to finish by maneuvering through incredibly narrow passages lined with spikes. The level features frequent gravity portals that flip the screen and your controls upside down (hold to descend, release to ascend), and speed changes that force you to adjust your reaction time instantly.

**Beginner Tips:**

1.  **Stay Calm:** Panic leads to over-correction. Try to make small, smooth adjustments rather than drastic movements.
2.  **Use the Beat:** The level's obstacles are synchronized to the 'tung-tung' music. Listen closely to the rhythm to anticipate turns and tight squeezes.
3.  **Focus Ahead:** Keep your eyes slightly ahead of your icon to prepare for upcoming patterns instead of reacting at the last second.
4.  **Practice Sections:** If you're playing the level within Geometry Dash, use Practice Mode to learn the patterns of difficult sections, especially the gravity portal transitions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Tung Tung Sahur an official Geometry Dash game?

No, it is a highly popular fan-made level created by a player named LLiSaMe within Geometry Dash (Level ID: 105634567). Its massive popularity has led to it being ported as a standalone game playable for free in a web browser on various sites.

### How difficult is Tung Tung Sahur?

In the Geometry Dash community, the level is rated as an 'Easy Demon.' For new or casual players, this means it is very challenging and will require significant practice. For experienced players, it's a fun but demanding test of wave control and consistency.

### What platforms can I play this on?

You can play the original level within the full version of Geometry Dash on PC (Steam) and mobile (iOS/Android). Additionally, you can easily find and play a free, standalone version in your web browser on a PC, Mac, or mobile device.

### Any tips for the gravity portal sections?

When you hit a gravity portal, your controls invert. The key is to anticipate the switch and mentally prepare. Instead of thinking 'up' or 'down,' think 'pull towards the floor' or 'pull towards the ceiling.' Practicing these transitions repeatedly is the best way to build muscle memory.
