## What is Melon Sandbox?

Melon Sandbox is a creative physics-based sandbox game where your imagination is the only limit. Played directly in your browser, this game drops you into an open 2D world with a powerful toolkit of characters, weapons, and objects. At its core, Melon Sandbox is less of a game and more of a digital playground. There are no missions, scores, or time limits; the main objective is to experiment. You can build elaborate structures, design wild contraptions, or create chaotic battle scenes between ragdoll characters. It's designed for players who love creative freedom, emergent gameplay, and seeing how things interact in a dynamic physics engine. Whether you want to tell a story, conduct a bizarre experiment, or just blow things up for fun, Melon Sandbox provides all the tools you need. It’s perfect for casual players looking for a relaxing yet engaging way to express their creativity and for fans of simulation games who enjoy testing the boundaries of a virtual world.

## How to Play Melon Sandbox?

Getting started in Melon Sandbox is incredibly simple. You begin on a blank map with a menu of items on the left. 

**Basic Controls & Mechanics:**
*   **Spawning Items:** Click the icon on the left to open the spawn menu, categorized into characters, melee weapons, firearms, explosives, and more. Click an item to spawn it in the world.
*   **Movement:** Left-click and drag any object or character to move it around the screen.
*   **Interaction:** Drag weapons or tools onto characters to equip them. Some items can be activated by right-clicking and selecting 'Activate'.
*   **Time Control:** Press the **Spacebar** to pause and unpause the game. This is your most powerful tool! Pause time to carefully arrange characters, place objects, and set up complex scenarios. Unpause to watch the physics-driven action unfold.
*   **Context Menu:** Right-click on any item to bring up a menu with options like 'Freeze' (to lock it in place), 'Disable Collision', 'Set Layer' (to move it in front or behind other objects), and more.

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Start Small:** Spawn a single character and a weapon to see how they interact.
2.  **Experiment with Syringes:** The syringes have wild effects, from healing and regeneration to acid and zombification. They're a great way to learn about status effects.
3.  **Use 'Freeze':** Lock items in mid-air to build structures or create stable platforms for your experiments.
4.  **Save Your Creations:** Find a setup you love? Use the save function to preserve your contraptions and scenes for later.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Melon Sandbox free to play?

Yes, Melon Sandbox is completely free to play directly in your web browser. There are no downloads or payments required, though the game may be supported by ads. You can jump right in and start creating.

### What's the difference between Melon Sandbox and Melon Playground?

Melon Sandbox is a browser-based game heavily inspired by the popular mobile game, Melon Playground. They share similar core physics and sandbox mechanics. The main difference is that this version is instantly accessible on PC via a web browser, while Melon Playground is a mobile app that also features a large community and support for mods.

### Is there a multiplayer mode in Melon Sandbox?

No, Melon Sandbox is a single-player experience. The game is designed to be a personal creative space, focusing on your own experiments and scenarios without the influence of other players.

### Are there any goals or levels to complete?

There are no official goals, levels, or objectives. Melon Sandbox is a true sandbox game where you create your own fun. The challenge is self-directed, whether you're trying to build a working vehicle, create a short movie, or design the most chaotic chain reaction possible.
