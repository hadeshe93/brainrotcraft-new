## How to Play Geometry Dash OFFline?

The controls in Geometry Dash OFFline are deceptively simple, relying on precise one-button input. Use your mouse click, spacebar, or the UP arrow key to make your icon jump. The key to survival is timing, not complexity.

Throughout the level, you'll switch between several game modes, each with unique mechanics:
- **Cube:** The default mode. A single tap performs a jump.
- **Ship:** Tap and hold to fly upward; release to descend. Master this to navigate tight, gravity-defying corridors.
- **Ball:** A tap instantly switches the direction of gravity, sending you from floor to ceiling.
- **UFO:** Each tap initiates a small hop, similar to Flappy Bird. This requires rhythmic tapping to maintain altitude.

To master OFFline, you must learn to use jump pads and orbs effectively. Yellow orbs require a tap while airborne to perform a mid-air jump. For progression, use the 'Practice Mode.'
