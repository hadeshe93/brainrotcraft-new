## How to Play FNF vs Sonic.EXE?

The core gameplay of FNF vs Sonic.EXE will feel familiar to FNF fans, but with a terrifying twist. Your goal is to outperform your opponent by hitting the correct notes and keeping your health bar in the green.

**Controls:**
- Use the **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to match the notes as they scroll to the top of your screen.
- Press the corresponding key precisely when the moving arrow aligns with the static arrow outline.

**Game Mechanics:**
- **Health Bar:** The bar at the bottom shows your performance. Hitting notes moves the icon towards your side (green), while missing notes moves it towards the opponent's side (red). If the opponent's color takes over the entire bar, you lose.
- **Special Notes & Events:** This is where Sonic.EXE changes the game. Be prepared for:
  - **Jumpscares:** Sudden, loud, and designed to make you miss a beat.
  - **Screen Effects:** The screen may shake, glitch, or go dark to disorient you.
  - **Unique Notes:** Some songs feature special notes you must avoid hitting or ring notes you must collect. Hitting a bad note can drain your health instantly!

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Start on Normal:** Hard mode is notoriously difficult. Learn the songs on Normal difficulty first.
2.  **Use Freeplay:** After unlocking a song in Story Mode, practice it in Freeplay to master its unique patterns and events without pressure.
3.  **Stay Focused:** The mod's greatest weapon is distraction. Train yourself to focus on the arrows and anticipate the gimmicks rather than being startled by them.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is FNF vs Sonic.EXE suitable for all ages?

No. This game is based on a horror theme and contains frequent jumpscares, disturbing imagery, and flashing lights. It is designed for players who are comfortable with horror content and is not recommended for young children or those easily startled.

### What makes this mod so difficult?

The difficulty stems from three main factors: fast and complex note patterns (charting), long and demanding songs, and intentional horror gimmicks. The game uses screen shakes, glitches, and jumpscares specifically to break your concentration and cause you to fail.

### Can I play FNF vs Sonic.EXE on my phone?

This browser version is optimized for PC play with a keyboard for the best performance and responsiveness. While it might load on a mobile browser, you may encounter significant input lag and performance issues, making the fast-paced gameplay nearly impossible to enjoy.

### What's the difference between Story Mode and Freeplay?

Story Mode guides you through the mod's narrative, where you'll face a set sequence of opponents and unlock songs as you progress. Freeplay lets you select and play any song you've already unlocked, including many secret and bonus tracks not found in the main story.
