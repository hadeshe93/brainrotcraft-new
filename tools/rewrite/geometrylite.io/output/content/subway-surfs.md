## What is Subway Surfs?

Subway Surfs is an electrifying, free-to-play endless runner you can play right in your web browser. Inspired by the world-famous mobile hit, this game drops you into the sneakers of a mischievous graffiti artist caught in the act. Your mission is simple but challenging: run for your life! With a grumpy Inspector and his dog hot on your heels, you'll need lightning-fast reflexes to navigate a treacherous subway system. Dash along tracks, leap over barriers, and surf on top of moving trains to evade capture. The goal is to run as far as you can, collecting shiny gold coins and powerful boosts along the way to smash high scores and unlock cool new characters. Perfect for players of all ages looking for a quick burst of action, Subway Surfs delivers the classic, addictive thrill of the chase without any downloads or installation. Are you ready to prove your agility and become a subway legend?

## How to Play Subway Surfs?

Mastering the subways is all about quick reflexes and smart moves. Your character runs forward automatically, so your job is to guide them safely through the chaos.

**Controls:**
- **Left/Right Arrow Keys:** Press to instantly switch between the three lanes.
- **Up Arrow Key:** Press to jump over low barriers and obstacles.
- **Down Arrow Key:** Press to roll under overhead signs and gates.
- **Mouse Control:** Alternatively, you can click and drag left, right, up, or down to perform the same actions.

**Gameplay Mechanics:**
Your main goal is to survive as long as possible. Along the way, **collect gold coins** scattered across the tracks. These coins are your currency for unlocking new characters with unique styles. Keep an eye out for special power-ups that can give you a massive advantage:
- **Jetpack:** Soar above the tracks, effortlessly collecting coins without any obstacles.
- **Super Sneakers:** Jump higher than normal, allowing you to leap onto the tops of trains.
- **Coin Magnet:** Automatically pull in all nearby coins for a short period.

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Stay High:** When possible, jump on top of trains. This vantage point gives you a better view of upcoming obstacles.
2.  **Look Ahead:** Don't just focus on your character. Scan the track far ahead to anticipate lane changes and prepare for jumps or slides.
3.  **Power-up Priority:** Always try to grab power-ups, especially the Jetpack, as they provide a safe and lucrative break from the intense action.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Subway Surfs the same as the official Subway Surfers game?

No, Subway Surfs is a free-to-play web-based game inspired by the original 'Subway Surfers' mobile game by SYBO and Kiloo. It offers a similar core gameplay experience but is unofficial and designed to be played directly in a web browser without any app downloads.

### How do I get a higher score in Subway Surfs?

To maximize your score, focus on surviving as long as possible, as your score multiplier increases over time. Grab every power-up you see, especially the Jetpack and 2X Multiplier. Completing in-game missions will also permanently increase your score multiplier for all future runs.

### Can I play Subway Surfs on my phone or tablet?

Yes, because it's a browser-based game, you can play it on most modern mobile devices by navigating to the game's webpage in your phone's browser (like Chrome or Safari). However, the controls are primarily optimized for a keyboard, so your experience may vary.

### What is the purpose of the Hoverboard?

The Hoverboard is a crucial single-use power-up. Double-tap to activate it. While active, the hoverboard will protect you from a single crash. If you hit an obstacle, the board breaks, but you get to keep running. It's a lifesaver for extending your run and achieving a new high score.
