## What is Cube Stack?

Cube Stack is the ultimate test of focus and timing, wrapped in a beautifully simple hyper-casual arcade game. Available instantly in your web browser on PC or mobile, Cube Stack invites you to achieve one simple, yet incredibly challenging goal: build the tallest tower of cubes imaginable. The concept is easy to grasp but difficult to master. A single cube slides back and forth above your growing tower, and with a single tap, you drop it into place. The catch? Any part of the cube that overhangs the layer below is sliced away, leaving you with a smaller and more difficult target for the next piece. This clever mechanic means the game's difficulty is a direct reflection of your own skill. Perfect for players of all ages looking for a quick, satisfying challenge, Cube Stack is the ideal game to play during a short break or to compete with friends for the highest score.

## How to Play Cube Stack?

Mastering Cube Stack is all about precision and rhythm. The controls are deceptively simple: a single left-click with your mouse or a tap on your screen is all you need to play.

**Core Mechanics:**
At the start of each game, you have a stationary base block. A new cube will begin sliding or swinging back and forth above it. Your task is to click or tap at the exact moment the moving cube is aligned over the one below. If your placement is off-center, the overhanging edges of the cube are instantly sliced off, permanently shrinking your tower's width and making the next placement even trickier. The game ends when you miss the tower completely.

**Perfect Drops & Scoring:**
Land a cube in perfect alignment with the one below to achieve a 'Perfect' drop. You'll be rewarded with a satisfying sound and visual cue, and your tower will maintain its full width. String together a combo of several Perfect drops, and your tower will actually start to grow wider, giving you a larger and more forgiving target. Your score is simply the number of cubes you successfully stack.

**Beginner Tips:**
1.  **Watch the Shadow:** The moving cube casts a shadow on the tower below. Use this as your primary guide for perfect alignment.
2.  **Find the Rhythm:** The cube moves at a constant speed. Relax and get a feel for its tempo to better predict when it will be centered.
3.  **Patience is Key:** Don't rush your taps. It's better to let the cube make a few passes to ensure a perfect drop than to make a sloppy one that costs you precious surface area.
4.  **Prioritize Width:** Always aim for Perfect drops. A wide, stable base is the secret to reaching an epic high score.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a 'Perfect' drop in Cube Stack?

A 'Perfect' drop happens when you stack a new cube in perfect alignment with the one below it. This is crucial because it prevents your tower from shrinking. Stringing together several 'Perfect' drops in a row will even cause your tower to grow wider, making the game easier and enabling higher scores.

### How can I play Cube Stack on my phone?

Cube Stack is an HTML5 game, which means it runs directly in most modern web browsers. You can play it on your smartphone or tablet by simply navigating to the game's webpage—no download or installation required. Some versions may also be available as standalone apps on the App Store or Google Play.

### What's the best strategy to get a high score?

The single best strategy is to prioritize making 'Perfect' drops. This maintains the width of your tower, giving you the largest possible target for subsequent cubes. Be patient, use the cube's shadow for alignment, and try to get into a rhythm with the cube's movement. A wide base is more forgiving of small errors.

### Is there an end to the game?

No, there is no official end to Cube Stack. The game continues for as long as you can successfully place cubes on the tower. The goal is to challenge yourself and others by building the tallest tower possible and setting a new high score before you inevitably miss a placement.
