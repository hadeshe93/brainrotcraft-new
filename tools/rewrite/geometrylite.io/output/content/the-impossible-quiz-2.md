## How to Play The Impossible Quiz 2?

Your goal in The Impossible Quiz 2 is simple: answer questions to reach the end. The execution, however, is anything but. You start with three lives, and each incorrect answer costs you one. Lose all your lives, and you're sent back to the last checkpoint.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many questions are in The Impossible Quiz 2?

The Impossible Quiz 2 features 120 main questions. The journey is broken up by checkpoints, so you don't have to beat all 120 in one sitting. Be prepared for every question to be more absurd than the last!

### Where can I play The Impossible Quiz 2?

You can play The Impossible Quiz 2 on your PC or Mac through various web game portals that use Flash emulation. Official, optimized versions are also available for mobile devices on the Apple App Store for iOS and the Google Play Store for Android.

### What are the timed 'Bomb' questions?

These are called 'Fusestoppers.' A question will appear with a lit bomb and a timer (usually 5-10 seconds). You must input the correct answer before the timer runs out, or you'll lose a life. They add a layer of intense pressure to the quiz.

### How do I get and use Skips?

You automatically earn 'Skips' after completing certain milestone questions. They are a limited resource. To use one, simply click the Skip button at the bottom of the screen to bypass the current question. It's best to save them for the timed bomb questions you can't solve.
