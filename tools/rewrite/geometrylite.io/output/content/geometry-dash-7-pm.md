## What is Geometry Dash 7 PM?

7 PM is a celebrated user-created level within the world of Geometry Dash, a rhythm-based action platformer. It's not a standalone game, but a masterpiece of design you can play inside the main Geometry Dash application. Your objective is simple: guide your icon through a perilous course synchronized to an energetic beat, all without crashing. The level's concept is built around the mesmerizing beauty of a sunset, with a color palette that shifts through gorgeous purples, pinks, and oranges, creating an unforgettable atmosphere. Created by a massive team of legendary level designers (a 'megacollab') including melX0exe and Serponge, 7 PM is classified as a Medium Demon. This makes it the perfect target for intermediate players ready to step up from Easy Demons, as well as any player who appreciates breathtaking visual artistry combined with satisfying, fast-paced gameplay. It’s a test of skill wrapped in a beautiful, atmospheric package.

## How to Play Geometry Dash 7 PM?

Mastering 7 PM requires rhythm, focus, and quick reflexes. The controls are deceptively simple: you use a single input (tapping the screen, clicking the mouse, or pressing the spacebar) to interact. However, the effect of your tap changes depending on your current form. As the Cube, you tap to jump. As the Ship or UFO, you hold to fly upwards and release to descend. As the Wave, you hold to move diagonally up and release to go diagonally down. The level automatically switches you between these forms, demanding you adapt your timing instantly. 

Your goal is to reach 100% in Normal Mode in a single, unbroken run. To get there, your best friend is Practice Mode. This mode allows you to place checkpoints manually, letting you learn and master the level section by section. 

**Beginner Tips:**
*   **Use Practice Mode:** Don't try to beat it in one go. Learn the patterns and transitions in bite-sized chunks.
*   **Listen to the Music:** The energetic song, "Santa-chan's Winter Wonderland Adventure," is your guide. Almost every jump and transition is synced to the beat. Let the rhythm tell you when to tap.
*   **Stay Calm:** The difficulty is in consistency, not pixel-perfect tricks. Focus on staying calm and executing the patterns you've practiced. Panicking during the fast-paced sections is the number one cause of crashes.

7 PM is not a standalone game; it is a famous user-created level within the official Geometry Dash game. To play it, open Geometry Dash, go to the user level search bar, and search for the level ID: 49774223. You must own Geometry Dash on PC (Steam), iOS, or Android to play it.
