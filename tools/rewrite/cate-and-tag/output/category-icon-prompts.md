## Usage Notes

1. **AI Tools Compatible**: These prompts are optimized for DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or similar AI image generators.
2. **Customization**: Adjust color codes to match your exact brand palette.
3. **Variations**: Generate 2-3 variations per category for selection.
4. **Post-Processing**: May require minor cleanup or background removal for final icon use.
5. **Consistency**: Use the same AI tool and settings for all categories to maintain visual consistency.
6. **Size**: Generate at high resolution (1024x1024 or larger) for quality scaling.
