## What is Merge Brainrot

Merge Brainrot, or Merge Fellas, is a game where players combine different basic Brainrot elements to create and evolve new characters, similar to the Suika/Watermelon game. It is easy to pick up but requires time and effort to master.

Merge Brainrot has been released as a web game, compatible with both PC and mobile devices. Its mechanics are simple and easy to understand, and the gameplay is engaging. After its release on May 9, 2025, it quickly became popular online.

---

## How to Play Italian Brainrot Clicker?

The core gameplay of Merge Brainrot is straightforward. Players drop characters into a container, and when two identical characters touch, they merge to form a new, larger character. The goal is to create the largest character possible without the container overflowing. The game ends when the characters pile up and cross the top line of the container.

*   **Controls:** Simple click-to-drop on desktop and tap-to-drop on mobile.
*   **Objective:** Create the biggest character, often referred to as the "Final Boss."
*   **Failure Condition:** The game ends if the container overflows.
*   **Scoring:** Players earn points for each merge, with larger characters awarding more points.

## Why is Merge Brainrot So Addictive?

One of the key factors in Merge Brainrot's attraction and longevity is its vast number of versions and modes. With over 25 versions available, the game offers a wide variety of experiences to players.

*   **Main Italian Brainrot Series:** The core of the game, with 14 numbered installments (I-XIV).
*   **Themed Editions:** These include variations like *Capy Brainrot*, *Jurassic Brainrot*, *Robot Brainrot*, and *Zombie Brainrot*, each with its own unique set of characters and themes.
*   **Special Features:** Some versions include special features like a "Maker Mode" that allows players to create their own characters, and boss battles against powerful, oversized characters.
