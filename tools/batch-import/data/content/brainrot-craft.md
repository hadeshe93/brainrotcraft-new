## What is Brainrot Craft?

Ever wondered what would happen if you mixed a "ninja" with a cup of "cappuccino"? Welcome to the wild world of Brainrot Craft! It’s a creative sandbox rather than another element-combining game, where your imagination runs wild and surprises are around every corner.

Your only mission here is to drag and merge basic elements (like "shark," "monkey," "cow," "elephant," "tree," "coconut," "cappuccino") to unlock new, sometimes totally unexpected, wacky creations. Brainrot Craft draws inspiration from the internet’s most viral Meme culture and those hilarious "brainrot" moments. You might end up creating the legendary "Ballerina Cappuccina," summon the mysterious merchant "Herios," or witness internet memes come to life at your fingertips.

There are no complicated goals, no penalties for failure. The only purpose is to keep combining and discovering, bringing you pure joy and laugh-out-loud moments. Every successful combo is a reward for your creativity in Brainrot Craft.

## How to Play Brainrot Craft?

<img src="/imgs/select-items-on-right-side.png" alt="The Pro Tips" />

The magic of Brainrot Craft is in its simplicity. No complicated controls or gaming experience needed—just a mouse and endless curiosity. Here’s your quick start guide:
1. Check out the basic items: When you start, you’ll see some basic objects on the right side of your canvas, like "shark," "monkey," "cow," "elephant," "tree," "coconut," and "cappuccino." These are your building blocks for combos and creations.
2. Drag and merge: Hold down the left mouse button to drag an element onto the center of the canvas. Then, drag another element and drop it onto the first one to try a combo.
3. Watch and discover: If the two elements can combine, they’ll instantly disappear and a brand-new element will appear! For example, merging "ninja" and "cappuccino" gives you a "Cappuccino Ninja." If they can’t combine, they’ll shake and bounce apart.
4. Repeat and experiment: Keep repeating the process, using new discoveries to try combos with everything you have. Experiment like a scientist until you unlock all the hundreds of hidden, magical recipes in Brainrot Craft.
