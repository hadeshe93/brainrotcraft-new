## **Introduce The Latest Platform Game - Rainbow Obby** 

Rainbow Obby is a new platform game that will put your reflexes and timing to the test in a brightly colored universe. Your mission is to help [Obby](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obby) navigate dozens of floating platforms, swinging hammers, disappearing tiles, and spinning traps. Each barrier is covered in a distinct color of the rainbow! Enter the colorful world and face your challenge right now!

### **Advices**

- Look Before You Leap! Take a good look at the next rainbow platform before jumping. 
- Don't fall! You will have to restart at the latest checkpoint. Try to land safely on each colorful step!
- Don’t forget to collect power-ups! Keep a lookout for shiny cash or diamonds along the rainbow trail. They could help you unlock cool new characters or entertaining enhancements!
