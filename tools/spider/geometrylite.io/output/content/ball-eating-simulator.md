## About Ball Eating Simulator

Enter a vibrant ball-filled world with Ball Eating Simulation, eat as many balls as you can, and grow as large as you can in the ball's arena! Keep in mind that the balls increase in number and speed as you get bigger, forcing you to modify your approach and improve your munching techniques. With multiple modes to keep things interesting and captivating, don't miss this arcade game! 

## How to play Ball Eating Simulator

Let's go into the arena and begin competing with various colored and sized balls. Your objective is to consume as many vibrant orbs as you can while dodging the rivals who are attempting to do the same. Using the WASD keys, you will navigate the map and devour balls smaller than yourself to grow larger and become the largest ball there is.
