**Moto X3M** is the perfect game for motorcycle enthusiasts who are confident in their driving skills. Prepare to ride your motorcycle across stages that have enormous, moving obstacles that you must stay clear of.

## About Moto X3M

You must maintain your balance while racing across a variety of challenging terrains in the action-packed bike racing game Moto X3M! This is the first edition in the well-liked [Moto X3M series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/moto-x3m), which Madpuffers created. Players will feel the exhilaration of racing motorcycles on a variety of terrains, surroundings, and weather situations in each iteration of this series. Prepare to sprint through obstacles, leaps, and ramps in the original version.

### Controls

- To accelerate, press the W/Up Arrow key. 
- To break, press the S/Down Arrow key.
- To position your motorcycle, use the A and D/Left Arrow and left-right arrow keys.
