Enjoy the thrilling feeling of speed in the motorbike racing game **Traffic Road**! This game takes you to busy traffic with all the hurdles a professional racer must get past!

## About Traffic Road

In Traffic Road, an immersive motorbike race, you can feel the excitement of speed as you navigate around moving cars in heavy traffic. This is a fantastic chance for you to demonstrate your expert driving abilities! Depending on your preferences and skill level, you can select from a range of game modes, such as Career mode, Endless mode, Time Trial, and Free Ride.

### More motorcycle upgrades

There are many different motorcycles in this game, and each has special features and handling. You can use your regular coins to personalize your rides as you advance in the game. Get access to more fast-moving vehicles to improve your racing.

## How to play

- You can use the Left and Right arrow keys to steer your motorcycle.
- The up and down arrow keys can be used to brake and accelerate.
