In this shooting game, **BuildNow GG**, all you have to do is outsmart and outgun your opponents. You'll spawn in a variety of arena environments, armed with a starter weapon, and the goal is simple: be the last person standing. You must have a strategy for each map to guarantee you have barriers to protect yourself and destroy your opponents. Remember that having the fastest reflexes isn't enough; you also need to be able to think on your feet and manipulate your surroundings.

### Controls

- Use WASD or the arrow keys to move.
- Use the Spacebar to jump.
- Use right-click to restart customisation.
- Use the mouse wheel down to quickly restart customisation.
