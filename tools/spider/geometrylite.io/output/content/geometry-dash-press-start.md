## **Overview of Geometry Dash Press Start**

Geometry Dash Press Start is [Geometry Dash SubZero](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-subzero)'s first official level, with a Normal difficulty rating of 4 stars. This level, designed to help novice players get a feel for the game's principles, is packed with visual effects, sharp coordination with the music, and fluid platforming action.

The level takes 97 seconds to finish and involves at least 77 leaps, making it enjoyable but not very difficult. There are three secret coins buried throughout the level, providing additional challenge and replay potential. These secrets make Press Start more thrilling for gamers who wish to go beyond simply crossing the finish line.

### **Why Should You Play This Game?**

Press Start is one of the best levels to start your Geometry Dash SubZero adventure. It's vibrantly colored, music-filled, and energetic, and it provides a fun challenge for players of any age. Whether you're just starting or looking for a quick level to master, this is the ideal choice. Plus, finding all three secret coins provides a fun objective to strive for!
