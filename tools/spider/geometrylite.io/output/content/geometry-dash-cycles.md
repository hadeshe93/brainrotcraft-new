## **Geometry Dash Cycles – Bounce to the Beat in a Colorful Rhythm Challenge**

- Developer: RobTop Games
- Level information: 9th original level in Geometry Dash
- Difficulty: [Normal](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal) (Rated [6 Stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/6-stars))

Exciting musical challenges await you in Geometry Dash Cycles! This level is known for its vivid colours and cheerful soundtrack, and it provides a fun yet difficult challenge that keeps players dancing to the beat. In Cycles, the gameplay differs from previous levels by introducing the ball game mode, in which gravity reverses every time you click or press.

### **Features**

- Catchy Soundtrack: Includes "Cycles" by DJVI, a fan favourite with a nice rhythm.
- Ball Mode Gameplay: Introduces novel mobility techniques with gravity-flipping controls.
- Star-Rated Level: Official 6-star level with 3 user coins to collect.
