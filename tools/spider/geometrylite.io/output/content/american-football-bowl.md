**American Football Bowl** is an American football game that has quickly become one of the most popular sports because of its attractive classic gameplay.

## Introduce American Football Bowl

American Football Bowl sets itself apart from today's popular [sports games](https://geometrylite.io/sports.games) with pixelated graphics and easy-to-grasp gameplay. This game captures the excitement of building a championship football franchise combined with simple controls that make it easy for players to access. Let's start the journey to take control of a football team and lead it to become a winning championship right now!  

## How to play

In the American Football Bowl, you will players take on the role of a head coach, managing all aspects of their college football team. Start by completing a series of coaching tasks including recruiting players, setting up game strategies, and making real-time decisions during matches. Your goal is to lead your team to become the champion of this football tournament!
