## **Tiny Fishing: A Relaxing Yet Addictive Fishing Adventure** 

In Tiny Fishing, you begin with a basic fishing rod and a shallow ocean. Your goal is to cast your line and catch as many fish as you can in one pull. The idea is simple: catch as many fish as possible to gain money, which you can then use to improve your fishing gear. Over time, you'll get access to more complex rods, fishing spots, and bigger fish to catch.

### **Step-by-step to play**

- Cast Your Line: Simply tap or click to send your fishing line into the water.
- Catch Fish: When the fish bites, hold the button to reel them in.
- Earn Coins: The size and rarity of the fish you catch impact the number of coins you get.
- Upgrade Your Gear: Use your money to upgrade your rods and reels, as well as get access to new fishing spots.
