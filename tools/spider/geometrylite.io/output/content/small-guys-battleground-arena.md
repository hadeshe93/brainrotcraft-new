## Introduce gameplay of multiplayer Small Guys: Battleground Arena! 

**Small Guys: Battleground Arena** transports you to a vibrant arena where a large number of players are engaged in competition. You may start **Small Guys: Battleground Arena** with ease if you know how to play [LOLBeans.io](https://geometrylite.io/lolbeans-io) because the gameplay is very similar! Your goal is to enter the race, do tasks, get past obstacles, and cross the finish line as quickly as you can!

### Controls

- To move, use the arrow keys/WASD.
- To sprint forward, press F.
- To leap, use the spacebar.
