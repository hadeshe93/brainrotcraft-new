**Geometry Dash Forms** is an exciting extension to the popular Geometry Dash series, featuring high-speed platforming, rhythmic puzzles, and unique transformations that alter gameplay dynamics.

## About Geometry Dash Forms

Geometry Dash Forms allows you to manage a personalized geometric avatar that goes forward while avoiding obstacles and traversing challenging levels. This edition offers new Forms, each having its own movement mechanics that require players to adapt rapidly. This game, like another variation of Geometry Dash, syncs obstacles and jumps with background music, requiring you to time everything correctly to progress.

### Key Features

- Flying, flipping, and gravity-shifting are all examples of unique movement mechanics.
- Unlock skins, colors, and effects to customize your geometric avatar.
