Welcome to the colorful world of **Blumgi Slime**! This jumping game challenges you to control Slime and perform jumps to safely reach the finish line.

## Gameplay of Blumgi Slime

This game's protagonist is a charming slime that jumps to move. Slime appears on a [platform](https://geometrylite.io/platform.games) far from the indicated destination as soon as the game launches. Controlling Slime's leaping movements to get it to the goal in each stage is your job. This presents a challenge for you to accurately perfect your timing and skills. When you begin your first dancing moves, you could feel lost, but don't panic! You may regulate the Slime's speed by practicing through a few levels. Wish you luck in completing as many levels as you can.

### Controls

To control your power, click and hold the mouse. The longer you hold it down, the higher and more forceful your jump will be.
