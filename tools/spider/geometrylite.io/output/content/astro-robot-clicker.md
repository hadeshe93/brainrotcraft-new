Welcome to **Astro Robot Clicker**! Let's begin and experience the thrill of this clicker game with the well-known character Astro Robot.

## Introduce the new clicker game Astro Robot Clicker

Astro Robot Clicker is the most recent addition to our [Clicker Games](https://geometrylite.io/clicker.games) collection, and it promises to provide you with an exciting entertainment experience! Astro is the main character in the platform game Astro Bot, which is popular all over the world and earned the Game of the Year 2024 award. In Astro Robot Clicker, we'll explore the broad and interesting array of Astro Robot characters by clicking hard on them. There are plenty of interesting things to uncover in this clicker game! 

### Upgrade Astro Robot characters 

All you have to do in this game is click and click. The more robots you gather, the higher your score, allowing you to access more new features and Astro characters in various forms such as Mickey Robot, Astro Sproutbot, Astro Samurai, and so on. Start the game and explore straight away!
