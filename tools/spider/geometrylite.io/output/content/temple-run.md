Welcome to **Temple Run**, an area of limitless adventure! Take control of your character and begin your never-ending running adventure, dodging obstacles and earning worthwhile rewards as you go.

## What is Temple Run?

In Temple Run, you have to sprint as far as you can while collecting cash and moving the character left and right to avoid obstacles. Our main character is a crafty explorer who is trying to get back the coins he stole from the Zulu people. A thrilling chase through the forest is about to start! To navigate the game's ever-changing landscape, you must have lightning-fast reflexes and extreme precision. You are up against obstacles like big logs, big rocks, and winding roadways!Â 

### Explore more Power-ups

You can advance throughout the game by using the special talents that power-ups grant you. Along the road, you will be able to gather them. As the game progresses, you will see them more frequently. These power-ups can be bought with coins or gathered while you're running.

- Coin Magnet: For a brief while, this power-up draws coins to you.
- Shield: Provides temporary protection from harm.
- Boost: Quickly increases speed for a brief period of time.
- Invisibility: For a brief time, invisibility renders you imperceptible to obstacles.

## How do I get more coins?

One of the most crucial missions in Temple Run is to gather coins. You can run while dodging hazards and swiping to pick up coins as you go. Activating power-ups like the Coin Magnet, which draws coins to you for a brief period of time, is another way to expand your coin collection. Make an effort to finish missions and objectives to earn more coins.
