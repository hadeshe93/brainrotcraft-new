**Geometry Dash Sky Tower** is an [Easy Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/easy-demon) with a difficulty rating of 10 stars from the talent-created game Alkali. Prepare to begin your trip to acquire 3 user coins in this level! 

## Introducing all user coins in Geometry Dash Sky Tower

According to several skilled players, all three coins on this level are in tough places. They're incredibly difficult and demand a lot of memorization and additional skill to gather. For those looking to complete this challenge, here's some information regarding the location and how to earn three user coins.

### Tips to collect

- 1st coin: To activate the next trigger orb and enable a passage on the spikes, you must contact the lever at 2:20. At 2:34, you can use this to go directly through the brown rocks and get the first coin.
- 2nd coin: To reach below the typical route at 2:45, you must use the orbs and jump as late as you can. You must jump and strike the green block at 3:02 to retrieve the coin that has been frozen within the ice block by the trigger orb.
- 3rd coin: The third coin requires the player to navigate a road with intricate waves and UFO gates, making it a challenging coin to obtain. To get this coin, make a precise jump at 3:50.
