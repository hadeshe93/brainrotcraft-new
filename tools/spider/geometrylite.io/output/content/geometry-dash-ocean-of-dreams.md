## About Geometry Dash Ocean of Dreams

Inspired by the blue ocean of dreams, Geometry Dash Ocean of Dreams is well-known for its intriguing and varied backgrounds. The spacecraft, which you must fly through without hitting any spikes or blocks, is one of the trickiest elements.

### How to collect all user coins

- The first coin: At 34%. It requires players to jump to the platform and then off of it.
- The second coin: At 65%. Start in the middle of the coin and fly into the hole from above. Out of the three user coins, this one is thought to be the most difficult to obtain.
- The third: At 86%. The coin needs to be found, thus you must enter that area and gently fly out of it.
