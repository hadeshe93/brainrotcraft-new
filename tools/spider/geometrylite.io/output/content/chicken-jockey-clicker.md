## **Chicken Jockey Clicker – The Latest Clicker Game Just Updated!**

Chicken Jockey Clicker transports you to a crazy universe in which jockeys ride chickens instead of horses. All you have to do in this game is click quickly to race, receive awards, and invest those coins in speed upgrades, better gear, and faster chickens. With each race, your poultry posse grows stronger and more entertaining. As you progress through the game, you will be able to acquire new characters and enhancements that will help you create Chicken Jockey more efficiently!

### **Game Features**

- Idle clicker gameplay featuring racing mechanics
- Upgradeable chickens with speed, stamina, and style.
- Funny animations and [Minecraft](https://geometrylite.io/tag/minecraft)\-style images.
- In-game boosters that automate tapping and increase [coin](https://geometrylite.io/tag/coin) gain
