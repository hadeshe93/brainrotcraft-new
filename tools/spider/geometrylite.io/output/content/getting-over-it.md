**Getting Over It** is an action game with unique and original gameplay in which you control a cat with a hammer and must navigate a series of platforms to reach the finish line.

## Gameplay of Getting Over It

Bennett Foddy designed the first version of Getting Over, which quickly became well-known for its innovative and tough gameplay. This game's gameplay is classic and challenging, requiring players to be patient and dexterous. Because it will be difficult to climb the platforms and move with only a hammer. It's time to showcase your abilities! 

### How to play

Move the mouse to direct the main character's movement. It is important to note that each hammer swing causes a significant shift in the cat's position.
