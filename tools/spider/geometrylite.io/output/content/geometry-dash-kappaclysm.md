Developed by [Glittershroom](https://geometrylite.io/tag/glittershroom), **Geometry Dash Kappaclysm** is a [Harder](https://geometrylite.io/tag/harder) with difficult gameplay reminiscent of Cataclysm.

## Rhythm-based Gameplay of Geometry Dash Kappaclysm

Geometry Dash Kappaclysm, like the other rhythm-based levels in the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) series, uses music that synchronizes with the gameplay. This provides fast-paced gaming that requires quick and precise responses. The throbbing beats will guide you through jumps, spikes, and portals to the finish line safely, so listen and get used to it.

### More information

- Geometry Dash Kappaclysm consists of 13,083 items.
- Kappaclysm is the only level that includes both a Map Pack ([Blade Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/blade-pack)) and a Gauntlet ([Chaos Gauntlet](https://geometrylite.io/tag/chaos-gauntlet)).
- The level's name is a portmanteau of Kappa, a Twitch emote and internet craze, and Cataclysm, the level on which it is based.
