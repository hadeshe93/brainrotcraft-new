**Escape Road City** puts your driving skills to the test as you try to flee the police! Drive through the packed metropolis, overcome obstacles, and get as far as you can!

## Introduce Escape Road City

The [Escape Road series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/escape-road-series) is back with the latest installment, Escape Road City! The game immerses players in the setting of a city chase with heavy traffic. Once again, you will take on the role of a group of bank robbers on the run. To avoid getting detected and penalized by the police, you must operate the [car](https://geometrylite.io/tag/car) and overcome obstacles while traveling as far as possible! Are you confident in your ability to perform this difficult task?  

### Instructions for driving your car

- Use WASD to drive your car.
- Take advantage of the opportunity to accelerate on empty highways to gain an advantage.
