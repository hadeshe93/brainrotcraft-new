**Doodle Baseball** is a fun and informal baseball game from Google's Doodle Games library that debuted in 2019.

## Doodle Baseball - A Simple and Fun Timing-Based Gameplay 

Doodle Baseball is a [baseball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/baseball) game with simple gameplay that focuses on time. Unlike traditional baseball, the characters in Doodle Baseball are unique and charming. You will have control over the batter, which is peanut-based. The batter's primary responsibility is to swing at pitches thrown by the pitcher and attempt to hit the ball as precisely as possible. Pitches are classified into several categories, including fastballs, curveballs, and others. Start a game today to find out!

### How to play

The batter will automatically stand in position while waiting for the ball to be delivered. You must click to swing the bat at the appropriate time to avoid missing any balls.
