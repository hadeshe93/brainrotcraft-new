Welcome to **Krew.io**! The newest game in the .IO Games collection will take you on a treacherous ocean voyage in seeking wealth!

## Set Sail with Krew.io right now! 

Starting Krew.io, you'll be lying on a cannon onboard a ship, floating in the waters between Spain, Guinea, Labrador, Brazil, and Jamaica. You aim to control your cannon, destroy other opponents at sea, and collect prizes. This is an action-packed [multiplayer](https://geometrylite.io/tag/multiplayer) game, so you'll be able to compete against millions of opponents from all over the world. Start playing today and climb the famous leaderboard!

## How to play

- Begin by selecting a ship and joining a server.
- You can work with other players to operate your vessel.
- Begin pointing your cannon, firing at enemy ships, and collecting rewards by climbing the leaderboard.

### Controls

- Use WASD to move
- Use the spacebar to jump
- Use 1 - 3 to Switch tools
- Use 5 - 7 to Upgrade your Stats
- Use C to sail from the island
