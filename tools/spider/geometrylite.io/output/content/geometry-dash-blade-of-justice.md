## **About Geometry Dash Blade of Justice**

Geometry Dash Blade of Justice is an [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) collaboration level that pushes players to their absolute limits. Created by Manix648, LazerBlitz, and knobbelboy, published by Manix648, and verified by RicoLP, this level has become one of the most memorable and celebrated challenges in the Geometry Dash community.

### **Blade of Justice - A dark design**

Blade of Justice stands out with its striking design and color palette, mostly dominated by blue, black, and white. LazerBlitz’s sections introduce hints of purple, adding a unique contrast. With its reputation, it secured a spot as #49 in GDToday’s 100 Greatest Levels of All Time.

- Difficulty: Extreme Demon ([10 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/10-stars))
- Length: Long, 2m 1s
- Song: Goukisan – Betrayal of Fear

### **A Visual Identity with Purpose**

Unlike many over-the-top levels, Blade of Justice thrives on a clean yet powerful design. The stage is painted mostly in deep blues, sharp blacks, and clean whites, with splashes of purple that bring contrast during LazerBlitz’s sections. This deliberate use of color gives the level a dramatic, almost cinematic quality while keeping the focus on gameplay clarity.
