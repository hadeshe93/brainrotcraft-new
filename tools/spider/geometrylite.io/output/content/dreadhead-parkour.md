**Dreadhead Parkour** puts you in the shoes of Dreadhead, a reckless urban runner with a talent for defying gravity. Your job is to get him as far as can while avoiding all the obstacles and traps in the city!

## About Dreadhead Parkour

Dreadhead Parkour is a platformer that immerses players in a grungy, urban setting where every leap and bound counts. GameTornado created this game, which quickly gained popularity due to its original and hard gameplay. The stages become tougher as you progress, with elaborate designs and surprising hazards to face! Enjoy the game's slick controls, difficult levels, and immersive design right now!
