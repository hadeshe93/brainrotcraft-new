## The gameplay of Geometry Dash 2.2

Geometry Dash 2.2 is a fan-made version of the Geometry Dash game including numerous design and sound modifications. In terms of primary gameplay, you must control an icon that runs and jumps over hurdles and spiky obstacles. Your success depends on synchronizing your actions with the dynamic beats! Always maintain concentration from the start of the level and don't be afraid to fail!
