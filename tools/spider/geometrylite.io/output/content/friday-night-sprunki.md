## Friday Night Sprunki: Where Rhythm Lights Up the Night

Friday Night Sprunki is ready to take you to a colorful and vibrant Friday night with the help of Sprunki characters. The gameplay in Friday Night Sprunki is similar to the original edition; all you have to do is listen to the rhythm and match each click with the directions on the screen. This is the updated version, which breathes fresh life into the legendary [Friday Night Funkin'](https://geometrylite.io/tag/friday-night-funkin) by featuring one of today's hottest characters, the lively and colorful Sprunki. This character is well-known from the [Sprunki Games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sprunki-games) series, in which you can mix and match melodies to create your own music. You can play this game after completing every note in Friday Night Sprunki!

### Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to play.
