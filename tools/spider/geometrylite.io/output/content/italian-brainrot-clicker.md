## **Introduce new clicker game - Italian Brainrot Clicker**

Italian Brainrot Clicker is a chaotic idle clicker game based on the strange world of Italian Brainrot, a viral Gen-Z meme trend with AI-generated characters, nonsense names, and absurd humour. Fuelled by internet culture, the game immerses you in a weird realm where each click gets you "Brainrot" and unlocks even more digital lunacy. Whether you enjoy humorous memes, idle games, or simply wish to collect creatures like Bombardino Crocodilo or Burbaloni Luliloli, this game is the ideal combination of satire and easy entertainment.

### **Unlock characters**

Click brings you closer to unlocking a variety of bizarre and humorous characters, such as:

- Bombardino Crocodilo
- Tung Tung Tung Sahur
- Tralalero Tralala
- Bombombini Gussini
- Cappuccino Assassino
- Lirili Larila

As you earn Brainrot points, you may spend them on upgrades that increase your clicking strength and unlock new features.
