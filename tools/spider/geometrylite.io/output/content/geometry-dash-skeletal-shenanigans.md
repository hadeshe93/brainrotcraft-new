## Introduce Geometry Dash Skeletal Shenanigans

**Skeletal Shenanigans** is a collaboration between talented game creators YoReid and Airz, full of spooky vibes, sharp obstacles, and [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) rhythm jumps. This is a [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon) - the newest featured event level in Geometry Dash 2.2 Mythical Event. It's perfect for players who enjoy a creepy theme combined with classic platform challenges. 

- Scratch version: By busydragon
- Music: "Slash Inferno" by Waterflame, BoomKitty, and Teminite
- User Coins: The 1st coin is at 25%, the 2nd is at 39%.

Stay tuned for more complete versions!

### How to play

Press \[Spacebar\], \[Up Arrow\], or Left Click to jump.
