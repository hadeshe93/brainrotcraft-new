Geometry Dash Moonlight is a 5-star level by Unzor that features a nocturnal woodland atmosphere, smooth gameplay, and the soothing sound Fantasy by Cloudfield.

## **About Geometry Dash Moonlight** 

Geometry Dash Moonlight is a beautifully made Hard-level custom map produced by Unzor that provides a tranquil yet intriguing contrast to his previous level, [Sunshine](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-sunshine). While Sunshine is bright and energetic, Moonlight transports you to a peaceful, moonlight world of mountains, forests, and subtle rhythms. The level combines ambient images with subtle transitions, resulting in a relaxing yet compelling gameplay experience that complements the soundtrack.

### **Background Music**  

The soundtrack is one of Moonlight's most fascinating aspects. The level makes use of Cloudfield's "Fantasy" starring Reichuu, which is recognized for its strange, ambient tones. The soundtrack nicely complements the visual concept, providing an intense, even contemplative experience while you play.
