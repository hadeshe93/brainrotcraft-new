## **Geometry Dash Demon Park - Enter the Demon’s Playground**

Welcome to Geometry Dash Demon Park, one of the most recognizable Easy Demon 10-star levels ever created. Developed by M2coL, this level marks the first entry in [Demon Pack 1](https://geometrylite.io/tag/demon-pack-1) and is widely known as one of the easiest Easy Demons — making it a perfect introduction for players new to Demon-tier challenges. Demon Park rewards focus and rhythm more than raw skill. The clean layout and clear visual cues make it a smooth, enjoyable test of timing and consistency. 

### **How to Play: Timing Is Everything** 

- Tap / Click / Space / Up Arrow: Jump
- Avoid: Spikes and obstacles layered inside blocks

### **Why You Should Play This Level**

Geometry Dash: Demon Park isn’t just another community-made map; it’s a piece of Geometry Dash history. As the #80 level in GDToday’s 100 Greatest Levels of All Time, it paved the way for countless Demon creators that followed. Perfect for beginners wanting to conquer their first Demon, it offers a fair challenge, a strong rhythm, and a taste of what the higher tiers hold.
