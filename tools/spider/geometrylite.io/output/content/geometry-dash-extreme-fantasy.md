**Geometry Dash Extreme Fantasy** promises to bring you a wonderful journey while jumping over obstacles and traps with exciting music.

## About Geometry Dash Extreme Fantasy 

Geometry Dash Extreme Fantasy is a user-level game that functions similarly to the classic Geometry Dash. This game is the brainchild of skilled game creators such as Rabbitical, sackwon, and irabb2ti. Although the level is not well-liked by the community because of its intricacy in gameplay, Extreme Fantasy is still a [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon) that you may use to test your skills. 

### How to play

Begin your immersive experience by navigating your way through rhythmic obstacles with the cube. Timing is key; pay attention to the rhythm because it will help you make the proper decisions.
