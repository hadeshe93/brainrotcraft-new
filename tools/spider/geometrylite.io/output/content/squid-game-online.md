## Introduce Squid Game Online

Squid Game Online is an online game based on the critically renowned Korean TV series of the same name. Your goal is to battle against people from all around the world and become the last survivor after completing 7 mini-games. These are all childhood-inspired challenges but with unexpected twists on the rules! This is a war for survival, and only the smartest and fastest will survive. Join today and compete against genuine players in a war for survival!

### Mini-games in Squid Game Online

- Red Light, Green Light
- Dalgona Candy
- Tug of War
- Marbles
- Glass Stepping Stones
- Judgment Night
- Squid Game

### Controls

- Use WASD or arrow keys to move.
- Use Space to jump.
- Use Left-click to attack.
