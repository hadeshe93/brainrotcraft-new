## Experience the Thrill of Drifting in Drift Hunters  

It's time to experience the thrilling and tight sensation of drifting in Drift Hunters! You will be driving fast automobiles and drifting through different tracks in this thrilling race. That sounds fantastic, don't you think? You must concentrate on precise driving, clever drifting, and mastery of the driving craft to finish this challenging circuit full of obstacles. In this game, let's explore real drift dynamics and demonstrate our driving prowess! 

## How to play

- Use Up Arrow/W to move forward more quickly; 
- Use Down Arrow/S to stop or go back;
- Use Left Arrow/A to move to the left
- To steer right, use the right arrow or D.

### Unlock more Extensive Car collection 

A large selection of cars are available for you to choose from, and you can enhance them. You can utilize the advantages you get as you advance in the game to boost your car's performance with better tires, engine tuning, and aesthetic changes.
