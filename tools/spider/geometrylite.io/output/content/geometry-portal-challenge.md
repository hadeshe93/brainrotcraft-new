**Geometry Portal Challenge** awaits you with an exciting environment to explore! This is a new user-created version based on the popular arcade game Geometry Dash in Geometry Games collection that you should not miss! 

## About Geometry Portal Challenge

Geometry Portal Challenge promises to introduce new features that will make the game of conquering rhythm more fascinating than ever! 

- Portal mechanics: This version has new features that require you to enter portals that vary your speed, gravity, and direction. This makes the game more exciting!
- Immersive soundtracks: The beat is set to high-energy music tracks that properly complement the game's action, like in previous iterations.
- Customizable Characters: In Geometry Portal Challenge, you may unlock and personalize your cube, making your journey to master rhythm even more exciting!

### How to play

Make perfect jumps to safely navigate a sequence of portals and reach the finish line. If you strike an obstacle, you will have countless retries, so don't wait and start playing right away!
