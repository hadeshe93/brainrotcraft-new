## Let's try the Scratch version of Planet Clicker

Planet Clicker is a compelling idle game created by Coltroc using the Scratch platform. Since its release, the game has gained popularity among players of all ages due to its easy yet addictive gameplay. Your mission is similar to those in the [Clicker Games](https://geometrylite.io/clicker.games) collection; simply click on your planet to develop resources such as cash, energy, and population. The more coins you earn, the more options you'll have to enhance your existing possessions.

### Introduce Upgrades in Planet Clicker
