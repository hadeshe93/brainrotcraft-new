## What is Brawl Stars Mega Simulator?

Brawl Stars Mega Simulator is a fan-made version of the well-known Brawl Stars game that is completely free to play. You will be able to enjoy the excitement of box openings, brawler unlocking, and discovering new brawlers, skins, gadgets, and star powers. Ready to start the battle in Brawl Stars Mega Simulator? Play as Brawl Stars and try to unlock all of the heroes!  

### Controls

- Tap the screen to open boxes.
- Tap the combatants to enhance them.
- Swipe to browse between menus and displays.
