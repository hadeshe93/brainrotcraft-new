**Tunnel Ball** is a basic obstacle evasion rolling game in which you manage a [ball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ball) to overcome obstacles in the tube and travel as far as possible!

## Introduce the Gameplay of Tunnel Ball 

Tunnel Ball has a deep tunnel full of difficulties for you to explore. It's time to immerse yourself in a bright world with lively music and continually shifting platforms in the tunnel. All you have to do is steer a ball across the distance between the platforms and as far as possible into the tunnel. If you hit any platform, the ball will break and the game will be over. Try to earn a high score and place your name on the global scoreboard.  

### Key Features

- Fast-paced gameplay: The ball's movement speed in the tunnel will gradually rise, resulting in fast-paced gameplay that requires you to react.
- Vibrant Visuals: This new Endless Runner game features bright and vibrant graphics.
- Multiple Levels: As you progress through the stages, the difficulty increases, allowing you to earn new ball skins and upgrades.
