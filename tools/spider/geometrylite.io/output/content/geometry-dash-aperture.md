Geometry Dash Aperture is a custom level in the Geometry Dash world that was inspired by the Portal game series. With mind-bending transitions, sci-fi visuals, and a brilliantly crafted path system, this level puts a new spin on basic [rhythm](https://geometrylite.io/rhythm.games)\-based platforming.

## **Introduce Geometry Dash Aperture**

Geometry Dash Aperture is the fourth level in the Cosmos Gauntlet, and it is a significant component of one of Geometry Dash's most visually appealing and [space](https://geometrylite.io/tag/space)\-themed collections. It was originally built for the Space Gauntlet Contest, where it finished fourth place because of its creative concept and flawless execution. The level transports players to a sleek, futuristic environment complete with luminous obstacles, laser fields, and sci-fi energy.
