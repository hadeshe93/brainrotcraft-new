## Why is Flappy Bird so addictive?

**Flappy Bird** is a 2D [side-scrolling](https://geometrylite.io/tag/side-scrolling) game that has gained popularity and recognition among platform game fans. The game was released in 2013 and has seen numerous version modifications across various platforms. And now you can play it for free on your PC or smartphone here! Let's look at why Flappy Bird has become such a popular game worldwide:

- Addictive Gameplay: Flappy Bird's combination of short rounds, a simple goal, and a competitive high-score system makes it difficult to quit playing.
- Easy Controls: Flappy Bird's one-tap control design makes it accessible to players of all ages. 
- Frustration: You'll experience the game's difficulties and frequent sensation of failure from the first few stages. This drives you to improve on future efforts.

### How to play

Your goal is to tap anywhere on the screen to make the bird flap its wings and go upward. The goal is to navigate between green pipes without hitting any other pipes or the ground.
