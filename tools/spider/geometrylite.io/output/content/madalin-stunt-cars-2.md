## Introduce Madalin Stunt Cars 2

If you want a driving game that allows you to execute stunts, Madalin Stunt Cars 2 is a must-have! You are invited to join in an exciting race in which players must demonstrate their best abilities. You will be able to explore more than 40 sports automobiles over three separate maps. Start this game by selecting your car and drifting, dragging, and racing your way to the top of this spectacular racetrack! 

### Try Madalin Stunt Cars Multiplayer Modes

Start by selecting "Multiplayer" from the main menu; you'll be able to show off your acquired abilities alongside other stunt game aficionados from all over the world. Begin by selecting your username before creating a new room or joining an existing lobby that still has space available. Select an online server from the in-game menu, and you will have the ability to compete with your teammates.

## Controls Guide

- Use W/Up Arrow to Accelerate
- Use S/Down Arrow to Decelerate
- Use A/Left Arrow to Turn Left
- Use the D/Right Arrow to Turn the Right
- Use the Spacebar to Brake
- Use Shift to Nitro
- Use G to Shift Gears
- Use C/T/B to Change Camera Angle
