## About Boxel Rebound

Boxel Rebound promises to deliver the most demanding difficulties in the platform game category. Since its release, this game has been adored by players all over the world for its basic design in classic style and hard gameplay. This game features 50 levels of increasing difficulty, providing new challenges to players of various skill levels. Remember that the black wall is always on your left; any delay or error will force you to restart the level!

### Key Features

- Addicting Gameplay: Mastering the cube's movement and overcoming the hard stages takes experience and skill.
- Challenging Level: Each stage is a meticulously designed puzzle that combines action and strategy to keep your reflexes sharp.
- Dynamic Physics Engine: Experience genuine bounce dynamics that require precision and provide a unique spin on standard platforming.
