## **Introduce Hollow Knight: Silksong in Geometry Mod!**

Get ready to dive into a brand new world where two awesome universes collide! In Hollow Knight: Silksong in Geometry Mod!, you can combine the amazing characters and items from the world of Hollow Knight with the unique style of the Geometry Mod universe. This is your chance to become a true game scientist and conduct the most exciting and crazy experiments you can imagine! 

### **How to Play**

- Place Your Pieces: To start, select an item or character from the selection panel and click to place it on the game board. It's like having your own digital playground!
- Get Your Crystals: Some special items and characters cost crystals to use. You can earn these by leveling up your profile as you play more and more!
- Move and Remove: You have full control over your experiments! You can move objects around by simply holding them down. And if you want to remove an object, just activate the trash can feature and click on it. It’s that easy!
