## Introduce Geometry Dash Yatagarasu

Geometry Dash Yatagarasu is a difficult level that promises to be a thrilling voyage of rhythm for gamers who enjoy darkness and surprises. This level was confirmed and published by TrusTa in 2017. Yatagarasu has always been known for its challenging gameplay and distinctive color scheme. Prepare for obstacles with a dark and fiery motif inspired by the mythological three-legged crow, Yatagarasu. Are you ready to begin this challenge?

## More Related Information

- Geometry Dash Yatagarasu was ranked #24 on GDToday's 100 Greatest Levels of All Time list.
- The song is 'Flawless Wings of Yatagarasu' by GlassSkinned (GLS for short), known for its terrible melody and highly diabolical tune.
- Yatagarasu's striking red look and demanding gameplay led many to believe it was a sequel to [Bloodbath](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-bloodbath), another Riot creation.
