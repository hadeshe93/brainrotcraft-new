## What is Gulper.io?

Gulper.io is a multiplayer snake game in which you must cross a luminous arena while consuming pellets and growing larger. This snake game is based on classics like Slither.io, but with faster movement, nicer controls, and a neon theme. Orbs of all sizes are spread across the arena, but you will face many opponents! Remember that quick reflexes and strategic thinking determine who becomes the biggest on the battlefield!
