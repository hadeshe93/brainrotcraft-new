**Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge** is a fast-paced platform game in which players must travel swiftly and accurately past obstacles and keep a rhythm. 

## About Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge

The level is well-known for its neon-themed colors, as well as the hard gameplay provided by the waves.

### Key Features 

- Neon Visuals: This version's most notable feature is its eye-catching neon-inspired design. The level is brimming with neon-inspired colors and effects, creating an eye-catching experience. 
- Difficult Obstacles: Similar to previous iterations, Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge has a number of challenging obstacles, like moving platforms, spikes, and other traps.
- High-Speed Gameplay: Players must complete five stages with progressively harder gameplay that tests their reflexes to get past obstacles. 

### How to play

The basic gameplay elements of the original Geometry Dash game are still present in Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge. To get over every obstacle in this level, you have to regulate your geometry jump. Use caution when handling the waves as this will greatly complicate the gameplay.
