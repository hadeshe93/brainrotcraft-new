## Enter a colorful world in Geometry Jump Sketchy

Here, a vibrant painting universe awaits your exploration and triumph over the [obstacles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacles). The primary character remains a geometrical block; to succeed, you must concentrate on trying to hop over each barrier. This level's obstacles aren't your typical straightforward ones; instead, they're pencils with sharp points. Move carefully because if you hit the pen tips, they could shatter the cube.  

## How to play

- First, select the geometric shape that best suits your needs.
- Select a running pace that is appropriate for your ability level.
- When you get close to obstacles, click the screen to make the square rotate and jump higher.

Keep in mind that the game is ended and you must attempt again if you strike any of the obstacles.
