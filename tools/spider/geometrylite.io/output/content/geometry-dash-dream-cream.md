## Gameplay Overview of Geometry Dash Dream Cream

Geometry Dash Dream Cream is noted for its complicated gameplay, vivid neon graphics, and difficult [obstacles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacles). If you want a level with a vibrant design and a high difficulty, Dream Cream is the finest level for testing your reflexes and timing. You'll go through a maze of tight passages lined with spikes, challenging portals, and intricate transitions between several gameplay forms, such as the cube, ship, and UFO. 

### Featuring 3 hidden user coins
