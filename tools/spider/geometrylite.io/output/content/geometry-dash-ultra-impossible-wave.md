## Introduce Geometry Dash: Ultra Impossible Wave

Geometry Dash: Ultra Impossible Wave promises to provide players with the ultimate skill challenge. "Wave" is a difficult problem to overcome, even for the most experienced players. This game promises to take the fast-paced features of the original Geometry Dash and push them to their limits with an extremely difficult wave mode!

### How to play

This is a precision test. Your goal is to keep the arrow traveling through the tube without hitting any obstacles. Listen to the rhythm, as it will effectively guide you to the last door!
