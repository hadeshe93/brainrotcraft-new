**Capybara Clicker** is an [idle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/idle) clicker game in which you collect coins by tapping on the cute Capybara on the screen.

## Introduce Capybara Clicker

Capybara Clicker is a well-known clicker featuring the worldwide recognized and cute main character Capybara. This game's gameplay is the same as other clicker games; all you have to do is click on the character on the screen. You can accumulate coins to unlock new characters. Select a clever upgrade approach to raise the number of coins you have. 

### Some Upgrades 

- Cursor
- Mr Clicker
- President Clicker
- King Clicker
- Pope Clicker
- God Clicker
