Welcome to **Geo Dash Mine**! This platform game promises to take you on an amazing adventure as you accompany the cube around the Minecraft landscape. 

## Introduce Geo Dash Mine

Geo Dash Mine is a creative platforming game that combines two of the most popular games, Geometry Dash and Minecraft. The iconic cube protagonist from the Geometry Dash series will begin his journey jumping across levels and overcoming obstacles in the Minecraft world. It is time to begin the voyage and see how far you can travel.

### **Controls**

- Use WASD or arrow keys to move.
- When you've decided to destroy the enemy, simply click.
