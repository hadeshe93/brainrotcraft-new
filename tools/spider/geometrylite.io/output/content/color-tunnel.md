**Color Tunnel** features eye-catching 3D tunnels to speed through! This is an endless runner game that requires you to navigate a high-speed tunnel loaded with colorful obstacles.

## Begin an exciting endless adventure in Color Tunnel

**Color Tunnel** is a fast-paced, endless runner game with recognizable gameplay. This gameplay is inspired by popular games in a similar genre, such as [Run 3](https://geometrylite.io/run-3) and [Slope](https://geometrylite.io/slope). Color Tunnel stands out with its eye-catching colorful tunnel design, which encourages players to begin their adventurous journey right away. Note that your traveling speed will eventually increase to an astonishing level, necessitating quick reflexes to overcome any obstacles. 

### How to play

Use the simple control keys listed below to navigate the tunnel full of traps and obstacles.

- To move, press AD or the left and right arrow keys.
- Once you lose, press the space bar to pause or replay.
