## Start the fast-paced multiplayer game Smash Karts 

Smash Karts offers the most intense go-kart race you've ever seen. This isn't your average race; you'll find explosive weaponry, power-ups, and other goodies as you go. In this arena, players have to complete racing missions and defend themselves from other players' attacks. It's time for you to enjoy the exhilaration of fast-paced racing combined with chaotic weapon-based fighting. Enjoy 

## How to play

- Select their character and vehicle first. 
- Enter the race and try to finish as the last kart standing or as quickly as possible. 

Remember to gather the power-ups that are strewn across the track. These could be tools and skills to defeat adversaries or defend oneself. 

### Controls

- To drive the kart, use WASD or the Arrow Keys.
- To use weapons and gathered objects, use the Space Bar.
- During gameplay, enter the key to chat.
