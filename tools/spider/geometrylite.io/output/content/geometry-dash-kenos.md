## **Geometry Dash Kenos: A Brutal Test of Precision**

Geometry Dash Kenos is a fan-favorite [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) level that’s not for the faint of heart. This level, created with 156,999 pieces and taking nearly 2 minutes to complete, has developed a reputation as one of the most difficult tasks in the Geometry Dash world. Every jump, dash, and dodge is exactly timed to match the track's fierce rhythm.

### **Features**

- High item count: The level is extremely detailed, with colourful effects and fast-moving features.
- Sync with music: Each jump, dash, and dodge is perfectly timed with the track's powerful rhythm.
- Visual overload: Expect quick flashes, changing colours, and intricate graphics that can be disorienting if you're not paying attention.
