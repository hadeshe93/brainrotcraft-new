**ZombsRoyale.io** is a fast-paced 2D battle royale game that requires players to navigate the terrain, scavenge for [weapons](https://geometrylite.io/tag/weapons) and materials, and fight to be the last survivor while avoiding hordes of merciless zombies. 

## ZombsRoyale.io: A fast-paced 2D battle royale game

**ZombsRoyale.io** promises to be a wonderful fast-paced 2D game that will give a furious and accessible battle royale experience you. You begin by parachuting onto a randomly created map, where you must quickly gather guns, armor, and other necessary items. To get an advantage in conflicts, each player must devise an effective approach strategy. Jump immediately into battle and find a range of weapons, including pistols, shotguns, assault rifles, and rocket launchers.

### Controls Guide

- Use WASD or arrow keys to move
- Use M or Tab to view the map
- Use E to interact
- Use Left click to shoot
- Use Right-click to open the emoticon list
- Use R to reload
