## About Geometry Vibes

Geometry Vibes is a new fan-made version of the Geometry Dash game. The game is based on the original version's [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) modes. In which the protagonist is an arrow. Your objective is to manage this arrow in the up-and-down direction while avoiding all obstacles. You can touch and move around the floor and ceiling without breaking. Many increasingly difficult levels await you to explore and conquer. Prepare to go on this amazing voyage right now!

### Game Modes 

- Classic Mode: Progress through ten stages that gradually increase in difficulty.
- Endless Mode: The goal is to survive as long as possible.
- Race Mode: Compete against up to four opponents in levels from Classic Mode.
