**Big Tower Tiny Square** is an exceedingly interesting platform game series in which players must complete a difficult rescue mission for Pineapple at the top of Big Tower.

## Introduce Big Tower Tiny Square

Welcome to the first installment of the Big Tower Tiny Square platform game series. This is a unique type of platform game series where players have to go on a tough escape mission and find their way through a tricky maze. Pineapple, a close friend of yours, is the main character in the game. He fails to avoid being captured by Big Square and is taken to the top of the Big Tower. To save your best friend, you need to get to the top of the tower.

## How to play

- To move, press the A/D or Arrow keys.
- To swim and jump, press SPACE.
- Jump when you are next to a wall-to-wall jump.
