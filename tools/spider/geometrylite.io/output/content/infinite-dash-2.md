## **Introduce Infinite Dash 2**

Infinite Dash 2 brings the thrill of Geometry Dash into a fan-made experience packed with action, creativity, and impossible jumps. Designed for both veterans and newcomers, this version introduces 6 custom-made levels filled with high-speed obstacles, clever traps, and rhythmic beats that test your reflexes to the limit. Jump, fly, and flip your way through a dazzling series of neon stages synced perfectly to energetic background music.

### **Game Modes**

- Wave & Ship: Soar by pressing and holding, descend by releasing.
- UFO: Jump in the air by tapping.
- Cube: Leap on the ground by tapping.
- Swingcopter: Reverse [gravity](https://geometrylite.io/tag/gravity) with a tap while airborne.
- Ball: Switch gravity while grounded with a tap.
