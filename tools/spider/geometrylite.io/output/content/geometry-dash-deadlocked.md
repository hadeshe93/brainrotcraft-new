Geometry Dash Deadlocked is one of the most intense and iconic levels in the Geometry Dash world. It is renowned for its scorching difficulty, captivating visuals, and fantastic soundtrack, and serves as a defining test for anyone looking to master the game.

## **Overview of Geometry Dash Deadlocked**

Geometry Dash Deadlocked is Geometry Dash's 20th official level, which was released in Update 2.0 by developer RobTop Games. It is rated as Demon-level difficulty, the highest tier in the official level lineup, and can only be accessed by gathering 10 secret coins from prior levels. This level's extreme difficulty, inventive dynamics, and powerful track "Deadlocked" by F-777 made it an instant favourite and a turning point. Gravity shifts, split gameplay, teleportation portals, and robot modes make Deadlocked a thrilling and terrible experience.

### **Tips and Tricks to Beat Deadlocked**

- Master the Wave Sections: Sections are tight and quick. Focus on control rather than [speed](https://geometrylite.io/tag/speed).
- Use Practice Mode Extensively: Deadlocked is almost tough to sight-read; use checkpoints to learn each part.
- Watch Others Play: Watching experienced players' performances can provide approaches and safer strategies for difficult sections.
