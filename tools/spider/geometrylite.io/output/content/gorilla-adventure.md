## **Introduce Gorilla Adventure**

Gorilla Adventure will take you on a journey to conquer the heights of the current epic urban city. Prepare your most divine skills and extreme patience to start the game. Platform [adventure](https://geometrylite.io/adventure.games) gameplay with harsh physics will make you angry but extremely fun.

Gorilla Adventure is a journey from the ground to the satellites in outer space. The segments will have different terrain characteristics. In particular, transitions through floors will have high difficulty, making it easier for the character to fall. Don't worry, because you can still continue at the drop point. Never lose!

### Gameplay 

You will navigate a gorilla trapped in an iron box, climbing the foundations of a floating city. How to direct the hammer to the most precise position is an extremely difficult puzzle to decipher.
