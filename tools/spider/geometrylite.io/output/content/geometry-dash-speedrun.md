**Geometry Dash Speedrun** is a recently updated user level in our Geometry Games collection. This version is classified as [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) and includes three user coins.

## About Geometry Dash Speedrun

Geometry Dash Speedrun brings you a fresh musical journey! This game claims to transport you to a lively music world at a quick pace. Geometry Dash Speedrun's lively music and ever-changing rhythm will push challenges to their limits. Start the level straight away and demonstrate your great reflexes by safely bringing the cube to the finish line. Don't forget to gather all three user coins to complete the level correctly!

### Coins Located 

- The first coin emerges at 15%.
- The second coin is located at 50%. 
- Finally, at 98%, the user coin appears at the end of the level.
