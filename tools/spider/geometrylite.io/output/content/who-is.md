**Who Is** is a fun and hard guessing game that puts your brain to the test as you solve a variety of puzzles such as detecting imposters, finding hidden objects, and answering riddles.

## Introduce the Gameplay of Who Is

Start playing **Who Is** - A popular puzzle and riddle game in which you are presented with a hundred hard scenarios that require you to think outside the box to answer them. The Who Are questions concentrate on predetermined scenarios that incorporate both humans and objects. Each character in the story can provide hints to help you solve the questions at each level. Let us begin the game and test your brain!

### Controls Guide 

- Interact with the characters in the photo by using your mouse.
- Click to select the solution for each level based on the level's suggestions and your own opinion.
