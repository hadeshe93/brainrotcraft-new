**Friday Night Dashing** is a rhythm-based platformer that creates distinctive and thrilling battles by fusing the greatest elements of FNF and Geometry Dash.

## Feel to the Beat in Friday Night Dashing

Friday Night Dashing is a [Friday Night Funkin'](https://geometrylite.io/tag/friday-night-funkin) mod that features amazing music! The two musical universes of Geometry Dash and [FNF](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fnf) come together once more to provide players with an amazing experience. Boyfriend enters the well-known Geometry Dash universe in this edition and engages in a rap war with some of the locals. 

### List of songs

- Geometrical Dominator
- Striker
- Infiltration
- At the Speed of Light

## How to play

As soon as you select the game mode, Geometry Dash and your partner will start fighting. By using catchy tunes to overcome your opponent in a series of rhythm matches, you want to impress your sweetheart. To win each round, you simply have to hit the right notes at the right moment.

**Controls**

To strike notes, use the Arrow or WASD keys.
