## Soccer Random: An Attractive Game for 2 Players

Soccer Random is a brand-new sports game for two players in a random way. With a unique random style and classic soccer principles, the game offers a lively and chaotic soccer universe. With every level bringing new characters, landscapes, and other surprises, soccer will be played in a very entertaining and unexpected way in Soccer Random. Let's get started so you can enjoy this thrilling soccer match for yourself right now!

## How to play 

Choose from one-player and two-player options to begin. On the same gadget, you can compete with your closest buddy or the CPU. There is little strategy in this fast-paced game, every move is unpredictable, and you never know when your opponent will alter the strength of their kick. To get the ball inside your opponent's goal, make intelligent passes and moves.

### Controls

To jump and kick, press W (Player 1) or up arrow (Player 2).
