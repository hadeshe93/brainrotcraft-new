## **Introduce Tung Tung Sahur in Geometry Dash**

Tung Tung Sahur in Geometry Dash is a creative fan-made spin inspired by the classic wave mode. Get ready for [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) action! Dodge spikes, leap over tricky traps, and sync your moves to the rhythmic “tung-tung!” beat. Players join Tung Tung Sahur, the dawn caller with his wooden drum, and guide him through a glowing neon city just before sunrise.

Wave control: hold to rise, release to fall—responsive to clicks, spacebar, or mouse taps.

### **Features**

- Main character: Tung Tung Sahur, the meme icon with his “tung-tung!” drum, redesigned in glowing neon style.
- Vibrant city background filled with glowing gates, speed pads, and [gravity](https://geometrylite.io/tag/gravity) flips that keep you on your toes.
- Exclusive wave mode gameplay—move up and down in sync with the rhythm to navigate tight spaces.
