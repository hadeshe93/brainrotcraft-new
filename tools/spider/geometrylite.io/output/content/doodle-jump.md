In the timeless classic platform game **Doodle Jump**, your goal is to guide The Doodler between platforms and up as high as you can.

## History of Doodle Jump

The mobile platform game Doodle Jump was first released in 2009 and is loved by many players. With its endearing artwork and straightforward mechanics, this endearing and addictive platformer has quickly gained popularity worldwide. The character The Doodler has to climb as high as he can by leaping on platforms, which adds intrigue to the game while maintaining the fundamental gameplay of platform games. Are you prepared to take on the challenge and play Doodle Jump for hours on end?

## How to play

- To move, use the left/right arrows.
- To shoot, use the left click.

### More Power-Ups 

Remember to gather hearts and coins. Coins can be used to buy power-ups, and hearts give you an extra life if you fall. Additionally, use power-ups strategically because each one has a special ability, and, depending on the circumstance, some may be more useful than others.
