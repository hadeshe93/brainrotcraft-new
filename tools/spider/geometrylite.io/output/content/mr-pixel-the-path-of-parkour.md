## **Mr. Pixel: The Path of Parkour - Step Into the Pixel Abyss**

Mr. Pixel: The Path of Parkour is a tough-as-nails 2D platformer that tests your reflexes, patience, and timing in a pixelated world full of deadly surprises. Every level hides spikes, lava pools, and tricky platforms waiting to punish the smallest mistake. With 24 handcrafted stages, this game dares you to jump, dodge, and master precision movement to reach the finish line.

### **Master the Moves**

- Move: A/D or Arrow Keys
- Jump: Space / W / Up Arrow
- Avoid: Spikes, lava, and traps that appear out of nowhere
- Goal: Reach the end alive. No checkpoints. No mercy.

### **Why You’ll Love This Game**

If you crave real challenge and the satisfaction of beating the impossible, Mr. Pixel: The Path of Parkour delivers. It’s punishing but fair, frustrating yet fun — every retry pushes you closer to mastery. This is more than a game; it’s a test of will, timing, and pure gamer spirit.
