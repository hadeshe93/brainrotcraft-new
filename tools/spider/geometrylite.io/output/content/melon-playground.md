## About Melon Playground

**Melon Playground** is an independent game inspired by the popular Steam game People Playground. To be safe in this game, you must create an arsenal of materials to prepare for confrontations that could occur at any time. Your character can make guns, explosive devices, and fortresses to assist him stay safe. Start the game now and use your imagination to create intriguing gameplay in your unique style.

### Controls

To control your character in this game, use the mouse and arrow keys to conduct actions.
