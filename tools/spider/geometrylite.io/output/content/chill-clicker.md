## Relax with the clicker game Chill Clicker

Chill Clicker is a new version based on [Chill Guy Clicker](https://geometrylite.io/chill-guy-clicker). This clicker features Chill Guy, a popular mascot across all worldwide social networking platforms! Chill Clicker's basic gameplay allows you to enjoy some light enjoyment. Unlike fast-paced games that require continual attention, Chill Clicker offers exceptionally "chill" gameplay in which all you have to do is click!

### How to play

Click on the character on the screen to generate a large number of chill dudes. As your points accrue, you can use them to obtain rewards and advance your progress over time. Many new updates will help automate your chill dude construction procedure and boost your score quickly.
