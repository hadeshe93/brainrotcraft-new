**Shooting Master! Craft the Gun!** is a simple action-packed adventure in which you race to aim and acquire as many weapons as possible.

## Gameplay of Shooting Master! Craft the Gun!

Shooting master! Craft the Gun! takes you on a fantastic voyage of sprinting, aiming, and gathering weaponry. This task will be a lot easier if you only need to select targets on the left or right and shoot precisely. Some doors will increase or reduce your bullet count, so select wisely! Progress through the game and discover ways to multiply the money you earn in each level several times more. You can use this money to unlock a wide range of weapons in Shooting Master! Create the Gun!. 

**Controls**

Move your character to the left or right with the mouse.
