Vex 9 is the newest chapter of the legendary [stickman](https://geometrylite.io/tag/stickman) platformer series. Run, jump, and survive deadly traps through 20 thrilling parkour stages.

## **Vex 9 - The Next Leap in Stickman Action**

Vex 9 takes everything players love about the series—speed, precision, and creativity—and refines it into the most polished version yet. As the latest chapter in the iconic Vex platformer saga, this installment introduces smoother animations, smarter enemy traps, and dynamic level designs that keep you constantly on your toes. The game features 10 main stages that steadily ramp up in complexity and 10 bonus hardcore levels crafted for those who crave extreme precision.

### **Master the Art of Parkour**

You’ll guide your agile stickman through dangerous mazes of spikes, spinning blades, and crumbling platforms, where every move counts. Master wall jumps, slides, and mid-air tricks to overcome challenging sections that reward quick thinking and steady hands. Whether you’re a veteran of the Vex universe or a first-time player, Vex 9 offers a perfect [balance](https://geometrylite.io/tag/balance) of difficulty and satisfaction.

**Controls:**

- Arrow Keys / WASD: Move, jump, and crouch
- R: Restart from the last checkpoint
- M: Return to the main menu
