## **Hollow Knight Overview – Enter the Kingdom Beneath**

Hollow Knight is an award-winning Metroidvania action-adventure developed by Team Cherry. Set in the mysterious underground world of Hallownest, the game invites you to step into the role of a silent knight on a journey through ancient cities, overgrown forests, and insect-infested caverns. With fluid combat, intricate platforming, and a haunting atmosphere, Hollow Knight is both brutal and breathtaking.

### **Explore, Fight, and Survive**

Every corridor in Hallownest hides secrets, powerful bosses, and hidden treasures. Players must master precise combat and agile movement to overcome deadly foes and treacherous terrain. You’ll gather charms to customize your abilities, Geo to purchase upgrades, and abilities like dash, wall jump, and double jump to reach previously inaccessible areas.

### **Controls**

- UP Arrow Key: Move up / Hold to look upward
- DOWN Arrow Key: Move down / Hold to look downward
- LEFT / RIGHT Arrow Keys: Move horizontally
- \[Z\]: Jump
- \[X\]: Attack
- \[A\]: Hold to Focus or travel via Dreamgate
- \[D\]: Hold to use Dream Nail or place a Dreamgate
