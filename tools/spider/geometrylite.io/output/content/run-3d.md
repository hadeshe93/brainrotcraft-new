Welcome to the thrilling world of Run 3D! This well-known endless runner game promises to take you on an incredibly thrilling adventure by fusing fast-paced action with platforming features.

## Discovering Run 3D's Exciting Universe

You will go through tunnels in outer space in Run 3D, which is packed with obstacles and traps. To reach the objective of traveling as far as possible, your character, a small extraterrestrial, must navigate an increasingly difficult series. To keep on course, simply hop and change your gravity. As you advance in the game and earn enough money, you can unlock other characters, each of whom has a distinct appearance.

### How to play

- Use the A/D or left/right arrow keys to move.
- Use the up arrow key or the spacebar to jump.

Keep in mind that the complexity of the game increases as you advance since you will run across more gaps and obstacles. This is an excellent chance for you to sharpen your abilities to get better grades. Have fun!
