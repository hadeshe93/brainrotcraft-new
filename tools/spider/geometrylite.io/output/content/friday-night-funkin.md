## Introduce the rhythm-based game Friday Night Funkin

Friday Night Funkin ([FNF](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fnf)) is a popular original game that has been around for many years. It is a music game with simple yet very appealing gameplay. The game's main characters are boyfriend and girlfriend. In all versions, the Boyfriend must use his musical ability to win the heart of the Girlfriend (a music lover). In each edition, the Boyfriend will fight various opponents, but all guarantee exciting battles and wonderful music.

### Game modes

- **Story Mode**: You will follow the protagonist, the boyfriend, as he attempts to persuade the girlfriend's father to approve of their relationship.
- **Free Play Mode**: In free play mode, you can play any song in the game without having to follow the boyfriend's story.

## Gameplay of Friday Night Funkin
