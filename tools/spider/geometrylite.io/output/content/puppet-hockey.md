## About Puppet Hockey

Puppet Hockey will add excitement to the hockey field like never before! This game recreates top hockey gameplay of professional players in an intriguing cartoon manner that you should not miss. Step onto the pitch and remember the goal: Score more goals than your opponent in 60 seconds. 

### Controls

- Use arrow keys to move.
- utilize the F key to hit or utilize tap controls.
