## **Italian Brainrot: Neuro Beasts - Game Overview**

Set in the brainrot multiverse, Italian Brainrot: Neuro Beasts builds on the viral popularity of [Italian Brainrot Clicker](https://geometrylite.io/italian-brainrot-clicker). This game, created by Superec Games, is intended to be a strangely compelling experience, mixing the simple principles of a clicker with an endless stream of weird, AI-generated "neuro-animals" and amusing Italian-themed themes. All you have to do in this game is tap, gather, and enhance your team of bizarre animals, which are inspired by the unvarnished corners of Italian meme culture.

### **How to play**

- Click on the screen to win "neurone chips" and "pizzas," which are the game's main money.
- Each level often unlocks new, progressively weird "neuro-animals" for the player to acquire.
- Focus early upgrades on auto-clickers to accelerate progress.
