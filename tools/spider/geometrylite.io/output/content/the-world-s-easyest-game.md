**The World's Easyest Game** is a simple puzzle game that presents players with deceptively simple yet intriguing challenges with surprising answers.

## Overview Gameplay of The World’s Easyest Game

The [Puzzle Games](https://geometrylite.io/puzzle.games) collection has recently been updated with a new and fascinating game called The World's Easyest Game. This game promises to give players relatively simple challenges that are often more difficult than they appear. This makes the game more exciting and unexpected. There is no need to prepare a lot of knowledge for this game. Show off your confidence and inventiveness by solving the problems in The World's Easyest Game! 

### How to play

Begin the game and discover the intriguing riddles in The World's Easyest Game. These are multiple-choice questions with pre-answered answers; all you have to do is pick your choices to get the correct answer.
