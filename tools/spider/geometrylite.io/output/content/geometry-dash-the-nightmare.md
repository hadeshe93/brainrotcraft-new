### Tips and tricks

When the player enters the cube portal, one of the level's most intricate sections starts. It will send you into a ship portal via a yellow pad. Here, tiny colored blocks serve as your guides as you make your way through a flood of unseen block pillars. All set to launch into a Cycles-esque ship sequence that will continually change gravity after that.
