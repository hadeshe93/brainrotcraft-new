Welcome to **We Become What We Behold**! Participants in this quick and thought-provoking game can see how social media can highlight little distinctions in our lives.

## Interesting facts about We Become What We Behold 

We Become What We Behold is a point-and-click game developed by Nutcase Nightmare and published on Kongregate in 2016 that reflects the current state of social media. Due to its distinctive gameplay, which mirrored the current state of social media networks, this game quickly attracted the attention of online gamers worldwide. The game is based on the famous phrase about the media industry: "We become what we see. We shape our tools, and they shape us." 

## How to play

When you start this game, a large number of characters will emerge on the screen at random. All you have to do is take a picture of the characters you believe are special. The image will immediately emerge on television for everyone to see. Stay tuned for the game's reaction; it's a striking reminder of the impact social media has on our lives.
