**Geometry Monster Dash** is a thrilling and fast-paced platformer inspired by Geometry Dash, the renowned rhythm-based game in which you and your cube must overcome all obstacles.

## Introduce Geometry Monster Dash

Geometry Monster Dash is a new version of the Geometry Dash game that features imaginative and addicting action. This game combines rhythmic gameplay with exhilarating obstacle-dodging action. Like in the original edition, you must guide the Geometry character through dangerous environments while avoiding traps and foes. Immerse yourself in bright music and begin your adventure straight now. This version includes 20 monsters that you must defeat to accomplish the challenge. 

### How to play

- Overcome obstacles by clicking or pushing the space bar.
- Collect all stars on your adventure to better your results.
