## **Geometry Wave: Challenge – Blue-Toned Wave Action**

Geometry Wave: Challenge is a brand-new fan-made creation by WayneGames, offering 8 unique stages inspired by the intense Wave mode of Geometry Dash. With its blue, black, and white color scheme, the design ensures that players can spot patterns and obstacles, making each level both challenging and visually striking.

### **Features**

- Dynamic [obstacle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacle) layouts that change timing and spacing as you progress.
- Fluid wave movement for smooth, responsive controls even at high speed.
- Level-specific patterns where each stage introduces its own visual traps.

### **How to Play Geometry Wave: Challenge**

Control your wave character as it moves forward, tapping or clicking to change its vertical direction. Time your moves precisely to dodge spikes, gaps, and tricky patterns. Mastering the rhythm and flow of each level is key to survival.

- Mouse click or spacebar – change [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) direction.
- Maintain steady control and avoid over-tapping to keep your grip secure.
