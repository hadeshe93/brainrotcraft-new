Welcome to **Escape Road Halloween**! This is a new installment in the Escape Road Series, an exciting driving game that immerses you in a daring escape scenario set against a frightful [Halloween](https://geometrylite.io/tag/halloween) background.

## Introduce Gameplay of Escape Road Halloween

Escape Road Halloween, like previous versions of the game, requires you to create an escape strategy and drive carefully to evade capture. The game offers fast-paced chases in which you must avoid police cars, tanks, helicopters, and other obstacles such as buildings and trees. Don't forget to gather the money bags spread around the city. You can use this to improve your car, increasing your chances of a successful escape.

### Controls Guide

To start and drive your car, press the A/D or left and right arrow keys.
