**Slow Roads** is a unique driving game with a slow speed that allows players to relax while driving around small, tranquil lanes lined with flowers.

## Let's try Slow Roads, a slow-paced driving game!  

In Slow Roads, speed is no longer a problem! Simply enjoy the calm experience of driving in this new game. You aim to drive down twisting, desolate roads. You don't have to compete with anyone; just enjoy the voyage and take up the quiet atmosphere. If you've had enough of fast-paced, competitive games, Slow Roads is a terrific way to unwind! 

### Controls

- Use the W/Up Arrow to accelerate.
- Use the S/Down Arrow to decelerate/reverse
- Use the A/Left Arrow to Turn Left.
- Use the D/Right Arrow to Turn Right.
- Use Shift to boost.
