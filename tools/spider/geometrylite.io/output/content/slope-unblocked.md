Welcome to **Slope Unblocked**! This Endless Runner game requires you to guide the ball as it travels through restricted paths, dodges obstacles, and accelerates over time.

## Discover the Addictive Fun of Slope Unblocked 

Slope Unblocked is an unblocked version of the popular arcade game [Slope](https://geometrylite.io/slope), in which you guide a ball down an infinite slope full of hazards. The gameplay has not changed since the original edition, but the new version promises to provide apparently infinite fun and challenges! Let's play Slope Unblocked whenever and whenever we want to enjoy this amazing entertainment experience! 

### How to play

- To control the movement of the ball, use the left and right arrow keys or A/D.
- Keep the ball in the center of the slope so you have time to react to sudden curves or obstructions.
