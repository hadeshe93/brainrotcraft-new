**Geometry Vibes Monster** is a fast-paced, [fan-made](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fan-made) game inspired by the classic Geometry Dash. Dive into a rhythm-based platform adventure filled with spikes, monsters, and non-stop action!

## **Introduce Geometry Vibes Monster**

Geometry Vibes Monster combines the core mechanics of Geometry Dash with new game modes and a wild twist—monster battles. You’ll control a cube character that moves to the beat while dodging dangerous obstacles, deadly spikes, and facing off against strange creatures. The game features multiple modes, including Endless Mode for survival fans and Race Mode, where up to 4 players can compete side by side. With sharp timing and quick reflexes, each level is an exciting, fast-moving challenge!

### **Controls**

**Single Player:**

- Click and hold the mouse or press and hold the SPACEBAR to ascend
- Release to dive downward

**Multiplayer Race Mode (2–4 Players):**

- Player 1: UP ARROW
- Player 2: SPACEBAR
- Player 3: H
- Player 4: L
