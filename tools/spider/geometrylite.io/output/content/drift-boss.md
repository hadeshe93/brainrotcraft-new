## Introduce the best driving game Drift Boss

When you start playing Drift Boss, you must mentally prepare for a risky ride as soon as the engines start! The course is not easy to navigate; many obstacles must be overcome, including abrupt twists and other racers. In this game, try to cross the finish line as quickly as possible to get points and unlock more awesome automobiles. Remember to monitor your score because your objective is to top the leaderboard!

## How to play

The objective of this game is to steer an automobile as far as you can without crashing. 

- Press and hold the space bar to go left. To go right, let go of the pressure. 
- Try to spin for as long as possible without hitting anything or going off the track.
