## **Introduce Geometry Dash Stereo Madness**

Geometry Dash Stereo Madness is the first level in both Geometry Dash and [Geometry Dash Lite](https://geometrylite.io/), providing an excellent introduction to the game's rhythm-based platformer. Its [Easy](https://geometrylite.io/tag/easy) difficulty level is intended to teach players the fundamentals—[jumping](https://geometrylite.io/tag/jumping), timing, and synchronising to music—without overwhelming them. However, don't be deceived by its simplicity; it still provides a fun and rewarding challenge!

### **The First Step Into Rhythm Gaming**

The first level lasts 89 seconds and requires at least 77 leaps to complete. Its simple layout and forgiving patterns make it an excellent tutorial level, while pleasing gamers of all ages.

### **Coin Hunt: 3 Hidden Challenges**

There are three user coins to gather, at 50%, 74%, and 98%. These necessitate precise diversions or carefully timed jumps. Mastering the level with all coins increases replay value even after you've finished it.
