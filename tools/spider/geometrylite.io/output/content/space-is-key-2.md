## Introduce Space Is Key 2

Space Is Key is back in its second installment, Space Is Key 2, with more pixel-exploding lunacy to test your abilities! This is not a simple path, as more hurdles have been added. Be prepared to precisely press the spacebar to control your character as you navigate a series of platforms and return safely. This version contains over 30 updated levels, including both original and challenge mode levels. Are you convinced that you can overcome all of them? 

### Tips to play

All you have to do is press the Space Bar appropriately to help your character avoid obstacles and safely cross the finish line.
