## Rainbow Friends Return: A colorful yet creepy world

Rainbow Friends Return brings back the vibrant yet spooky world of Rainbow Friends with a completely new twist! It's time to embark on a tense survival adventure in which you must outwit the spooky yet playful Rainbow Friends. This exhilarating adventure holds many secrets for you to discover, including new characters, unexpected twists, and even more heart-pounding chase scenes.

### How to play

Start this game to explore unusual areas, solve riddles, and avoid getting caught by the Rainbow Friends. The game is full of traps and surprises, and your goal is to collect the necessary objects to unlock the final door.

**Controls**

- To move around, use WASD.
- Press the spacebar to jump.
- To pause, press Tab.
- Use E to hide.
