In the driving game **Sledge Rider**, you have to maneuver your sled through a variety of slopes during a frigid winter that is full of hazards. Are you ready to master the freezing slopes?

## Gameplay of Sledge Rider

Keep in mind that your objective is to use your sled to go down a hill covered in snow while dodging obstructions like rocks and trees. There will be plenty of opportunities for you to take in stunning [winter](https://geometrylite.io/tag/winter) scenery. Savor the excitement of speeding downhill since your score will increase with how quickly you can descend without running into obstacles. Remember that gathering the stars strewn around the incline will also greatly boost your point total. 

### Controls 

To steer, press the left and right arrow keys.
