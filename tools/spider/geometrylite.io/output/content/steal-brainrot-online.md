## **What Is Steal Brainrot Online?**

Steal Brainrot Online is a chaotic, fast-paced casual heist game where your goal is simple — collect as many viral Brainrots as possible while stealing them from other players around the world. Inspired by internet meme culture, this title turns humor, chaos, and competition into an unpredictable [multiplayer](https://geometrylite.io/tag/multiplayer) experience.

In this world, Brainrots are the new form of currency — hilarious viral icons like Tralalero Tralala, Ballerina Cappuccino, Tung Tung Sahur, and Cappuccino Assassino become your prized possessions. Build your collection, protect your stash, and outsmart rivals who are just as eager to take them from you.

### **Gameplay Overview**

Players run, jump, and strategize to grab rare Brainrots scattered across the map while dodging traps and rival thieves. Each match is a blend of speed, timing, and chaos, where every player competes to become the ultimate collector. Lose focus, and someone might swipe your hard-earned memes right from under your nose.

**Controls:**

- Use WASD: Move
- Use Spacebar: Jump
