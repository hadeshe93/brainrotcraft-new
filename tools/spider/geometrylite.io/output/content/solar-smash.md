In **Solar Smash**, are you prepared to destroy every planet in this universe? You can experience chaos and devastation in this planet destruction simulator game.

## Introduce Solar Smash

The demolition simulation game Solar Smash was created by Paradyme Games. You have the opportunity to investigate and try out a range of [weapons](https://geometrylite.io/tag/weapons) and tactics to accomplish the goal of destroying solar system planets in this game. Numerous weapons, like nuclear missiles, laser beams, asteroids, spacecraft, and terrifying alien monsters, are just waiting for you to use them to create a war and wipe out the world. Ready to begin and finish this fantastic mission?

## How to play

It is possible to utilize a wide range of weapons to wipe out an entire planet in Solar Smash. As you gain more bonuses, you will be able to improve your weapons and unlock other planets. To begin, utilize the default weaponry and destroy the earth.  

**Controls** 

To gradually destroy the generated planet images, click or hold on to them with your mouse.
