**Moto X3M 4: Winter** is a thrilling motorbike stunt game set in a harsh winter environment that builds on the previous installments of the [Moto X3M series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/moto-x3m).

## Conquer Snowy Tracks with Thrilling Stunts in Moto X3M 4 Winter! 

[Moto X3M](https://geometrylite.io/moto-x3m) returns in the fourth part: Moto X3M 4 Winter features numerous special winter-themed challenges. You will once again use your motorcycle to compete in high-speed races, complex stunts, and difficult obstacle courses. However, this time, winter-themed activities may prove considerably more challenging.

### Main obstacles

Players in Moto X3M 4 Winter will confront a variety of tough terrains, including ice patches, snowbanks, and frozen obstacles that will test their ability to retain balance and control. Please keep in mind that driving in cold, icy conditions is more challenging. Danger lurks everywhere, especially around corners!

### Controls Guide

- To accelerate, press Up Arrow or W. 
- To brake or reverse, press the down arrow or S.
