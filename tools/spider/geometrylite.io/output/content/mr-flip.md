Mr Flip turns every jump into a daring [parkour](https://geometrylite.io/tag/parkour) stunt where you must flip high, land precision-perfect, and build big combos. In this parkour game you’ll use simple controls to assess the environment, adjust momentum and show off your flipping flair.

## Mr Flip - Flip High, Land Clean, Score Big

In Mr Flip, your goal is simple: jump high, rotate your body in the air, and stick the landing with perfect control. Each leap requires you to read the environment, build the right power, and choose the correct moment to stop spinning. Clean landings earn points, while risky flips create powerful combos. The more precise you are, the higher your score — one mistake and your landing falls apart, so timing is everything!

### Parkour Style & Simple Controls

Mr Flip belongs to the parkour stunt genre, made with a kid-friendly style and smooth animations. The game feels like a fun, modern parkour challenge, perfect for casual players. Controls are easy to learn:

- Hold Mouse: Charge jump power
- Release: Jump
- Hold Mid-air: Flip and rotate
- Release Again: Lock your landing
