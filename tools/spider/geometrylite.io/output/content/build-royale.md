**Build Royale** is a multiplayer .io game where you must survive in a place encircled by a poisonous ring. Become the sole survivor and winner of the royal battle! 

## About Build Royale

Build Royale is an exciting battle royale IO game that awaits your exploration! This is a new action game that requires you to create defenses while battling other players from across the world. Build your region, collect weapons, fortify your defenses, and survive in Build Royale. Be the last player standing by upgrading swiftly and using weapons and supplies to repel enemies. To begin playing this game, you can select one of three game modes:

- Solo
- Duo
- Squad

### Controls

- Use E and F to interact with ground items and chests.
- Use the left mouse button to shoot your weapon.
- Use the right mouse button to Scope to see adversaries from a distance.
- To create walls, toggle the build mode by pressing Q.
- Use the space to jump over walls and other obstacles. Shift Sprint
- Press R to reload your weapon.
- Use the tab to view and manage your inventory.
