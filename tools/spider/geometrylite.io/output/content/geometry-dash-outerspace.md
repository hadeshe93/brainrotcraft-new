**Geometry Dash OuterSpace** is the Fire Gauntlet's first [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard) level, and it presents many fascinating difficulties to gamers who enjoy this platform game.

## About Geometry Dash OuterSpace

Geometry Dash OuterSpace enthusiasts should prepare for a tough obstacle course experience! OuterSpace claims to transport players to a realm filled with vibrant, dynamic levels, hard challenges, and pulsating beats. This level's challenge has a difficulty rating of 5 stars and is suited for both beginners and expert players. Collect all three user coins in OuterSpace to finish your quest. 

### Found all the coins in Geometry Dash OuterSpace.

- The first coin: At 14%, fly up just high enough to avoid colliding with the ceiling spikes and grab these coins.
- The second coin: At 57%, jump without hitting the green jump orb, then press the blue gravity pad adjacent to the coin to return to the normal route.
- The final coin: At 74%, fly under a laser shot by the monster near the ground or drop to the floor completely to get the final coin.
