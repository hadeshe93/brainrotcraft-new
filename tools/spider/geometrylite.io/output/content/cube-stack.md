**Cube Stack** is a fantastic cube-stacking game that is sure to help you relax. Now begin stacking countless multicolor cubes!

## About Cube Stack 

Starting Cube Stack, players can test their talents and strategies. In this stacking cubes game, your goal is simple: stack as many cubes as you can without them falling. To accomplish this, the main technique is to carefully stack each cube on top of the preceding one. A sturdy base will allow you to simply stack the cubes as high as feasible. The game's straightforward, easy-to-understand gameplay makes it appealing and addictive, yet mastery is difficult to achieve. Begin now to discover how tall you can make your cube stack. 

### How to play

Stack the cubes by clicking with your mouse.
