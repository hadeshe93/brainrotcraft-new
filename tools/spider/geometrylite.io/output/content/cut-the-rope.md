## Introduce Cut The Rope

Cut The Rope is a popular puzzle game series with very addicting gameplay. This game requires players to solve physics-based puzzles by cutting ropes to feed a charming little creature named Om Nom candies. To achieve this goal, players must select a reasonable [cutting](https://geometrylite.io/tag/cutting) strategy and overcome all obstacles in each stage. Each level contains a variety of obstacles and riddles that must be solved with intelligent thought and quick reactions. 

### Tips to play

To complete all levels, players must select how to cut the rope strategically. Because the glue's trajectory will be influenced by numerous environmental elements such as air bubbles, trampolines, and platforms. The higher the level, the more complicated the challenges will be; this is an excellent opportunity for you to hone your logical thinking skills. 

Each level also includes stars as added rewards. In addition to the purpose of gifting Om Nom sweets, select a clever trajectory to get as many stars as possible.
