## **Dash to the Beat with Geometry Dash BuTiTi!**

Get ready to jump, fly, and dash through an amazing world in Geometry Dash BuTiTi! This incredible level, created and published by the famous JonathanGD, is a super fun and [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) adventure. With its impressive scenery that changes all the time, you'll be on the edge of your seat as you try to conquer this challenging level!

### **A Challenge from a Master!**

BuTiTi is a [Harder](https://geometrylite.io/harder.games) 7-star level, so it’s perfect for players who love a good challenge! This is one of the best level creators in Geometry Dash. He made sure every part of this level is exciting and fun!

- Awesome Scenery: Get ready to be amazed! The scenery in the level is impressive and constantly changing, so you'll never know what awesome design you'll see next.
- A Great Song: This level is set to the cool song "Milkshake" by Bossfight. The [music](https://geometrylite.io/tag/music) is a perfect match for the fast pace and makes the game even more exciting!

### **Master the Fast-Paced Adventure!**

- Look Ahead: With the fast pace of BuTiTi, it's super important to look ahead to see what's coming. Pay attention to the colors and designs so you can get ready for the next jump or fly section.
- Try the Whole Level: The level is 1 minute and 59 seconds long, which is a great length for a fun and exciting run! See if you can make it all the way to the end without crashing!
