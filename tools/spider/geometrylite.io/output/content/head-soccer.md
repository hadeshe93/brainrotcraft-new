**Head Soccer** is a dynamic soccer game in which two players with large heads compete on both offense and defense.

## Introduce Head Soccer

Head Soccer is a new title that has recently been added to our [Soccer Games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/soccer). In this very dynamic game, players must continually move to demonstrate their ability and skillfully dribble the ball. Head Soccer's easy and appealing gameplay has helped it reach a large number of players and become a popular soccer game.

### Features

- Match of one-on-one soccer battles: You can participate in this exciting one-on-one soccer match, which creates a tense and fiercely competitive environment. 
- Several Characters: This year's Head Soccer tournament has drawn well-known players from all across the world. To begin the match, you can select from a wide range of characters. 
- Different Game Modes: Play this game in two-player mode on the same computer as your best friend.

## How to play

In fast-paced soccer matches, you have to be in command of both offense and defense. Players advance toward the opponent's goal along a single vertical axis. You must precisely move your character to block the ball from getting to your goal and deliver it in the direction of your opponent.
