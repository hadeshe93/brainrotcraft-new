Welcome to **GeoGuessr**! This game will put your geography knowledge to the test as you answer questions on prominent tourist destinations across the world that appear on the screen.  

## Start your journey of discovery in GeoGuessr! 

GeoGuessr is a new puzzle game that tests players' abilities to solve geographically oriented riddles. The game uses Google Street View to take players around the world. You will explore well-known tourist landmarks around the world, as well as neighborhoods that embody each country's culture. Your objective is to provide the correct answers for these sites. Are you confident in your geographic knowledge? Start playing today and prove it! 

### Tips to play

Use the Google Street View camera wisely and successfully to find tiny clues in the photographs. You must carefully examine every feature in the image, including specific locations, vegetation, architectural styles, and even road signs. All of these can provide hints about the final answer.
