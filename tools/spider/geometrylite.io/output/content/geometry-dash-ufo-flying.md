**Geometry Dash: UFO Flying** is an interesting new version of the Geometry Dash Game. It is time to accompany a [UFO](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ufo) to overcome barriers in the music industry.

## Introduce Gameplay Geometry Dash: UFO Flying

In Geometry Dash: UFO Flying, instead of managing a cube, you must fly a UFO through an obstacle course. This version features fast-paced action and complex level designs, making it a new and exciting journey for Geometry Dash aficionados. Control the UFO to fly up at the proper time based on the beat to dodge all obstacles and finish the level!
