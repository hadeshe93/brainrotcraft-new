**Getaway Shootout is** an action-packed two-player game that blends intense platforming with fast-paced shooting. You must navigate chaotic landscapes full of rivals and obstacles while fighting to escape.  

## How to play Getaway Shootout

- Choose a game mode to begin playing this game: Play two on the same computer and compete against a buddy or the AI (Single Player mode).
- Reach the getaway goal on each map by engaging in combat with adversaries.
- Remember to gather weapons and perks that will help you defeat your opponent. 

### Tips and tricks

You need to continue to move to dodge enemy fire to be safe in this game. It will be tough for opponents to hit you if you keep moving as they attack. Additionally, remember to increase your arsenal by using the perks. Your advantage over various opponents in each stage will increase with the variety of weaponry you possess.
