**Geometry Dash Chamber of Time** is a mystery hidden region in the Geometry Dash universe that contains secret mythology, enigmatic messages, and hidden gameplay elements.

## **Introduce Geometry Dash Chamber of Time**

**Geometry Dash Chamber of Time** is not a level that can be completed quickly. Instead, it's a secret room that must be unlocked by performing different game activities, such as interacting with the Vault of Secrets, the Gatekeeper, and meeting certain requirements. Once opened, it reveals further mythology and grants entrance to The Challenge, an extra level accessible only through the Chamber.

### **Gameplay**

- First, acquire enough silver user coins to access the Vault of Secrets.
- Enter the code "The Challenge" into the Vault of Secrets.
- Complete the Challenge to get a key.
- After completing the preceding procedures, navigate to the Coming Soon door on the main menu and enter it.
- To get admission to the Chamber of Time, solve the riddles and speak with the Gatekeeper.
