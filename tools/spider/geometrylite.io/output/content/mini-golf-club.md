## Introduce Mini Golf Club

Mini Golf Club is a free multiplayer game that claims to provide you with a fun and unique experience with the classic golf game. Your goal remains to hit the ball into the target by following the proper ball route, avoiding obstacles, and using just enough force. Keep in mind that the objective is to make as few attempts as possible to get the ball into the hole. This is a great chance for you to learn how to play golf on a small grass course.

## How to play

- Click to hit the ball.
- Press C to change the camera.
