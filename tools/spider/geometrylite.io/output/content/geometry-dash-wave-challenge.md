**Geometry Dash: Wave Challenge** is a famous platform game based on the classic Geometry Dash in which players must guide a small arrow to the finish line safely!

## Introduce Wave mode in Geometry Dash: Wave Challenge

Geometry Dash: Wave Challenge introduces [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) mode gameplay, a popular and well-known subgenre of the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) series. Instead of manipulating a cube, Wave Challenge requires you to move a little arrow across levels. The arrow does not jump, fly, or dodge obstacles; instead, it moves in a zigzag fashion, generating long waves to the final point. Your goal is to keep the arrow under control and avoid obstacles along the route.

### Controls

- Click and hold the left mouse button to soar up.
- Don't click anything to fly down.

Geometry Dash: Wave Challenge includes incredibly demanding gameplay, with tiny gaps and rapid direction changes that will test players' abilities to complete the waterfall!
