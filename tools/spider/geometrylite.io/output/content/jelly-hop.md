Welcome to **Jelly Hop**! This is a fun and addictive platform game in which you lead a bouncing jelly past hard obstacles while earning rewards.

### Introduce the Gameplay of Jelly Hop

In **Jelly Hop**, you will control a continually hopping jelly creature as it hops from one stage to the next. Does that sound interesting? If your character jumps at the wrong time and becomes trapped between two platforms, the game ends. The higher you reach on the platforms, the more jelly you can collect and grow. This also adds complexity and interest to the game! 

**Controls:** Click to make your character jump.

### Unique Game Features 

After ten jumps, you can collect and refresh the skin of your main character. You will be able to create new jellies as you progress through the levels by collecting and merging smaller jellies. As you progress through the game, you will be able to unlock other characters.
