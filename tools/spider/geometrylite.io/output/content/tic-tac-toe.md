## Introduce the Tic Tac Toe game online

Tic Tac Toe is a well-known game among people of all ages. An online version of the game has been released, allowing players to relax at any time while also competing with other great players in real-time. In multiplayer mode, players can choose between playing against AI opponents or real players.

### Tips to play Tic Tac Toe 

On a 3x3 grid, you alternately place your marks (X or O). Making a line with at least three of your characters is your aim. These lines may be diagonal, vertical, or horizontal. Selecting the most strategic location, the center of the grid will improve your chances of winning. since it provides you with the greatest chances to write winning lines.
