## **Cosmic Dash - Thrilling Interplanetary Action**

Get ready for takeoff in Cosmic Dash! Launched on September 05, 2025, this platform game challenges you to race across the solar system, dodging debris, smashing through asteroids, and gathering tokens to earn huge high scores. Your mission is to push your limits as you race through space.

- Dodge and Break: You must dodge debris and break through asteroids to keep your run going. Quick reflexes are key to surviving the endless stream of space junk!
- Gather Tokens: Make sure you gather tokens along your race! These help you chase higher scores and prove you are the best pilot in the galaxy.
- Unique Moves: You have three special moves—boosts, shrinks, and jumps—to help you navigate the chaos and maximize your speed!

### **Controls**

- Press UP arrow: Leap over gaps and small debris.
- Press DOWN arrow: Squeeze through tight gaps and dodge low-hanging space junk.
- Use LEFT / RIGHT arrows: Change position to [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) obstacles or grab tokens.
- Press SPACE: Speed up and smash through asteroids for powerful progression!
