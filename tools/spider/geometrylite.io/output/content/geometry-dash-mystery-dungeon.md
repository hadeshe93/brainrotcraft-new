### **A Featured Adventure**

Mystery Dungeon is rated as a Hard level with [5 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/5-stars), so it’s a perfect challenge for players who love a good puzzle. This level is also a part of the super fun Cookie Pack, so it's a great adventure to continue after playing the other levels in the pack!
