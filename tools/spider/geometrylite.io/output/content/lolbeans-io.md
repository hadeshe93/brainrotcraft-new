In the fun multiplayer game **LOLBeans.io**, players take control of cute bean avatars and compete against other players through wacky, chaotic courses that are full of leaps, traps, and unusual obstacles.

## Introducing LOLBeans.io – The Ultimate Online Obstacle Course Adventure!

LOLBeans.io is a fantastic multiplayer .IO game that blends fast-paced obstacle course tasks with vibrant graphics. Players are placed in an arena where they must compete with numerous opponents to take the top spot, maintaining the principles of an IO game. Beautiful beans are awaiting your arrival; you may personalize your beans and engage in a variety of game modes while earning several milestones.

## How to play

Start by choosing and personalizing your cute bean character to your liking. When you submit your name and are prepared to compete with other players, the game will start. Racing through different stages with hazards like moving platforms, swinging hammers, and slick surfaces is the aim.

### Controls 

-  To move your bean about, use the WASD or arrow keys.
- To get over obstacles, hit the space bar.
- To avoid risks and traps, move quickly.
