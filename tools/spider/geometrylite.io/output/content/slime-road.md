Prepare for a colorful and addictive adventure on Slime Road! Your goal is to steer a bouncing slime ball through hard obstacle courses to the final line.

## Introduce Slime Road

Slime Road is a casual arcade game with simple one-tap controls and engaging physics-based gameplay. The main character is a soft and lively slime that is always wandering down a twisting road full of hazards. Accompany this bouncing slime ball and complete hard levels right away! Many versions provide a range of levels and obstacles, guaranteeing new and thrilling challenges!

### Gameplay of Slime Road

You will guide a blob of slime over curving routes and dodge obstacles. You should expect a deliciously tough and curiously fulfilling experience as you make your way to the finish line. Slimes cannot hit the giant circles; otherwise, the game will finish immediately!
