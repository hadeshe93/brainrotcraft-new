## **Geometry Dash Jawbreaker: A Legendary Nine Circles Classic**

Geometry Dash Jawbreaker is one of the first five Nine Circles levels ever created in Geometry Dash and remains a fan favorite for players who enjoy intense wave-based challenges. Designed by ZenthicAlpha and rated [10 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/10-stars) (Hard Demon), it builds upon the classic Nine Circles formula with a unique rhythm, creative gameplay twists, and a pulse-pounding difficulty curve.

### **Classic Wave Challenge with a Twist**

The level is famous for its extended wave sections, just like other Nine Circles levels—but Jawbreaker adds its own flavor. Expect tight corridors, sudden gravity and size changes, and multiple speed shifts that keep players on edge throughout the run.
