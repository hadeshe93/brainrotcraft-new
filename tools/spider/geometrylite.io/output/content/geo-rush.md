## **Get Ready to Rush in Geo Rush!**

Geo Rush is a super fun, fan-made platformer with a cool, simple design. In this game, you get to control a little geometric character, like a cube or a rocket, as it rushes forward automatically. Ready to start your rush? Play Geo Rush today and see if you have what it takes to beat the impossible!

### **Dash Through a Minimalist World!**

The controls for Geo Rush are simple, but the challenge is huge!

- Move Forward: Your character moves forward all on its own, so all you have to worry about is timing your jumps perfectly.
- Jump and Change Form: To make your character jump or change its form, just a single tap is all you need! You can either click your mouse, hit the spacebar, or use the on-screen button.
- Beat the Level: Your main goal is to complete each level from start to finish without running into anything!
