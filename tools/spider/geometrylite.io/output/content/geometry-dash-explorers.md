## **Geometry Dash Explorers – RobTop’s April Fools' Surprise**

Geometry Dash Explorers is a short, Easy-level joke made by RobTop for April Fools'. Rated just [2 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/2-stars), this level offers light-hearted fun with a surprising twist that’ll make even experienced players grin. At first glance, it looks like a normal [Easy](https://geometrylite.io/tag/easy) level—but when you reach the teleportation orb, the infamous second spider part from Geometry Dash Dash suddenly appears out of nowhere! And not just once—you’ll need to pass it three times in a row to finish the level.

### **How to play**

- Tap / Click / Press Space: Makes your cube jump or perform actions based on the form (cube, spider, etc.)
- Jump over spikes, [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) hazards, and pass through portals that change your form.
