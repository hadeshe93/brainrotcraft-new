Escape Road 2 is a new version of the driving game that promises to bring more excitement than ever in the chase between the police and the bank robber.

## Introduce the New Gameplay of Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 brings back the [Escape Road series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/escape-road-series) with a fresh appearance and numerous gameplay changes. The scenario remains unchanged in this edition, as you play as a bank robber fleeing the police. The chase will be considerably more fascinating in this version since you will be able to swap between automobiles on the road with only one click. That instance, if the automobile is stopped by the police, you can find a method to escape and get into another car to continue your journey.

### Controls

- To drive your car, use the arrow keys/ASD keys.
- Press the spacebar to enter/exit the car.
