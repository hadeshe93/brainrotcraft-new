**Moto Obby** is an action-packed racing game in which you play as a courageous rider attempting to overcome difficult parkour tracks on your super awesome motorcycle.

## Introducing the motorcycle racing game - Moto Obby!

If you're looking for an entertaining racing game, Moto Obby is an excellent alternative! Moto Obby's main character is the well-known [Obby](https://geometrylite.io/obby), and the game promises to take you on hard races throughout various terrains. It's time to put your abilities and reflexes to the test by navigating steep slopes while maintaining balance and speed. Don't miss out on new stages because each one has fresh challenges!

### Controls

- To move your character, use the up and down keys, or «WS». 
- To make your character spin, press the left and right arrow keys or «AD».
