## **Geometry Dash WANNACRY – A Medium Demon Challenge**

Geometry Dash WANNACRY is a [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon), 10-star level created by kira9999. This impressive fan-made creation quickly earned recognition by securing 4th Place in the NCS Gauntlet Contest. Packed with intricate design, fluid transitions, and a dark aesthetic, WANNACRY challenges players with both [skill](https://geometrylite.io/tag/skill) and patience. It is a level that pushes Geometry Dash enthusiasts to the edge of their abilities.

### **Level Design Details**

- Difficulty: Medium Demon
- Song: Annabelle’s Tea Party by NIVIRO
- Objects Count: 69,999
- Length: 2 minutes and 11 seconds

### **Gameplay Highlights**

WANNACRY begins with a unique 3D cube section where players move in reverse, setting an unusual tone from the start. Standout moments include the massive swing tunnels with giant monitors that flip directions, the mini ball part where a hacker breaks free from shattered glass, and the intense computer-smashing transition. The climax features high-speed sequences, blinding flashes, and a terrifying monster chase, all culminating in an ironic ending as the player falls into a trash can.
