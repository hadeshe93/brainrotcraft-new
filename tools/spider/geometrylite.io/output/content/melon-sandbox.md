**Melon Sandbox** is a physics-based [sandbox](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sandbox) game that allows you to interact with various objects in a dynamic, open-world environment.

## Experience A Fun and Creative Playground in Melon Sandbox Game

Melon Sandbox is an excellent game that allows you to express your imagination and creativity by building and developing the universe according to your preferences. This is a sandbox game with unique gameplay that is popular all around the world. Melon Sandbox, without the fast-paced action, provides players with a new and exciting experience, including unforeseen events when they take specific actions in the game. 

### How to play

Simply interact with the game's items and wait to see what occurs. Let's start by exploring the map and getting acquainted with the physics-driven world. As you go through the game, you will unlock additional maps and items.
