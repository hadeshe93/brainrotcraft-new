**Crazy Roll 3D** has a simple yet addicting gameplay loop in which you control a bright [ball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ball) while negotiating a succession of deadly tracks hung in the sky.

## Dive into the thrilling world of Crazy Roll 3D! 

Prepare for Crazy Roll 3D, which features perilous obstacles like rotating platforms, swinging hammers, and tiny routes that require precision and lightning-fast reflexes. Your goal is to steer the ball, but understanding momentum and timing is critical. Go as far as you can before falling into the abyss to earn a high score and make the leaderboard!

### Controls

- To steer, press the left and right arrow keys or the AD key.
- To use power-ups, press 1, 2, or 3 keys.

Crazy Roll 3D's vivid colors, dynamic soundtrack, and addictive gameplay provide an immersive experience that will keep you coming back for more. Have fun!
