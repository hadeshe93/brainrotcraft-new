**Geometry Dash Base After Base** is the fifth level in both Geometry Dash and [Geometry Dash Lite](https://geometrylite.io/), and it marks the beginning of the real action. This level marks the beginning of the Hard difficulty, increases the challenge with trickier jumps, faster rhythms, and tighter timing.

## **Introduce Geometry Dash Base After Base**

Geometry Dash Base After Base was released as part of the core Geometry Dash Official levels. The level, designed with dynamic music and unusual gameplay patterns, requires more time and reflexes than previous stages. This is a fundamental level that every Geometry Dash player has undoubtedly attempted at least once!

- Difficulty: [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard)
- Stars: [5 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/5-stars)
- Length: 86 seconds
- Minimum Jumps: 103
- Music by: DJVI

### **Coin Locations**

- 1st Coin – At 22%: Found near a gravity portal, hidden off the main path.
- 2nd Coin – At 47%: Requires [jumping](https://geometrylite.io/tag/jumping) off a tricky edge into a raised platform.
- 3rd Coin – At 67%: Found after a series of elevated jumps—watch your timing!
