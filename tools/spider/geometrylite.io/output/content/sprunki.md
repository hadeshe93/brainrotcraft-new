**Sprunki** is the first installment in the music title [Sprunki games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sprunki-games) series, which features cartoon characters capable of producing beautiful music with ease. 

## What’s Sprunki?

Sprunki is a creative music game that immerses players in a fascinating music world where they may build their music by combining beats, melodies, voices, and sound effects, all while employing a unique set of animated characters. The game's primary characters are animated, with various vocal characteristics and pitches. Your objective is to change the sound of each character to make a complete and amazing piece of music.

## How to play

To begin playing this game, simply select a character from the lineup at the bottom of the screen and drag them to the top to hear their distinct sound blended into your composition. Do the same with the other characters in the game to create your unique song.
