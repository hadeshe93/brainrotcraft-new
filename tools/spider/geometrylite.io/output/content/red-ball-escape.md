## Introduce Red Ball Escape 

Platform game fans should not miss **Red Ball Escape**, a straightforward puzzle platformer game with 100 difficult levels. The basic gameplay of a standard platform game is still present in the game: Controlling the [ball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ball) to the end of the journey without running into any platforms or obstacles is your job. Let's test your abilities in Red Ball Escape right now with progressively harder levels!

### How to controls

The red ball will automatically move forward, your task is to control the ball to jump at the right time to avoid hitting the platforms in front.
