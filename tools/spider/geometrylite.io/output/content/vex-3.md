## About Vex 3

The third installment in Alan T.'s [Vex series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/vex-series) is called Vex 3. The difficult platformer games in this series featuring the adventures of the stick figure Vex are well-known. You will go through several finely constructed stages in this game that are chock-full of varied traps, obstacles, and puzzles. The objective remains the same as in previous sections: get the vex to the escape door after the game level via navigating perilous obstacles.

### Key Features

- Challenging and addictive gameplay: This version has more perilous obstacles and stages to overcome. 
- Diverse Levels: There are ten basic levels and nine extra challenge acts in this game. Each challenge act has its themes, obstacles, and challenges, which makes the gameplay fascinating and new.
- Simple and smooth controls: Simple controls that make it possible to move fluidly while sprinting, leaping, climbing, and sliding.

## How to play

Now let's get this game started and sprint, jump, climb walls, slide under barricades, and other dangerous obstacles out of the way. You can explore a variety of environments in the game as you earn new levels, each with its own unique aesthetic and thematic components. Remember to gather the jewels strewn all over the levels to boost your bonus points.

**Controls**

Use the arrow or WASD keys to move in this game.
