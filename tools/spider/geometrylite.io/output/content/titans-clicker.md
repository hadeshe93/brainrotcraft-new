**Titans Clicker** features new monster clickers and legendary titans, allowing you to combat adversaries and safeguard the kingdom by continuously clicking to attack! 

## Introduce Titans Clicker

Welcome to Titans Clicker! This is a new clicker game that has just been added to our library. This is not a simple clicker with simple clicking gameplay; instead, you will engage in action and strategy as you fight a series of opponents to defend your kingdom against a monster onslaught. Click hard to acquire new weapons and quickly boost your combat ability. It's time to grab your sword and form a team of heroes to combat the mighty giants who rule over countless titans and monsters. 

### Tips to quickly increase strength

To raise your power in Titans Clicker, collect as many heroes' abilities and weapons as possible. You will get bonuses with each battle; use them intelligently to choose helpful upgrades.
