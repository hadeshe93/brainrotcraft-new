Geometry Dash Tidal Wave is a unique summer-themed level with [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) wave gameplay similar to [Nine Circles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/nine-circles). It is also notorious for being much more difficult than it appears due to its open design, abundant decorating, and three-minute duration.

## **Ride the Rhythm: Diving Into the World of Geometry Dash Tidal Wave**

Geometry Dash Tidal Wave is an [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) level designed by OniLink and validated by Zoink. It was launched on September 9, 2023. The level, known for its summer and beach-themed visuals, contains fast-paced wave action inspired by the "Nine Circles" approach. Despite its open design, the level's extensive décor and three-minute length add to its high difficulty.

### **Features of Tidal Wave**

- Extreme Difficulty: Tidal Wave, regarded as one of the most difficult Geometry Dash's [user levels](https://geometrylite.io/tag/user-levels), tests even the most experienced players with its intricate layout and quick-paced action.
- Amazing Graphics: Top-notch images that improve gameplay and draw players into the distinct style of the level.
- Engaging Music: Dion Timmer's "Shiawase (VIP)" soundtrack heightens the level's ferocity and blends in with the gameplay to produce a seamless whole.
