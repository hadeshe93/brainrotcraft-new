**Stickman Hook** is a popular action game known for its physics-based swinging motion and appealing stickman graphics.

## Stickman Hook: An Addicting Swinging Adventure

Stickman Hook promises to deliver hard levels in a swinging adventure with a stickman character. This game was created by the French game studio Madbox and quickly gained popularity this year. The gameplay and controls are simple, but completing all of the levels is difficult. Learn how to play Stickman Hook now! 

### How to play

Your objective is to direct a stickman who utilizes a [rope](https://geometrylite.io/tag/rope) to latch onto hooks dispersed across the landscape. You must hone your abilities by swinging from one hook to another and navigating obstacles. Remember to time your motions properly, releasing the hook at the exact right moment to avoid obstructions. Reach the finish of each level to get bonus points and access more interesting levels.
