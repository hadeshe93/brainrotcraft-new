## About the Gameplay of Geometry Dash: Battle of the Memes 2!

Geometry Dash: Battle of the Memes 2! is a new fan-made version of the rhythm-based game Geometry Dash. This level combines hard-wave gameplay with an intriguing meme world! Battle of the Memes 2! offers even more humor, frustration, and satisfying successes on the path to mastering rhythm. Many upgrades have been introduced to this part, providing players with an excellent experience. Don't miss out!

### New Updates in 2nd version

- Six more levels present a fresh challenge.
- Six distinct noises were used to boss six memes.
- 6 distinct impacts in each stage.
