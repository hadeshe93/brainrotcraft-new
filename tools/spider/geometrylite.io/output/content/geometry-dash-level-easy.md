Geometry Dash Level Easy is a [Normal](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal) 3-star level created, verified, and published by Cody. As the very first level in the Beginner Pack, it has become one of the most iconic community creations in the game.

## **Overview of Geometry Dash Level Easy**

Released as part of the [Beginner Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/beginner-pack), Geometry Dash Level Easy was designed to give new players a smooth introduction while still keeping the gameplay exciting. Over time, it made history by becoming the first user level to surpass 40 million downloads, showing just how much the community embraced it. It’s also ranked as the fifth most-liked user level in Geometry Dash, cementing its spot as a fan favorite.

### **Why You Should Play Level Easy**

Level Easy isn’t just another user level—it’s a piece of Geometry Dash history. Its accessible design makes it fun for newcomers, while its place in the Beginner Pack and community records give it legacy status. Whether you’re collecting stars or revisiting classics, it’s a must-play.
