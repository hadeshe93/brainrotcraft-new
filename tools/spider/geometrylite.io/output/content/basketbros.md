**BasketBros** takes you to a fun and exciting online basketball game! It's time to see how skilled you are by taking the virtual court and playing in this exciting game.

## About BasketBros

BasketBros is a [multiplayer](https://geometrylite.io/tag/multiplayer) game that basketball lovers cannot miss. Based on the mechanics of traditional basketball, BasketBros has many gameplay innovations, giving players a great entertainment experience. You can choose between game modes: 1v1 or 2v2. Team mode is very suitable for those who are looking for an opportunity to challenge their teamwork and strategy in this basketball game.

### Key Features 

- A multiplayer game: You can play against random opponents or join a basketball battle with pals.
- Customize your characters: Personalize your characters by selecting from a wide range of eccentric characters. You can alter the character's appearance, demeanor, and skills to suit your tastes.
- Easy Controls and Basic Gameplay: This game's controls are so basic that even new players can pick it up quickly.
- Unlock new arenas: As you advance in the game, you can unlock various basketball courts, each with a unique layout and set of obstacles that help to keep the action exciting and new.

## How to play

**In single-player mode**

- You may control your baller using the arrow keys or WASD. 
- Press the up arrow to jump, then jump one more to shoot.
- If you are on defense, hit Enter or Space to launch an attack.

**In 2-Player mode**

- Player 1: To move, press WASD; to attack, press G.
- Player 2: To move, use the arrow keys; to attack, press L.
