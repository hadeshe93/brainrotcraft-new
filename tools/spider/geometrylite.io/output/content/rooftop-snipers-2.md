## About Rooftop Snipers 2

Rooftop Snipers 2 is the sequel to the popular pixel-style shooting game with the same name. The gameplay is continuous throughout this player's portions in that he participates in gunfights to knock down his opponents as they fall off the roof. This version includes new characters such as the Joker, Mr. T, and John Wayne as opponents that you must defeat. Let's start by selecting a 1- or 2-player option and go to the roof right away!

### Controls

- use the mouse to move and pass the ball!
- To leap, use the W button!
- Shoot by releasing the E button!
