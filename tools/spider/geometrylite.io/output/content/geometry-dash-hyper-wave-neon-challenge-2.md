## Introduce Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge 2

[Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-hyper-wave-neon-challenge) returns with a second version with more difficult levels and a characteristic neon style. Geometry Dash Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge 2 is based on the popular arcade game Geometry Dash, which many players enjoy for its hard gameplay and strong waves. Your job is to overcome obstacles and securely get the cube to the finish line. Enjoy the wonderful music in Hyper Wave: Neon Challenge 2 and take on this challenge right now!

### How to play

Immerse yourself in the brilliant neon universe, listen to the rhythm, and execute precise jumps to safely carry the cube to the finish line.
