## Geometry Dash Rester: Immerse yourself in a challenging world of music

Geometry Dash Rester is a popular user-level game made by two outstanding game developers, YunHaSeu and Cenos. Unlike earlier levels, the music in Geometry Dash Rester is soft and peaceful. In addition, the photos utilized in this level have a harmonizing style with low contrast. However, Geometry Dash Rester's gameplay is far from boring. On the contrary, this is a more difficult level that many well-known players in the community greatly enjoy!
