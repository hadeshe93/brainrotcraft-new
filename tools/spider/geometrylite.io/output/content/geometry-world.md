## **About Geometry World**

Get ready to immerse yourself in the vibrant melodies and tricky matrices in Geometry World. You will navigate a small cube to avoid colliding with all the barriers on the map and reach the finish line safely. This game requires gamers to have quick reflexes and accurate timing. One small mistake can cause the puzzle to return to the starting line. Each level will have different ways of designing the matrix. However, familiar obstacles and gravity gates with changes in the entity's shape will still appear.

### Explore the challenge maps

Geometry World offers players unbelievable challenges with maps full of dangerous obstacles. How do accurately time your cube's jump? Concentrate as much as you can to keep up with the formidable pace in each round. You won't know what situations will happen on the journey if you don't try. Regular practice is extremely necessary, especially for new players. Get ready for the most spectacular adventures and impressive achievements.

## **The signature control**

No longer unfamiliar to familiar players of the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games), this version of the game also has similar gameplay and controls. You can maneuver your cube using the left-click or the spacebar. Although the command keys are very simple to get used to, they require the most accurate reflexes. This game is not for players who give up easily!
