## Stack Rush - Spin, Stack, and Shine

In Stack Rush, blocks of many shapes fall from above, and your job is to turn the platform left or right to fit each piece just right. Multiple perfect drops will clear layers and earn points, but mistakes will build the [tower](https://geometrylite.io/tag/tower) higher and make things tougher. Kids and new players will love its simple arrow-key controls, while puzzle fans enjoy the satisfying challenge. As you progress, you can unlock 17 stylish planets, each bringing a fresh look to your stacking adventure.

### A Whimsical Puzzle World

This game belongs to the 3D puzzle genre and is designed to be friendly, colorful, and [relaxing](https://geometrylite.io/tag/relaxing). Though the developer information isn’t clearly listed, Stack Rush is built like a modern web puzzle release that feels fresh and fun for young players.

### Tips to Become a Block Master

- Rotate early so you're ready when the [block](https://geometrylite.io/tag/block) lands
- Keep your tower balanced and avoid leaving open gaps
- Focus on clearing full layers instead of rushing placement
- Practice smooth timing — calm moves win!
