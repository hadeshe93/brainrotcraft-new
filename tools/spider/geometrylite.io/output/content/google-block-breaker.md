**Google Block Breaker** is a [classic](https://geometrylite.io/tag/classic) arcade game in which you use a paddle to deflect a bouncing ball and shatter a wall of blocks above.

## Introduce Google Block Breaker – A Classic Arcade Challenge 

Google Block Breaker, often known as "Breakout" clones, is strongly connected to the early days of arcade and home video games. This game type had a straightforward premise: the player used a paddle at the bottom of the screen to deflect a bouncing ball and destroy bricks at the top. Google Block Breaker continued to improve as developers added new features, graphics, and gaming mechanisms. This game genre's lasting popularity reflects the ageless appeal of its simple yet addictive gameplay.

### Key Features

- Classic Arcade Action: Google Block Breaker offers a nostalgic experience similar to classic arcade games.
- Multiple Levels and Challenges: Increasing the difficulty using various block formations. 
- Variable Block Patterns: Different levels include distinct block layouts, keeping the gameplay interesting.

## How to play

- Controls the paddle, which is a horizontal bar at the bottom of the screen.
- A ball is fired from the paddle, or it may begin to bounce immediately. 
- Move the paddle horizontally to catch the bouncing ball. The goal is to keep the ball from dropping over the bottom of the screen.
