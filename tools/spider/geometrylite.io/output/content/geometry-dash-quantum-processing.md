Welcome to **Geometry Dash Quantum Processing**! Difficult challenges await players in this [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon), which they must overcome using their rhythm jumping abilities.

## Introduce Geometry Dash Quantum Processing

Geometry Dash Quantum Processing is a collaboration of Riot, Hinds, Zobros, and Sil3nce. These are all talented and famous game creators in the Geometry Dash community. Although it is just a remake of the main level Blast Processing, Quantum Processing has become quite famous for its many upgrades in design and gameplay, making it a highly difficult level rated 10 stars. One of the most difficult parts you need to overcome to conquer this level is the waves designed in tight spaces that require extremely precise movements.

### Controls Guide

To make your character jump and get past things in the way, tap the screen or press the space bar.
