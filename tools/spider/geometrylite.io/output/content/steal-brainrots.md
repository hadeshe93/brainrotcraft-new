Instead of traditional grinding, you’ll steal, recruit, and manage “Italian Brainrots” to grow your chaotic empire. ### **How to Play**

Begin by grabbing your first Brainrot, exploring the map, and expanding your hideout. As you collect more characters, invest in defense systems so no one steals your stash. To grow faster, sneak into other zones, steal valuable Brainrots, and escape before security catches you.
