**Escape Road Winter** is a driving game set in a snowy winter environment in which you must demonstrate your superior driving abilities to avoid a police pursuit.

## Play Escape Road Winter: Enjoy Thrilling Adventure In Winter!

Prepare yourself for an amazing trip in Escape Road Winter, where everything gets much more difficult as you drive down perilous snowy roads. Even the most experienced racers will find this a true test of their abilities. Begin the game and complete this challenge right now! 

### How to play

You are placed in the shoes of a gang of bank robbers on the run. Any error on the way could result in your automobile being stopped and the cops arriving right away. Your mission is to drive through a snowy winter terrain, overcoming obstacles and traveling as far as possible!
