## Introduce Worm Hunt - Snake Game iO Zone

Worm Hunt - Snake Game iO Zone is an amazing multiplayer snake game created by Wild Spike that offers a new twist on the classic snake genre. This game plays similarly to the original [Slither.io](https://geometrylite.io/slitherio-eat-them-all) game but with a more colorful layout! You can control your snake with the mouse, and it will move forward automatically. There will be food and currency on the board that you may collect to help your snake grow. Killing other snakes allows you to get their points and eventually grow to be the largest of them all.

### Key Features 

- Customizable Worms - Unlock skins and personalize your worm for a distinctive appearance. 
- Real-Time Multiplayer Battles - Compete with players from all around the world in a dynamic, fast-paced snake war. 
- Power-Ups and Boosts - Use speed boosts and unique powers to get an advantage over your opponents.
