In **Candy Clicker**, try to collect as many candies as possible by clicking on the sweets on the screen. Get good scores, and you can automate the candy creation process by unlocking new improvements!

## Introduce New Clicker Game: Candy Clicker

Candy Clicker is an excellent choice for fans of [idle games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/idle)! A beautiful world of delectable candy awaits you to explore. This is a game that blends the excitement of gathering candy with excellent progression mechanics that you should not pass up. All you have to do is click hard and choose your upgrades carefully. 

### Tips to get high scores

- Maximize click power: Trying to click as rapidly and consistently as possible is the most basic strategy to quickly earn more sweets and enhance your score.
- Upgrade regularly: Choosing to upgrade smartly will allow you to boost your production rate, and the process can be automated.
