## Introduce Speed Master

In the vehicle racing game Speed Master, you are transported to a neon racetrack with plenty of rivals. The player's goal is to finish first in addition to avoiding obstacles that may occur on the track. The players with the best driving skills are being challenged to conquer an endless track where the thrill of speed and the noise of the engine create a heart-pounding atmosphere. Do you think you can get past every obstacle in this game? Let's begin to accelerate so that your CPU rivals fall behind! 

## How to play

As you drive along this endless road, you must not only control your speed and get past obstacles but also outmaneuver your rivals strategically. Remember the money that is strewn along the path; you can use it to modify your vehicle and purchase amazing new vehicles that will add interest to the race! 

### Controls

Use the mouse, the arrow keys, or "A" and "D" to steer your vehicle left and right.
