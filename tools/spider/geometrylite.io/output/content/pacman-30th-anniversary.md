**Pacman 30th Anniversary** is one of the most iconic arcade games, in which you control the yellow titular figure as he moves through the maze and eats all of the dots.   

## About Pacman 30th Anniversary

Pacman 30th Anniversary is a Google Doodle game commemorating the 30th anniversary of the arcade game's release and worldwide success. This version of the game keeps the original gameplay, with the main character navigating through a maze to hunt animals. If you enjoy [classic games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/classic) that evoke nostalgia, Pacman 30th Anniversary will not be missed! 

### Gameplay of Pacman: One of the most iconic Arcade games 

Pacman's gameplay hasn't altered much over the years, and it's known for its simplicity while remaining addictive. The player's goal is to guide the titular character, a yellow, round creature with a voracious appetite. This character will navigate around a maze, eating all of the dots while avoiding the ghosts Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde. Create a sensible approach to conquer the maze and begin right away!
