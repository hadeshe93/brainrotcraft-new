Vex Hyper Dash brings a fresh twist to the beloved Vex series. This brand-new spin-off drops you into a [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) platforming gauntlet, where one wrong move means starting over.

The core of the game is simple yet merciless: keep moving forward, leap across platforms, and dodge hazards that come at lightning [speed](https://geometrylite.io/tag/speed). The challenge ramps up as you progress, with sharper jumps and trickier traps that test your focus and reflexes. To keep your adrenaline high, the game is set to a pulsating soundtrack inspired by Geometry Dash.

### **Features worth dashing for**

- A new spin-off in the Vex universe.
- Endless [survival](https://geometrylite.io/tag/survival) platforming with escalating danger.
- Precise jumps and perfect timing as the key to victory.
- Music-driven action that keeps every run exciting.

### **Controls**

- Jump: Arrow Keys, Spacebar, or Mouse Click
- Dash: K or Shift (customizable in settings)
