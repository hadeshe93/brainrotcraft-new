## Start your endless running journey on the subway in Subway Surfs

An action endless runner game called Subway Surfs is modeled after the well-known Subway Surfers. Experience the thrill of fast-paced pursuits and urban adventure in Subway Surfs! You'll play as a young graffiti artist who has to make his way through busy metro rails to get away from the obstinate Inspector and his dog. With its well-known yet difficult gameplay, Subway Surfs promises to take you on an amazing adventure full of surprises. Will moving quickly enable you to escape the inspector's chase? Now let's get this game going and explore!

### Key Features

- **Endless Running Gameplay:** Exciting gameplay in which the player controls a boy through an endless subway system packed with hazards and trains.
- **Colorful Graphics:** Take in the eye-catching, comical graphics and exquisitely created surroundings.
- **Variety of Characters:** As the game progresses, you can use the gold you gain to unlock different characters, each of whom has a distinct style.
- **Power-ups and Boosts:** Collecting helpful power-ups like the Jetpack, Magnet, and Super Sneakers is essential to maximizing your score in Power-ups and Boosts.

## Controls

To maneuver your character, use the mouse or the arrow keys.
