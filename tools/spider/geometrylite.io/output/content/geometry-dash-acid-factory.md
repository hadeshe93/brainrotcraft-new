**Geometry Dash Acid Factory** is a [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard) level that immerses players in the spooky atmosphere of an acid factory. Begin this trip and gather all three user coins right now!

## Explore the special theme in Geometry Dash Acid Factory

As the name implies, Geometry Dash Acid Factory transports players on a unique adventure through an acid factory. The entire level is painted a gloomy gray, with bright green neon colors, pipes, and other factory-themed objects. This scene is visually stunning, creating a spooky atmosphere in a factory filled with traps and obstacles. Can you conquer them and cross the finish line safely?

### Tips to play Geometry Dash Acid Factory 

Geometry Dash Acid Factory is listed as Hard, however, the difficulty is just four stars. As a result, even experienced players can easily complete this level. The key to success is to retain concentration from the beginning of the level. After a few trials, you'll have a rough idea of the pattern of obstacle placement. Remember them and progress in your upcoming efforts.
