## **Quantum Geometry: Enter the Neon Gauntlet**

Explore the thrilling realm of Quantum Geometry, a fan-made variation on the Geometry Dash universe. This extremely tough platformer requires you to navigate a tiny cube through bright tunnels riddled with dangerous traps, portals, and gravity flips—all while jamming to an intense soundtrack. With its colourful neon sights and strong challenge, this game is ideal for experienced gamers searching for a spectacular trip.

### **Core Features**

- Hardcore challenges: [Avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) whirling saws, jump over spikes, go through portals, and adjust to gravity variations. Quick reactions are necessary.
- Simple controls: To jump, simply tap or click—but this is not as simple as it appears.
- Striking visuals: The game's neon flair jumps off the screen, making each level as thrilling to watch as to play.
