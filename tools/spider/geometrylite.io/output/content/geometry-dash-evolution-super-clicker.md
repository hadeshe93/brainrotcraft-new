## About Geometry Dash: Evolution of Super Clicker 

Geometry Dash: Evolution Super Clicker! is an excellent blend of the popular rhythm-based platform game and the peaceful gameplay of the clicker game series. The main character in this clicker is the lobotomy face from Geometry Dash; your sole objective is to click hard on the face displayed on the screen. In an idle clicker game, you can listen to catchy music while playing as the character Lobotomy Geometry Dash.
