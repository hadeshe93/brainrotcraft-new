## **Geometry Dash Dreamland - Overview of the Level**

Geometry Dash Dreamland is a [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard) 4-star level designed by Scanbrux, appearing as the second level in the Beginner Pack. While its difficulty may not be extreme, it’s often remembered as one of the trickiest in the pack thanks to its sharp timing, deceptive jumps, and clever obstacle placement. Despite being an older level, it still holds a strong reputation within the community and remains a favorite for players wanting to sharpen their skills.

### **Key Features**

- Hard difficulty ([4 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/4-stars)): More challenging than most Beginner Pack entries.
- Classic monochrome design: Minimal colors but sharp contrast to highlight obstacles clearly.
- Deceptive obstacles: Hidden traps and tricky jumps keep players alert.
