**Experience the intensity of Zrist DX! Join in futuristic levels to dodge high-tech obstacles and unleash your skills in this adrenaline-pumping adventure.**

Zrist DX welcomes you to the speed race with more thrilling experiences. You will join the journey with a cube that can jump up or slide down. The barriers that constantly appear are the challenges you need to avoid. Each different segment has new changes in map design. This endless adventure will last hundreds of levels until an unfortunate collision occurs. Except for the red boxes, all other terrain is safe for the cube to move through. However, you will not be able to manage the character's speed, so don't be negligent at any time to conquer impressive scores.

### **Explore Engaging Features**

- The design is simple, but it creates an extremely tricky adventure path.
- The rules of the game are easy to understand, as you just need to maneuver the character to avoid red blocks to continue.
- The controls are simple with clicks but not easy to master because of the fast pace.
- The main tracks have no destination, making the experience even more thrilling.
- Each turn will be a map with different arrangement structures.
- Tons of character skins and terrain designs are waiting for you to unlock.
