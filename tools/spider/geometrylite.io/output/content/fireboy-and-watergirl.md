Welcome to the first installment of the popular puzzle game series **Fireboy and Watergirl**. Your mission is to help Fireboy and Watergirl find the exit door by solving maze puzzles. 

## Begin the thrilling adventure in Fireboy and Watergirl

The thrilling adventure awaits you in Fireboy and Watergirl! It's time to accompany the two main characters through 32 levels of Forest Temple and guide them to safe doorways. A variety of traps and tough circumstances will test your problem-solving abilities. Do not forget to collect gems along the road to the finish line. It will help you raise your score. 

### Game Rules

- Fireboy should avoid locations with water or green.
- Watergirl should avoid locations with fire and green.
- Solve the problems in each chapter and guide both characters to the exit door.
