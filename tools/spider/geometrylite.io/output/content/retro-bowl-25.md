Retro Bowl 25 brings fresh energy to the beloved retro-style [football](https://geometrylite.io/tag/football) game with updated features and smoother gameplay. Take leadership of your squad and guide them to victory in this classic yet modern football adventure.

## **Retro Bowl 25: Game Overview**

- Developer: New Star Games
- Release Year: 2025
- Genre: Sports, Simulation, [Retro](https://geometrylite.io/tag/retro) Arcade

In Retro Bowl, you take on the roles of both coach and quarterback, overseeing rosters, trades, drafts, and, of course, on-field action. The game has a large fan base that enjoys its simple pick-up-and-play simplicity, strategic depth, and vintage vibe.

### **How to play**

- Use W and S to move your player!
- Use A or D to dive!
- Use your mouse to use [management](https://geometrylite.io/tag/management) menus and to pass the ball in matches!
