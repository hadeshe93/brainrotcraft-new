## **Geometry Dash Horror – A Frightening Platformer Experience**

Geometry Dash Horror is a fan-made level that takes the bright, lively world of Geometry Dash and turns it into something horrible. This version, with its crimson and black colour scheme, creepy sound effects, and tight, unpredictable traps, adds a horror element to the conventional jump-and-dodge gameplay. The goal is the same: timing your jumps to [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) spikes and clear all obstacles. However, with scarier images and more intense level design, you'll need greater focus to survive.

### **How to Play**

**Controls:**

Click/tap to leap and avoid obstacles.

**Objective:**

- The objective is to time your jumps to avoid hazards and survive till the end.
- One mistake and the game is over—so concentrate, tap fast, and react quickly!
