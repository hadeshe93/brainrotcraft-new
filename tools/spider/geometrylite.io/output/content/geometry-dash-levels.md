Welcome to **Geometry Dash Levels**! Immerse yourself in the lively melody of this platform game while guiding a square-shaped character over all obstacles.

## About Geometry Dash Levels

Geometry Dash Levels is a fan-made version of the Geometry Dash game developed by Luminex Games. This is a fast-paced rhythm-based platformer recognized for its hard and fascinating levels, which rival the original versions released by RopTop Games. This version takes you on a new journey with Geometry blocks, featuring a stunning design, exciting music, and precise gameplay mechanics. It's time to embark on a new trip, conquering challenges to accomplish all levels.

### How to play

- To jump, press the spacebar, up arrow, or left mouse button. 
- You must jump over all obstacles to reach the finish line.
