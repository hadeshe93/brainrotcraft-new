## About Geometry: Black Wave 5

**Geometry: Black Wave** returns in its fifth installment, Geometry: Black Wave 5, with a bevy of gameplay changes that promise new experiences and excellent music! This is a new exciting platform game inspired by Geometry Dash that includes Wave mode gameplay to test your response time and precision. Your objective is the same as in other [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games): manage a triangle while avoiding various obstacles. The goal is to complete the level perfectly while overcoming all difficulties.

### New features in Geometry: Black Wave 5

- Wave mode gameplay has been significantly modified, with challengingly built platforms that provide new sensations to players.
- Speed Shifts: The music's rhythm constantly changes, requiring players to time their actions accordingly.
- Gravity Variations: One of the most noticeable difficulties in Geometry: Black Wave 5 is gravity variations, which generate substantial oscillations in gameplay, making the level more appealing!
