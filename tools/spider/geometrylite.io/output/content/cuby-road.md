In the endless runner game **Cuby Road**, you must overcome obstacles by navigating a beautifully pixelated environment full of colorful cubes. 

## The gameplay of Cuby Road 

In this game, the Cuby serves as the primary character and the story takes place in an obstacle-filled jungle. It is your task to manipulate the cube to free your other pals who are imprisoned in this woodland. You'll need to use cunning maneuvers and lightning-fast reflexes to avoid the numerous traps and obstacles positioned throughout the forest to cross the finish line safely. It's important to note that the platforms will eventually crumble and turn into water, so you'll need to act quickly.

### Controls

To move this cube, press the arrow key. 

### More upgrades 

Remember to gather coins as you travel. By doing so, you can raise your score and get access to new cube characters. In the pixel world, there are a ton of adorable, vibrant characters just waiting for you to unleash.
