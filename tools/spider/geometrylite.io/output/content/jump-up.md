## Gameplay of Jump Up

In the fantastic adventure game Jump Up, you play as a character who must ascend through a colorful, vertically oriented environment chock-full of obstacles and riddles. This is an assessment of your ability to maneuver to reach the top place. Proceed with caution as there are numerous traps throughout the route that could cause you to collapse at any moment! Remember to always look ahead in this game if you want to progress. This will assist you in foreseeing problems and organizing your future steps.

### Controls

- To move your character, use WASD.
- To select a posture, use F.
