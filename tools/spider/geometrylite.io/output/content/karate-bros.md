Welcome to **Karate Bros**! This is a retro-inspired side-scrolling beat 'em up. You will be in a combat where two martial arts masters team up to punch, kick, and combo their way through waves of foes.

## Karate Bros – A Sports Game With Classic Arcade Style

In Karate Bros, players take on the role of martial artists battling across dynamic arenas packed with difficult opponents. Drawing influence from [classic](https://geometrylite.io/tag/classic) arcade fighters such as Double Dragon and Streets of Rage, the game features fast-paced, button-mashing combat, smooth animations, and beautiful pixel art visuals. Whether competing against AI or battling friends in local or online multiplayer modes, each match is a test of skill and strategy.

### Features That Pack a Punch

- 2-Player Co-Op Mode: Work together with a friend to deal twice as much damage.
- Classic Side-Scrolling Action: Battle your way through city streets, rooftops, and more.
- Power-ups & Special Moves: When the bar is full, unleash powerful strikes.
- Pixel Art Style: Retro designs with a vibrant, cartoony feel.
