Wacky Flip is an arcade-style flipping game that mixes hilarious [ragdoll](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ragdoll) physics with [skill](https://geometrylite.io/tag/skill)\-based action. Your goal is to flip your character off different platforms, spin through the air, and land cleanly on your feet (or attempt it!). The physics-based animations make each flip surprising and amusing, lending the game its wild charm. From roofs to trampolines, each jump presents a new challenge.

### **How to play**

- Hold the Left Mouse Button: Adjust your [jumping](https://geometrylite.io/tag/jumping) power and angle. The longer you hold, the stronger your launch.
- Release LMB: To launch your character into the air at the specified power and angle.
- Click Midair to begin flipping: You can start rotating your character in midair with a single click.
- Click again to lock in the landing: Timing is everything; click at the right moment to land on your feet and score big.
