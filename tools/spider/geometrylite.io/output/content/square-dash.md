**Square Dash** is a fast-paced vertical runner that was inspired by Geometry Dash. In this fan-made twist, click to change directions and avoid obstacles.

## **Square Dash – Climb, Click, and Conquer the Vertical Chaos!**

Welcome to Square Dash! In this fan-made vertical runner, you're rising skyward, dodging traps, bouncing off walls, and reacting in real time with easy clicks, rather than dashing left to right. The issue is determining when to shift course to avoid obstacles while maintaining smooth upward progress.

### **How to Play Square Dash** 

- Don’t let the simplicity fool you—Square Dash demands focus and quick reaction: 
- Click or tap anywhere on the screen to change your direction.
- Navigate between walls, avoid spikes, traps, and surprise obstacles.
