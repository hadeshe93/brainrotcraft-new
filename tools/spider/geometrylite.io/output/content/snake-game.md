## Introduce Snake Game: The Timeless Classic of Arcade Fun 

Snake Game is a classic game that has been loved by millions of people around the world since its release on mobile devices. This game became a global craze in the late 1990s and early 2000s thanks to its basic yet hard and addictive gameplay. Many alternative variants have been produced, all based on Snake Game's addictive gameplay. This version claims to give players a vintage experience with one of the most iconic arcade-style games. 

## Gameplay overview 

At the start of the game, you control a "snake" that wanders around the screen, attempting to devour apples. Your goal is to eat as many apples as possible to make the Snake grow bigger. Moving across the board with a longer body is not easy!

### Game Rules

- Eat as many apples as possible to help your snake's body grow longer.
- You must avoid crashing into the walls or the snake's body.

### Controls

Use the arrow keys to guide your snake around the screen, and eat the apple.
