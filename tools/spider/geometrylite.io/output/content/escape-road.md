It's time to test your driving talents by racing through the traffic on **Escape Road**. Imagine you've just committed a bank robbery and are immediately apprehended by the cops! This is the start of an epic car race!

## Gameplay of Escape Road

As the title of the game implies, you must drive a [car](https://geometrylite.io/tag/car) on the roads to complete the escape goal. As you flee from the police cars, you'll have to avoid oncoming hazards including trees and other cars. On your way out, don't forget to gather coins. With this money, you can add more vehicles to the game, such as a time machine, drag racer, hovercraft, rocket launcher, etc.

### Controls

- To accelerate, press up.
- To brake, apply pressure.
- To switch lanes, press left or right.

## Key Features

- Its vibrant pixelated graphics distinguish it from other [Racing & Driving games](https://geometrylite.io/racing-driving.games).
- Travel across many topographies and environments with differing degrees of difficulty.
- A varied array of cars makes for an engaging experience.

Come along and let's see how far you can get away from the cops!
