**Electron Dash** is a fast-paced, reflex-based game in which you accompany an astronaut as they overcome levels and traps in a tunnel.

## Introduce Electron Dash 

Experience a thrilling voyage through a future tunnel in space with **Electron Dash**! You will control an electron as it travels through an infinite, spinning tunnel loaded with obstacles such as laser beams, unstable tiles, and gaps. The goal is to survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups that improve performance. The game frequently uses lively, electronic music to improve the immersive experience. In Electron Dash, you must focus on accurate movements and quick reactions to advance as far as possible!

### Controls

To go through the tunnel, use WASD or arrow keys.
