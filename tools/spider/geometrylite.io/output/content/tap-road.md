Enjoy a challenging trip as you navigate a ball across obstacles on a sloping platform in **Tap Road**, a neon-filled universe.

## About Tap Road

In the thrilling 3D arcade game Tap Road, you'll test your reflexes against obstacles and your skill to control the ball as it slopes. The goal of this fast-paced adventure is to control a ball without running into any [obstacles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacles) as you travel down a twisting, constantly changing slope. One excellent option for your entertainment is the Endless Runner game, which has straightforward but captivating gameplay in a dynamic setting.

### Key Features

- Beautiful graphics: You are drawn into a world of speed by the vibrant graphics and fluid animations, which provide an immersive experience.
- Gradual difficulty: As players have to make quick maneuvers to steer left or right, the game gets harder as the slope and ball speed both steadily increase.
- More power-ups: As you advance in the game, you can gather awards and power-ups to unlock additional new features.

## How to play

To control a rolling ball, simply click, and it will accelerate forward. You can also control the ball's direction by clicking left or right to avoid obstacles.
