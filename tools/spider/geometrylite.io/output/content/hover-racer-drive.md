**Hover Racer Drive** is a terrific arcade racing game that gives players a completely different experience as they control their hovercrafts and race between dimensions at tremendous speeds.

## Introduce the Gameplay of Hover Racer Drive

Now is your chance to show that you are the best racer on the futuristic Hover Racer Drive racecourse. Hovercrafts are what you will be controlling in this endless flying game. Through a city full of structures and obstructions, these hovercrafts are traveling at amazing speeds. Controlling your spacecraft to travel as far as you can without running into any obstacles is your task. The Hovercraft will be moving quickly as soon as you decide to start this game, so stay focused right away! 

### Controls

Use your mouse to navigate your hovercraft across obstacles.
