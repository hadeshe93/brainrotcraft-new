**Only Up!** is a 3D platformer in which you must ascend a seemingly infinite, bizarre terrain, where a single misstep can send you tumbling back down. In this hard adventure, you'll put your patience and platforming skills to the test.

## Introduce the Core Gameplay of Only Up!

Only Up! is a platformer game created by SCKR Games and released on May 24, 2023. You play as Jackie, traveling upward through a succession of [obstacles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacles) without checkpoints, to reach the moon. The game's main obstacle is its merciless design—a single miscalculation can result in a considerable loss of progress, forcing you to restart. You must use exact time and smart preparation to rise, with difficulty rising as you climb higher.

### Controls

- Use WASD to control your movement.
- To change the camera view, use your mouse.
- Use the Spacebar to jump.
- Tab to Options/Pause. 

Remember that as you advance, you will face a variety of situations that will put your adaptability and problem-solving abilities to the test.
