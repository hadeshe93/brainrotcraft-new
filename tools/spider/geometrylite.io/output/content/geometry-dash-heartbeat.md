## **Introduce Geometry Dash Heartbeat**

Geometry Dash Heartbeat is not for the faint-hearted. This 10-star [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) level is a tough challenge that will put your abilities, patience, and muscle memory to the ultimate test. Created as a mega-collaboration by LmAnubis, Blaireswip, and KrmaL, this fan-favorite in the Geometry Dash 2.0 period has built a reputation for being one of the most intense levels in the game.

### **Soundtrack – Heartbeat by SnoodDood**

The dramatic and gloomy track "Heartbeat" by SnoodDood complements the level's terrible sights and unrelenting gameplay. The throbbing [rhythm](https://geometrylite.io/rhythm.games) and powerful beat will keep your adrenaline pumping as you attempt to survive wave after wave of geometric chaos.
