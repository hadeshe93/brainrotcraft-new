## Introduce Wrestle Bros

Wrestle Bros is an interesting wrestling game developed by Blue Wizard. If you enjoy fast-paced sports games, Wrestle Bros is not to be missed! You will be able to enter the virtual arena and compete against legendary opponents from the world of wrestling. Each match promises to be fascinating, but not easy; you must establish your mettle in the ring and demonstrate your wrestling abilities! 

### How to play

**Single player mode**

- To move, use the right and left arrow keys or the AD key.
- To jump, use the W or up arrow key.
- To block, use the S or down arrow key.
- Use Space, G, or L to take action.

**Two players mode**

- Player 1: Use WASD + G
- Player 2: Use Arrow keys + L
