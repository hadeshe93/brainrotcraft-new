## **67 Clicker Overview**

67 Clicker is a fresh and funny idle [clicker](https://geometrylite.io/clicker.games) game born from the viral “67” meme on TikTok and Instagram. Behind its meme-inspired surface lies a clever mix of math, planning, and resource [management](https://geometrylite.io/tag/management). Your mission? Click the glowing “67” at the center of the screen to earn points, upgrade your tools, and push your total to astronomical levels — maybe even break the world record!

### **Click, Upgrade, Repeat!**

Every click gives you more “67s,” which can be spent on strategic upgrades. The key to success is balancing short-term gains with long-term growth. Will you invest in click power, passive income, or massive production boosts? Choose wisely — the right combination of upgrades will multiply your earnings and send your score skyrocketing. Upgrades Include:

- Cursor: +1.7x click power (cost: 25 points)
- Auto: +1.7 points/sec (cost: 100 points)
- Farm: +10.7 points/sec (cost: 500 points)
- Mine: +100.7 points/sec (high cost, massive reward)
