## Doodle Cricket: A Mini Game with Big Fun 

Doodle Cricket is a fun, browser-based cricket game designed by Google in honor of the 2017 ICC Champions Trophy. This mini-game offers charming insect avatars, with crickets facing off against a squad of snails in a simplified arcade-style version of cricket. It's simple to play, entertaining for all ages, and ideal for brief gaming breaks. If you're looking for a fun game that's both cute and addictive, Doodle Cricket is a great choice. 

### How to play

- Tap or click at the appropriate time to strike the ball.
- Score runs by getting the ball past the fielders.
- Avoid getting out, as the game ends when you're bowled or caught!
