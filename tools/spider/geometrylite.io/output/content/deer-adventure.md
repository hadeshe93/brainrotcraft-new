## **Deer Adventure – When Serenity Meets Absolute Chaos**

Deer Adventure is no ordinary [simulation](https://geometrylite.io/simulation.games) game. At first glance, it looks like a peaceful escape where you can wander around town, interact with animals, and enjoy a carefree day. But hidden beneath the calm surface is a wildly chaotic option—unleash destruction on everything in sight and turn the town upside down.

### **Gameplay**

Your deer can either live a quiet life of frolic or go on a rampage, destroying cars, houses, and anything that dares stand in your way. But beware—this town has defenders. You’ll soon find yourself battling against bizarre yet formidable foes like kung fu sheep, bears that morph into police cars, and rabbits with ears strong enough to knock you flat.

### **Controls**

- Use WASD: To Move
- Use Arrow keys: To Interact with humans
- Use Mouse: To Control the camera
- Use LMB: To Attack
- Use Spacebar: To Jump
- Use Shift: To Dash
- Use Q or RMB: Stretch your neck (yes, really)
- Use F: Trigger a special action
