## **Space is Key Christmas: Dashing Through the Spikes**

Welcome to Space Is Key Christmas! Developed as a seasonal spin-off of the original Space is Key, this Christmas edition retains the essential one-button gameplay—tap the spacebar to make your square jump at the proper time. All you have to do is avoid [obstacles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacles) and make it through each level, which includes spikes, moving blocks, and tough timed traps. Remember, tapping the spacebar at the right time is the key to winning this game!

### **Special Features**

- New Christmas visuals include snow, holiday colors, and festive shimmer.
- A cheerful festive music will keep you in the Christmas spirit.
- New challenging stages that become more difficult as you progress.
