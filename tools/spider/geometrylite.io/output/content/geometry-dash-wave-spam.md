## **Geometry Dash Wave Spam**

Geometry Dash Wave Spam is a new fan-made version of the Geometry Dash video game that adds the Wave mode, another wave challenge you can't miss! This game describes a gaming style in which the Wave mode is used in short, quick bursts, usually in extremely narrow passages. The player must click or tap frequently and methodically to [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) colliding with spikes or blocks. This will be a true test of your abilities to handle each dynamic wave.
