In the fantastic multiplayer shooter game **Pixel Shooter**, you enter a [Pixel](https://geometrylite.io/tag/pixel) universe where intense gunfights are taking place.

## Introduce the Pixel Shooter game

Pixel Shooter is a must-play for any shooter fan! The game claims to lead you to a high-stakes encounter with numerous frightening opponents. Making accurate shots is your goal and mission. In this violent arena, you must fight valiantly to become the sole survivor. Don't forget to improve your arsenal to gain strength! 

### How to play

- To move around, use WASD.
- Use R to reload.
- Use space to throw a grenade.
- Use the left-click to shoot.
