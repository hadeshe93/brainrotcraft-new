## Egg Race: A physics-based one that requires balance!

Egg Race is an entertaining race featuring a unique character that you probably never imagined! The characters accompanying you on the race are eggs! A difficult task awaits you: manage your [car](https://geometrylite.io/tag/car) across varied terrains and obstacles without dropping the egg on the car. You should plan a fair approach for safely navigating tough terrain! How far can you get in this game? Start the game right now and see what happens! 

### Tips to play Egg Race

In this game, speed is not the key concern due to the fragile nature of the eggs. To keep the egg stable, drive gradually and prevent rapid acceleration or braking. Additionally, pay attention to approaching terrain changes and adjust your speed accordingly.
