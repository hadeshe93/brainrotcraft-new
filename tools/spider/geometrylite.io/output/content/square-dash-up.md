## **Square Dash Up – Jump Higher, Go Faster!**

Get ready for a vertical challenge that’s simple to play but hard to put down! Square Dash Up is a fast-paced vertical arcade game where you guide a bouncy square through sky-high platforms, collecting coins and unlocking a colorful cast of cube-shaped characters. With just two buttons, you're in full control of the chaos—just don’t fall!

### **How to Play Square Dash Up**

- Press the A key or tap the Jump button to leap onto the next platform.
- Press the S key or Go Up button to dash upward through tricky gaps.
- Unlock all 18 characters, from Eye Square to Soldier Square and Santa Square!
