## **Introducing the Thrilling World of Speed Stars!**

**Speed Stars** captures the enthusiasm of Olympic sprinting events in a simple yet demanding system. The game became popular due to its simple controls, addictive gameplay, and competitive leaderboard system. Your reflexes and rhythm will be put to the test in this thrilling Olympic-style [running](https://geometrylite.io/tag/running) game. Embrace your inner athlete and race through 100-meter dashes, aiming to smash global records and better your own best.

### **How to play**

Use the arrow keys to control your athlete.

### **Why Should You Play Speed Stars?**

Speed Stars is ideal for gamers who enjoy competitive sports games and seek a [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) challenge that relies on ability and timing. It captures the excitement and exhilaration of true sprinting competitions, when every fraction of a second is crucial. There are no complicated rules or systems – simply pure racing, where your instincts determine success.
