## About Geometry Dash Prismarine

Geometry Dash Prismarine promises to be an attractive and challenging level. This is the fourth level of [Crystal Gauntlet](https://geometrylite.io/tag/crystal-gauntlet), and it features a stunning design as well as interesting gameplay that is coordinated with music. You'll start with a cube, and you'll have to squeeze rings while avoiding hazards like spikes. Remember that all obstacles are synchronized to the music, so let the beat lead you! 

### Location of the Coins

- The first coin is at 33%.
- The second coin is at 53%.
- The third coin is at 96%.
