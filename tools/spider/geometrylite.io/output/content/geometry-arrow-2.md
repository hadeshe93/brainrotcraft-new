Prepare for a thrilling journey in **Geometry Arrow 2**, the fast-paced sequel to the original rhythm arcade smash. With six dynamic levels, beat-synced obstacles, and thrilling personalization choices, it's time to challenge your timing and reflexes to the limit.

## **Introduce Geometry Arrow 2** 

Geometry Arrow 2 is the latest version of the well-known rhythm arcade game [Geometry Arrow](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-arrow). Let's examine the improved version, which features more precise timing and explosive beats. Players progress through the Arrow and Wheel sections, dodging obstacles in rhythm with the music. The game's combination of rhythm, reflex, and customization keeps each run fresh and distinct.

### **How to Play**

Your goal is to go to the gateway while avoiding moving obstacles that dance to the music. Each level combines Arrow and Wheel portions, keeping the action interesting and hard.

**Controls:**

- PC: Press SPACEBAR or LEFT MOUSE BUTTON to move/jump. 
- Press ESC to exit the level.
