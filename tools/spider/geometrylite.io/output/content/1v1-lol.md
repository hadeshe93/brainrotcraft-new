In the competitive third-person shooter **1v1 lol**, you must advance across the battlefield, take out opponents, and survive to be the last one standing. 

## Introduce 1v1 lol

Being the last shooter standing in the arena is your goal in 1v1 lol, the ultimate test of your building and shooting prowess! Intense 1v1 duels against people from all over the world take place in this fighting game. Though exceedingly challenging, the task presents an excellent chance for you to hone your skills.

### Game Modes

First, select the game mode that best suits your needs: 

- Practice mode: Practice mode is a fantastic tool for getting familiar with the controls and honing your abilities.
- 1v1 mode: A player faces another person one-on-one in 1v1 mode.
- BOX mode: A two-player team battle in which two teams face off against one another.

## How to play 

You will be able to explore an arena stocked with tools, supplies, and other things. Here, you can make weapons, construct buildings, and cure yourself with these resources.

### Controls

- To move, press and hold the WASD or arrow keys.
- The left mouse button is used to shoot. 
- The right mouse button is used to build.
