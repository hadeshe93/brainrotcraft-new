## **Introduce Geometry Dash Flappy Hexagon**

Geometry Dash Flappy Hexagon is a 4-star Hard level with challenging and interesting gameplay that you should not miss. This game is inspired by Darnoc's Geometry Dash Flappy Bird but pushes it to the next level. As the final level in the [Sapphire Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sapphire-pack), it provides a gratifying conclusion for those hoping to flap their way to victory.

### **Gameplay**

Your aim, as with all of the levels in the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) collection, is to conquer obstacles in a rhythmic manner and safely cross the finish line. The entire level is designed around the UFO game mode, which means that every tap of the screen causes your character to "flap" upward, just like in Flappy Bird. However, the Geometry Dash twist includes pulsing lights, dynamic music, and precise obstacle timing, making it a test of rhythm and focus.
