## **Geometry Dash 2D: Platformer – A New Fan-Made Adventure**

Geometry Dash 2D: Platformer brings a fresh perspective to the iconic rhythm-based series. Instead of endless runner mechanics, this fan-made version takes you through three distinct worlds, each filled with challenging levels. Whether you’re leaping over molten platforms, diving into deep seas, or surviving toxic terrains, the game keeps you hooked with unpredictable obstacles and smooth platforming action.

### **Explore Three Unique Worlds**

The game is divided into three themed worlds:

- Lava World – Intense fiery stages where timing is key to [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) scorching traps.
- Ocean World – Flow through watery environments with slippery surfaces and hidden challenges.
- Toxic World – A dangerous land filled with green hazards and tricky jumps that push your skills to the edge.

Each world contains 4 levels, giving players a total of 12 stages to conquer.

### **How to Play Geometry Dash 2D: Platformer**

- Jump: Press the designated key or tap to leap over spikes and traps.
- Avoid Obstacles: Every level has unique hazards that demand quick reflexes.
