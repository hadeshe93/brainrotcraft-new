## **Overview of Geometry Dash Nock Em** 

Jump into Geometry Dash Nock Em! Prepare for an exciting ride where you'll discover awesome new ways to jump, fly, and dash through a world of blocks and spikes! Start your journey to conquer each sub-level in [Geometry Dash Subzero](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-subzero) right now! 

Nock Em introduces several gameplay mechanics to SubZero players:

- Wave and Spider Modes – Both make their debut in this level, adding variety and demanding more from your reflexes.
- Green Jump Rings – Launch your character with perfect timing to avoid spikes and reach upper routes.
- Size Portals – Change your character’s size mid-game to fit through tight spaces or jump higher.
- Random Trigger – A fun twist in the form of randomized "Rub" text decorations that shift each time you play.

### **How to Play**

- Depending on your current mode, you can jump, fly, or alter gravity by pressing the spacebar or left-clicking.
- Learn new dynamics such as wave mode (touch to rise, release to fall) and spider mode (teleport to ceiling or floor).
