**Pixel Speedrun** is a minimalist platformer that requires players to guide a pixel through increasingly challenging stages to locate a safe entrance.

## Introduce Pixel Speedrun

Pixel Speedrun, created by Vendara and released on May 21, 2021, has gained popularity for its simple mechanics and interesting gameplay. In this game, your character navigates a complex maze to complete each level swiftly. [Avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) perils like red-hot lava and perilous platforms.

### Tips to play

- Memorise level layouts: In Pixel Speedrun, you need to become familiar with the level layouts and obstacle patterns to anticipate problems and optimize your approach.
- Practice precision: Practice mastering the game's controls and performing precise jumps, dashes, and wall jumps.
