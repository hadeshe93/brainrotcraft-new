**Geometry Dash Nine Circles** is one of the most popular user-levels in the rhythm-based platform game Geometry Dash. This well-known game not only features difficult gameplay but also has a stunning design that transports you to a fantastic universe of geometry blocks!

## About Geometry Dash Nine Circles

Geometry Dash Nine Circles is a [Hard Demon](https://geometrylite.io/hard-demon.games) level created, verified, and published by Zobros. This game has become one of Zobros' most successful and well-known levels due to its gameplay, design, effects, and difficulty. This game preserves the platform gameplay, in which you must control the cube to safely navigate a series of obstacles to reach the finish line. However, this level has a more elaborate design, confusing effects, and difficult gameplay, making it a challenge even for experienced gamers!

### More Interesting Facts 

- Nine Circles is the second most popular map pack and gauntlet level. 
- This level was ranked third on GDToday's list of the 100 Greatest Levels of All Time.
- This level design influenced many other users to create levels with similar gameplay, known as [Nine Circles levels](https://geometrylite.io/tag/nine-circles).
