## Introduce Curve Rush

Curve Rush is a platform game that takes players on an infinite adventure through huge desert landscapes. Your goal is to handle a fastÂ [ball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ball) with skill and accuracy to avoid obstacles and changes in the track and land smoothly. Hitting any barriers will force the ball to break, bringing your adventure to an abrupt halt! Begin the race now, strive to outperform other players, and watch your name rise on the daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards.

### How to play

- Press and hold to accelerate the ball as it falls the slope.
- Release to allow the ball to rise and capture air off ramps.
