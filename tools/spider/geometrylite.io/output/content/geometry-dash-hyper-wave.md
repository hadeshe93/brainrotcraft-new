**Geometry Dash Hyper Wave** is a fan-made rhythm platformer that puts your wave-riding skills to the test! With neon visuals and 10 intense wave levels, it’s a thrilling ride through a glowing digital world. 

## **What is Geometry Dash Hyper Wave?**

Inspired by the original Geometry Dash, Hyper Wave focuses entirely on the fast-paced Wave mode. Players control a triangular wave ship that rises or dives based on their input; timing and control are everything. The game has 10 unique levels, each with colourful neon artwork, throbbing music, and precise obstacle formations. It's all about navigating tight spaces, sharp bends, and rhythm-based traps at a rapid pace.

### **How to Play** 

- Click and hold the mouse/press SPACEBAR to ascend
- Release to dive downward
