**Chicken Scream Race** is an infinite runner in which your voice, not your fingers, controls the chicken's movement. Talk gently to make it stroll, yell to make it jump, and try not to giggle as your own voice transforms into the ultimate gaming controller!

## **Game Overview**

- Genre: [Endless runner](https://geometrylite.io/tag/endless-runner) / Voice-controlled game
- Platform: Mobile & Web browser

Chicken Scream Race requires you to steer a chicken through a succession of tough platforms using just your voice. Whispering or speaking gently propels the chicken along, whereas harsher sounds cause it to leap over gaps, [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) spikes, or escape lethal traps. It's a funny take on standard platformers, ideal for parties or streaming entertainment.

### **Tips & Tricks to Play**

- Use a quiet room, so background noise does not interfere.
- Practice your voice tones to operate the chicken effortlessly.
- Try humming or whistling to get the chicken to walk steadily.
