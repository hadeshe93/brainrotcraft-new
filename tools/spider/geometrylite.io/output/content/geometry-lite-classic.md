A fan-made adaptation of [Geometry Dash Lite](https://geometrylite.io), Geometry Lite Classic blends accurate platforming with rhythm-based action. Are you certain that you can manage every beat?

## Introduction to Geometry Lite Classic

Geometry Lite Classic offers gamers who enjoy Geometry Dash Lite the traditional version with all of the official levels. This is an excellent pick for those who are looking for a traditional version with hard levels that will challenge both their reflexes and timing. Start Geometry Lite Classic and immerse yourself in colorful music and images right away!
