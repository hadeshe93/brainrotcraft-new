## **What Is Growden.io?**

Growden.io is a charming and addictive .io farming simulation game created by Legion, the talented team behind hits like Kour.io and Poxel.io. Starting with a small plot of land and a few carrot seeds, you’ll farm, harvest, and trade your way to the top — proving that hard work and smart strategy can turn even the smallest field into a thriving agricultural empire.

### **Gameplay Overview**

In Growden.io, you begin your journey with $10 carrot seeds — your first step into the farming world. Each crop you grow and harvest earns you money to unlock better seeds and new plants. As your farm grows, so does your profit potential. Work your way up through a variety of crops:

- Carrots – $10 starter seeds that grow quickly.
- Strawberries – $50 seeds that bring better profits.
- Blueberries – $100 seeds, a sweet mid-tier investment.
- Tomatoes – rare seeds costing $800, prized for their high returns.

### **Controls**

- WASD / Arrow Keys: Move
- Space: Jump
- E: Pick up plants or interact with the shop/sell area
- Hold Right Mouse Button: Rotate or move the camera
