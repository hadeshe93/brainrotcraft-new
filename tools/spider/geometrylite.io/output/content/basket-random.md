**Basket Random** brings you to the exciting world of basketball games for [2-player](https://geometrylite.io/tag/2-player), your goal is to hit as many baskets as possible!

## Basket Random: An innovative and unique basketball game!

Let’s explore Basket Random - Both casual gamers and basketball fans should give it a try! Players of all ages can simply play the game because of its straightforward controls and gameplay. Once you select one of two game types, you will begin to play: either one or two players. To earn points by putting the ball into the hoop of the other team, you must manipulate ragdoll characters on a basketball court.

### Discover a wide range of characters

Every aspect of the basketball game is unpredictable and random, in keeping with the defining gameplay of random sports games. unique characters emerge with every level, giving the game a unique feel. From improved jumping to eccentric shooting techniques, each of them contributes their own flair and special skills. Strive to progress in the game and play as a variety of characters!

## How to play

Your objective is to shoot the basketball into your opponent's hoop in order to score more points than they do, much as in a typical basketball game. When you only have to manage the characters' leap rhythm, everything becomes simple. To catch the ball and toss it to the opponent, they must jump precisely.

### Controls

- Player 1 (In Single Player Mode): Use the “W” key to jump
- Player 2: Use the “Up arrow” key to jump
