**Basketball Stars** is a fast-paced online multiplayer [basketball](https://geometrylite.io/tag/basketball) game that allows you to compete in real-time 1v1 duels. Madpuffers created this game, which puts you in the shoes of professional streetball players that are ready to shoot, steal, and slam dunk their way to victory!

### **What Makes Basketball Stars So Entertaining?**

Basketball Stars puts you on the court and pits you against other players in competitive matches. The gameplay focuses on quick reactions and smart strategies, with various modes to explore, including 1v1 online encounters.

- 1v1 online matches
- 2-player local mode (split-screen fun!)
- Career and tournament modes

Each battle is a race to outscore your opponent in short, adrenaline-pumping rounds—ideal for quick play or longer sessions.

### **How to play**

- Player 1: Use WASD - Move, use B - Shoot/Perform action, use S - Pump, use V - Super-shot, use D twice - Dash
- Player 2: Use Arrow keys- Move, use L - Shoot/Perform action, use DOWN Arrow- Pump, use K - Super-shot, use LEFT Arrow twice - Dash
