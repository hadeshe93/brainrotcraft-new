Welcome from **The Impossible Game** to players who enjoy extremely difficult rhythm-based games. You will put your accuracy and reflexes to the test with this one! 

## About The Impossible Game

As the name implies, The Impossible Game presents an insurmountable task to complete. It is well-known for its difficult rhythm-based gameplay. When this game was originally released in 2010, it gained popularity right away because of its interesting gameplay. It has influenced numerous other well-known [Platform games](https://geometrylite.io/platform.games) and is still extremely popular today. The difficult rhythm-based gameplay of this game will be recognizable to fans of Geometry Dash. 

### A simplistic design and attractive gameplay 

The Impossible Game's simple style and captivating gameplay are some of the factors that contributed to its rise in popularity. Players must possess lightning-fast reflexes and pinpoint accuracy to succeed in the music-based gameplay. Furthermore, the obstacles at each level vary, requiring not just talent but also memorization. Every level is based on a straightforward layout that emphasizes the challenges and main character.

## How to play

A cube that advances automatically in a straight line will be under your control. It is your job to play with this cube such that it can leap over a variety of barriers and spikes. 

**Controls**

To jump, tap the screen, click, or press buttons.
