## Eggy Car: A Challenging Balance Game

Eggy Car is a driving game with unique gameplay in which you can't drive normally because you're transporting an egg. Because eggs are so delicate, the game combines basic controls with difficult physics. Even a minor change in speed can lead your egg to fall from the automobile at any time. This is a challenging test of your ability to maintain balance. Can you keep the egg in the car? How far can you drive?  

### Features

- Physics-based gameplay challenges you to balance an egg on your automobile. 
- A unique graphic configuration.
- A harmonious mix of sound and light. 
- Collect coins to unlock new vehicles with unique handling.
