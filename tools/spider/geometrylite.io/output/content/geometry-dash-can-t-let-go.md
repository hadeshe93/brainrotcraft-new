**Geometry Dash Can’t Let Go** is the sixth official level, and it introduces a bold twist: upside-down [gravity](https://geometrylite.io/tag/gravity) mechanics that flip your gameplay on its head!

## **What is Geometry Dash Can’t Let Go?**

Geometry Dash Can't Let Go, released following Base After Base, pushes the bar with tighter jumps, faster tempo, and a game-changing new mechanic: reverse gravity. The level also has a great soundtrack that complements the aggressive speed, forcing you to stay concentrated as the visual patterns change quickly.

- Difficulty: [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard)
- Stars: ★★★★★★ ([6 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/6-stars))
- Length: 83 seconds
- Minimum Jumps: 108

### **Why You Should Play Can’t Let Go?**

Can't Let Go is a fan-favorite level for those who are ready to tackle more challenging mechanics. Gravity flipping adds a new level of difficulty, requiring rapid thinking and rhythm-based reaction time.
