**Drive Mad** is a thrilling racing game that transports you across obstacle-filled roadways. Make sure your car is balanced so you can cross the finish line safely!

## Introduce the Gameplay of Drive Mad 

Now get behind the wheel and go on insane roads in Drive Mad! Drive your vehicle through challenging racetracks, sharp turns, and around obstructions. You will receive rewards upon finishing each level. Utilize power-ups to outperform your competitors as you use them to unlock our assortment of cars. Now let's put your driving prowess to the test against players from all across the world! 

### Controls

- To proceed, use the W, D/Up Arrow, and Right Arrow.
- To reverse, use the S, A/Down Arrow, and Left Arrow.
- To immediately restart the level, press the R key.
