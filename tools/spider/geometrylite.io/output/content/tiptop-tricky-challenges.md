## About TipTop: Tricky Challenges 

TipTop: Tricky Challenges is a new entry in our [Puzzle Games](https://geometrylite.io/puzzle.games) category that tests your skills and logical thinking. In this game, you must tackle a range of tough puzzles, which are the most popular challenges on the TikTok platform. If you use TikTok regularly, it will not be too unusual or difficult! Can you be the player with the highest score and dominate the leaderboard? Let's check it out!

### How to play

To play, challenge yourself with each mini-game in TipTop: Tricky Challenges. Each mini-game presents a task with varying degrees of complexity, putting your manual dexterity, logical thinking abilities, and so on to the test.
