## A new mod of the FNF game: Friday Night Funkin vs Whitty

Whitty is the new character in this FNF game mod! Whitty, the rock star, challenged his boy pal to a musical duel after abruptly emerging from a dim alley. When a boyfriend has to confront a musician, it will be an exciting confrontation mission. Great music and a heated struggle are what this promises to be. Let's take on the well-known rock star in Friday Night Funkin vs Whitty right now!

### List of songs

- Lo-Fight
- Overhead
- Ballistic (new)

## How to play

You will appreciate the song and complete the goal of properly pressing the keys on the screen, just like in other versions. You must focus immediately upon the beginning of this rendition due to its unique, lively, and fast-paced music.
