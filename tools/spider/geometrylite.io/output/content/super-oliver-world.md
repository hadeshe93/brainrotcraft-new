The Super Mario series is the origin of inspiration for **Super Oliver World**, a platformer game. Are you prepared to go on a new journey?

## Introduce Super Oliver World

In this edition, the player takes on the role of Oliver, the primary character. He's going to go on a fantastical journey through colorful, creative worlds full of obstacles and foes. Oliver has several carefully crafted stages that he must sprint, jump, and navigate. It is your job to guide him through each one. There are plenty of risks and surprises around every corner as you traverse across a variety of terrain types, including verdant forests, freezing mountains, dangerous caverns, and flaming vistas.

### Features

- Players who enjoy the classic game will find it nostalgic in its retro design and simple gameplay.
- A wide range of environments full of fresh and intriguing stuff, and a diverse level design with a range of difficulties.
- Gather numerous goods to get ready to take on strong opponents, as well as a variety of power-ups to improve your skills.
