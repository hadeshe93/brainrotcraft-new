## About Endless Runner Game Geometry Rush 4D

Players drive a cube across a colorful, obstacle-filled landscape in the rhythm-based platform game Geometry Rush 4D, which is reminiscent of games in the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) series. In addition to adding depth to the action and a new 4D element, the updated Geometry Dash game boasts a vibrant and colorful visual interface. The feeling of jumping, sliding, and flipping in a 4D environment will undoubtedly be captivating. 

## How to play

To avoid obstacles, leap, and slide while earning coins, use the arrow key. Pay attention to the beat and precisely execute the dance steps!
