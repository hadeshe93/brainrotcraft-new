**Traffic Jam 3D** is a realistic driving game set on a congested highway, with the goal of arriving at your destination on time. 

## Unleash Your Driving Skills in Traffic Jam 3D

In **Traffic Jam 3D**, heavy traffic is a major barrier that keeps you from speeding. There are many different kinds of cars on the road, and you must utilize your driving abilities to keep the car under control, steer clear of other vehicles, and yet cross the finish line safely. This is a fantastic chance for you to become more proficient as a driver! Can you navigate the crowd and reach the finish line in time? Play the game right now to find out!

## How to play

- Use the arrow keys to drive.
- To brake, use the space bar. 
- Press N to activate Nitro. 

### Upgrade your car

Try to reach the finish line in each stage to get bonus points. Use this money to improve your present vehicle, or perhaps buy a new vehicle with higher performance and features.
