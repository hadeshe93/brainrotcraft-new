## Unleash your musical talent in Sprunki Phase 3! 

[Sprunki Games](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sprunki-games) has returned in a new version with many new and innovative features in Sprunki Phase 3! This is a fantastic fan-made adaptation of the renowned music game Incredibox. Special Sprunki characters have returned to create a bizarre but incredibly appealing music world with fresh sound combinations and different visual styles. It's time to rekindle your musical creativity and make the best song!

### How to play

- Choose your sounds: A collection of sounds from instruments and effects appears below the characters. Begin by selecting the sounds that you believe are required for your music.
- Assign Sounds to the Sprunki characters. Simply drag & drop the selected sounds onto the desired characters with your cursor.
- Create and mix. Tracks: Build your musical masterpiece by layering sounds from the characters and cleverly blending to produce incredible masterpieces!
