## **Glitch Dash: Enter the Glitch and Survive**

Glitch Dash is not your typical [running](https://geometrylite.io/tag/running) game; it is an exciting first-person [obstacle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacle)\-dodging adventure created by David Marquardt. This game throws everything at you from the first level, including razor-sharp traps, swinging hammers, moving walls, and unexpected perils. Think quickly, move quickly, and don't blink—a single mistake move could end the game.

### **What Makes Glitch Dash Unique?**

This game puts you in the action with a first-person view, making every obstacle feel dangerously close:

- Fast-paced action: To [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) whirling saws, slam doors, lasers, and spike traps, you'll need to have precise timing.
- Collect diamonds: Collect diamonds along the route to earn points, but don't allow them distract you from your goal of survival.
- Stylish vibe: Each level has throbbing music and glitchy, futuristic visuals that complement the game's fast-paced excitement.

### **Controls**

- Use the Arrow keys – Move left or right
- Use Spacebar – Jump over traps and gaps
