**Geometry Dash Benevolent** gives players a new adventure to complete a [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard) level with a difficulty rating of 10 stars and 3 user coins.

## Introduce Geometry Dash Benevolent

This [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/hard.games) level contains numerous hurdles for you to overcome. Throughout the level, you will meet obstacles like spikes and saw blades, as well as moving platforms and gravity-altering parts. Memorizing, particularly the harder sections, can help you overcome these difficulties more readily. You may have to start again several times, but this will allow you to collect more experience and quickly develop your talents. 

### Controls Guide

To leap over obstacles, press the w key, arrow keys, or click.
