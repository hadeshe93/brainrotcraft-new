## Introduce Funny Shooter 2 

Funny Shooter 2 is a daring first-person shooter game in which you must fire accurately to defeat hordes of humorous foes in a wacky atmosphere. This is the second installment of the amusing Shooter game, and there are numerous new gameplay upgrades as well as more amusing and scary opponents for you to defeat. Furthermore, as you go through the game, you will unlock new weaponry. Are you ready to join the arena now?

### How to play

Slowly, opponents will enter the arena and move toward you. To safely complete each level, you must plan your movements and strategically eliminate your opponents. Attack weapon switching allows you to adapt to varied opponents!
