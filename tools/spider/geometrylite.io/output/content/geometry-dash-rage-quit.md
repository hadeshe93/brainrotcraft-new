Geometry Dash Rage Quit is an Easy level in the Geometry Dash world created by the incredible user bli. Despite its [Easy](https://geometrylite.io/tag/easy) rating, the level stands out for its fast speed, clever traps, and rage-inducing moments, which live up to its name.

## **Introduce Geometry Dash Rage Quit**

Welcome to Geometry Dash Rage Quit! While it is labelled as "Easy," don't be fooled—this [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced), troll-filled level is designed to startle, challenge, and, yes, possibly force you to leave angrily. What makes it so unique? Rage Quit's a unique reimagining of Stereo Madness, Geometry Dash's first official level. Rage Quit reimagines the original pattern, incorporating clever traps, inverted tunnels, and perplexing twists.

### **How to play**

To play Rage Quit, move a [cube](https://geometrylite.io/tag/cube) through a wild remix of Stereo Madness, avoiding spikes, surprising reversals, fake-outs, and obstacles. Many traps require memorisation; use checkpoints!
