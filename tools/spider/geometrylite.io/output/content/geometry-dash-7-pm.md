**Geometry Dash 7 PM** is well-known for its distinctive theme and atmosphere, which are inspired by the colours and mood of a twilight sky around 7 p.m. This [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon) level, created by a skilled team that includes melX0exe, Serponge, Lebreee, and others, provides a visually captivating experience while being balanced in difficulty.

## **About Geometry Dash 7 PM**

Geometry Dash 7 PM's design nicely portrays the peace and beauty of dusk, with rich purples, pinks, and oranges mixing together to resemble a sky right after sunset. Bright colours allow players to readily follow the cube's movement, making tough areas clearer and easier to navigate. With smooth gameplay and thrilling transitions, 7 PM provides an immersive atmosphere that keeps players interested from beginning to end.

### **Music That Matches the Mood**

The song "Santa-chan's Winter Wonderland Adventure" by nstryder may sound cheerful, but its energetic pace pairs surprisingly well with the level's evening feel. The strong beat guides your jumps and movements, keeping the pace interesting throughout the run.
