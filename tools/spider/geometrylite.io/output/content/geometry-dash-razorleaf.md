## **Geometry Dash Razorleaf - Overview**

Geometry Dash Razorleaf is a fan-made creation by eplecentraa that pushes the limits of creativity and rhythm. Unlike regular levels, this pack introduces three visually stunning and musically rich challenges, each designed with glowing neon colors that captivate players from start to finish.

### **Levels & Difficulty**

- Leafwood Forest (Normal, 3 stars): A bright and invigorating starting point with a balanced difficulty level, ideal for warming up.
- Aurora Theory (Hard, 5 stars): A more challenging stage with fast transitions and rhythmic patterns to test your timing.
- Ocean Paradise Planet (Harder, 7 stars): The most challenging of the three, with [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) action, limited pathways, and stunning vistas.

### **Why You Should Play Razorleaf?**

Geometry Dash Razorleaf isn’t just about difficulty—it’s about immersion. The glowing neon landscapes, combined with catchy music, create a hypnotic flow that keeps players hooked. The high-energy beats perfectly complement the vibrant design, making every jump, flip, and dash feel alive.
