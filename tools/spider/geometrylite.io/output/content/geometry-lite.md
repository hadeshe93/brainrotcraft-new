## **GEOMETRY DASH - THE MAIN VERSION**

Geometry Dash is the main game of this hit 2013 rhythm-based [platformer](https://geometrylite.io/platform.games) series. Robert Topala, a Swedish developer, first released this game under his company’s name, RobTop Games. 

The game’s sole developer has worked tirelessly to update and expand its geometric world. Initially inspired by the 2009 Impossible Game, he set out to create a game featuring a cube that could crash and jump. After four months of development, the game launched in August 2013 with eight levels. By integrating rhythm into the gameplay, Robert created a pioneering experience unlike any other games available at the time.

After its release, with frequent updates and new gameplay, it soon carved out its own identity and gained a large fan base.

### **General Gameplay**

Geometry Dash Lite challenges players to guide a geometric icon through obstacle-filled levels synced to music. Your icon can jump or fly, but if it collides with any obstacle, you have to replay the level. You will experience thrilling levels filled with moving platforms, spikes, lasers, etc.! Each presenting distinct challenges that will put your timing and reflexes to the ultimate test. To conquer the game's increasing difficulty, practice makes perfect!

### **How to Controls**

You can use one of three keys here to make the character jump:

- Spacebar
- Up arrow key
- Left mouse button

If you play in practice mode, you can press the Z key to activate checkpoints and use the X key to remove the closest one.

### **Tips and Tricks on How to Dash Like a Pro**

To have an outstanding achievement in Geometry Dash Lite, you can refer to some tips:

- **Adapt to the different geometric forms**: Your icon can transform a cube into a ship, UFO, robot, and more. Each form has its unique abilities, so players should learn to adapt to the other forms quickly. This helps players understand the game’s mechanism better, reducing the chance of failure and restart.
- **Be patient**: The game is challenging, but it is essential to be diligent and keep trying.
- **Practice regularly:** Practice frequently to significantly improve your level and skills. Experiment with other jumps and flights to find what works best for you.
- **Take breaks**: If you have failed a level repeatedly, take a break and come back to the game later. Extreme obstacles are inevitable; relax a bit and calm down. When you come back, you will find a new motivation to solve the challenges in front of you.

If you have fallen in love with the intuitive but addicting gameplay of Geometry Dash Lite, then, besides the original Geometry Dash game, let's try out other titles in the franchise:

### **Editor and User Levels**

Besides the spin-off versions, Geometry Dash has never been out of trend thanks to numerous user levels created by dashers (including you).

The editor in Geometry Dash is the tool that allows players to create their own custom levels. It’s a level design interface where players can place obstacles, set the layout, customize the difficulty, and add elements like spikes, platforms, and collectibles. Players can check out user levels, make adjustments, and share it with the community once they have created it.

User levels can have difficulty even higher than the official game's hardest level, **Demon**. In user levels, there are five sub-levels within the Demon difficulty:

**[Demon](https://geometrylite.io/demon.games)**

**[Easy Demon](https://geometrylite.io/easy-demon.games)**

**[Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/medium-demon.games)**

**[Insane Demon](https://geometrylite.io/insane-demon.games)**

**[Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/extreme-demon.games)**

Various user levels stand out for their creative designs and challenging gameplay, attracting players from all over the world. For example:

- **[Geometry Dash Nine Circles](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-nine-circles)** (Hard Demon) by Zobros is the second most-played user level. This particular level skillfully incorporates the theme of hell into its gameplay, blending challenging mechanics with a fiery, intense atmosphere. 
- **[Geometry Dash Dastardly](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-dastardly.htm)** (Insane) by Subwoofer in version 2.2 is also getting attention for its challenging and exciting gameplay. 
- **[Geometry Dash Bloodbath](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-bloodbath)** (Extreme Demon) by Riot is a unique collaboration, becoming the most visited user-level ever in the series.
- **[Geometry Dash Kappaclysm](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-kappaclysm)** (Harder) by Glittershroom and Evasium is the only level to ever appear in both the Map Packs and the Gauntlet.
- **[Geometry Dash Retray](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-retray)** (Easy) by DiMaViKuLov26 is a level that most new players can beat on their first try, an ideal practice ground.
