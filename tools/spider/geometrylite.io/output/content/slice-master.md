## Introduce Slice Master

**Slice Master** takes you to the pinnacle of timing, accuracy, and smart slicing! You can practice your slicing abilities with a single button while simultaneously taking in the soothing effects of an ASMR game. The game challenges the player with a large number of stages full of obstacles and more difficult targets. Let's start slicing through targets and gathering coins so you can use them to unlock all of the knife's versions.

## How to play

Everything in your path, including fruits, trees, food, etc., can be sliced. However, you have to stay clear of the pink spikes and keep the knife from dropping to the ground. Remain focused at all times since you can participate in the knife tossing and get bonus points by multiplying your score multiple times once you cross the finish line. 

### Controls

To flip the knife and jump, use Space or Left-click.
