## **Jump Into Action with Geometry Cube: Challenge**

Geometry Cube: Challenge delivers a [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced), reaction-based platformer that tests your timing and rhythm. Take control of a nimble cube and navigate through seven thrilling levels, each packed with its traps, layouts, and movement patterns. With a customizable gameplay system and smooth controls, this game is all about precision and focus.

### **Features That Set It Apart**

- 7 Levels of Cube-Based Chaos: Each level ramps up in difficulty, introducing new platforms, spike placements, and hazards. The minimalistic visuals keep your focus locked on gameplay.
- Customized System for Replay Value: You’ll have access to a unique system that tweaks your gameplay experience—whether it's jump sensitivity or [cube](https://geometrylite.io/tag/cube) design. Make the challenge your own!
- Simple Yet Addictive Controls: You can jump by pressing the left mouse button, spacebar, or simply tapping the screen. Hold it down for continuous jumping—perfect for dodging a tight series of obstacles.
