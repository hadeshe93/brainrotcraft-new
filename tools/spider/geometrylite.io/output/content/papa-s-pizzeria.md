## Manage your restaurant in Papa's Pizzeria

**Papa's Pizzeria** is part of a management game series created by Flipline Studios and published in 2007. This game has become an icon in the world of Papa's games due to its simple but captivating gameplay. You will play the role of a pizza cook, fulfilling customer orders and providing them with the most delectable pizza items. The number of orders will progressively increase, putting pressure on time. This demands you to have strong managerial abilities to run the kitchen and serve guests as swiftly as feasible.

### How to play

Drag and drop ingredients to combine them into pizzas that meet the expectations of customers.
