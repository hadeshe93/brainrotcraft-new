### **Wild Gameplay Meets Meme Culture**

- Wave Mode Mechanics: To rise, use the left-click or up arrow, and to fall, use the release button, as in classic Geometry Dash wave controls.
- Colourful Meme Levels: Each level presents a fresh group of Italian Brainrot "animals," complete with flashing colours and unpredictable patterns.
