**Geometry Dash Ultra Violence** is a fan-made [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon) level that delivers serious style, sync, and speed. If you're into high-skill challenges with killer beats and razor-sharp gameplay, this one's a must-play.

## **About Geometry Dash Ultra Violence**

Geometry Dash Ultra Violence is a fan-made version based by RobTop Games' original Geometry Dash game. It was not designed by RobTop, but rather by passionate community members who wanted to challenge gamers with something new and extreme. Geometry Dash Ultra Violence is a unique Medium Demon level designed by [Xender Game](https://geometrylite.io/tag/xender-game) and validated by Viprin. It combines powerful visuals, precisely synchronised audio, and tight gameplay.

### **What Makes Ultra Violence Stand Out**

- Medium Demon Difficulty: Tough but not impossible. Ideal for experienced players trying to advance.
- Developed by Xender Game: Known for its refined level design and innovative gameplay innovations.
- Polished Aesthetics: Clean effects, snappy transitions, and a compelling visual [rhythm](https://geometrylite.io/rhythm.games) that keeps you interested.
