Welcome to the **Space Rocket Simulator**! It's time to create and construct rockets, launch them into space, and navigate through the numerous celestial bodies.

## Introduce Space Rocket Simulator

In the **Space Rocket Simulator**, you can go into [space](https://geometrylite.io/tag/space) and learn about rocket physics! Space Rocket Simulator is a realistic and immersive experience for constructing, launching, and maneuvering rockets over the vastness of space. You'll become acquainted with the components of a spacecraft. And you must put them together in the correct configuration to launch into space.

### Tips to play Space Rocket Simulator

- Master Orbital Mechanics: Learn the fundamentals of orbital mechanics to plan your paths.
- Design Efficient Rockets: Learn about rocket parts and improve your rocket design.
- Practice Maneuvering: Use your maneuvering talents to navigate difficult orbits.
