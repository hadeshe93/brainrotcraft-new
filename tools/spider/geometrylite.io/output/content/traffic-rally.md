**Traffic Rally** is a new addition to our Racing & Driving games collection. This game will put you in the driver's seat of a fast car as it navigates through a congested city.

## Your goal in Traffic Rally

In the middle of heavy traffic, your goal in Traffic Rally is to handle your car to become the leader and cross the finish line first! It will be difficult to reach this goal since you must avoid colliding with other vehicles or obstructions on the route. This is a test of your driving skills and reaction time on the road. Are you ready to start this race right now? 

### Controls

- To steer the car, use the left and right arrow keys.
- Use the up arrow key to accelerate.
