Inspired by Geometry Dash, **Geometry Vibes X-Ball** is an entertaining arcade game that tests players' reflexes and synchronization of rhythm.

## Introducing Geometry Vibes X-Ball: A Rhythm-Powered Arcade Challenge!

Welcome to the Geometry Vibes X-Ball! This is a new fan-made version that includes the popular [Wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) mode from the original game Geometry Dash. In this game, you control a customized ball as it navigates through bright stages full of obstacles, such as whirling gears and stinging spikes. This version has numerous improved game types and levels of varying difficulty. Have fun!

### Game Modes

- Classic Mode: 10 progressively harder stages that lead to a gateway that takes you to the next level. 
- Endless Mode: See how far you can go without running into any problems to see how resilient you are.
- Challenge Mode: Take on five carefully crafted levels that offer experienced players challenging and distinctive experiences. 
- Race Mode: Race through the levels with up to four people on the same device to see who gets to the end first.
