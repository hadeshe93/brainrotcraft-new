## **Geometry Dash Sunshine – A Bright and Rhythmic Journey**

Geometry Dash Sunshine is a visually stunning [Harder](https://geometrylite.io/tag/harder) level designed by [Unzor](https://geometrylite.io/tag/unzor). This level, set in a vibrant world of color and song, has a unique twist: two animated figures, the sun and the moon, sing along to the soundtrack, bringing the entire experience to life. The soundtrack tune, Tessa Violet's "Just Right", nicely complements the level's lively charm and rhythmic gameplay.

### **Coin Guide – How to Collect All 3 Coins**

- Coin 1 – 16%: You must collect 3 small spheres scattered before the 16% mark to unlock this coin.
- Coin 2 – 24%: First, collect the small sphere at 19%. Then [avoid](https://geometrylite.io/tag/avoid) the green sphere at 23% to access the coin path.
- Coin 3 – 87%: Simply skip the green sphere before the coin section to stay on the correct route and grab the final coin.
