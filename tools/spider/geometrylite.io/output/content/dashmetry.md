**Dashmetry** is a fan-made rhythm platformer inspired by Geometry Dash, featuring custom levels, endless modes, and a dynamic music library.

## **Enter the Electrifying World of Dashmetry**

Dashmetry brings a thrilling twist to the classic rhythm-platformer formula that fans of Geometry Dash love. With a sleek interface, community-built levels, and multiple game modes, this fan-made version keeps your reflexes sharp and your adrenaline high. Whether you want to create your challenge or compete for top scores, Dashmetry has something for everyone.

### **Key Features that Keep You Hooked**

- Private Profile System: Log in and save your in-game progress to come back anytime without losing your achievements.
- Multiple Game Modes: Challenge yourself in Endless Mode or race against the clock and other players in Race Mode.
- Player-Created Levels: Experience a world of limitless creativity with custom levels built by players around the globe. No two runs are ever the same.
- Music Library Access: Upload your favorite tracks or download community picks to add a new layer of excitement to your gameplay.

## **How to Play Dashmetry**

The core of Dashmetry is all about precision timing and fast reflexes. Navigate a cube across complex [obstacle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacle) courses that constantly change with each segment. Every jump and movement must be perfectly timed to avoid spikes, rotating hazards, and shifting platforms.

**Controls:** Use left Mouse Button/Up Arrow Key/Spacebar
