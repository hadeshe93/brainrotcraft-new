## **Introduce Geometry Dash Sakupen Circles**

If you thought you’ve conquered the hardest Geometry Dash levels, Geometry Dash Sakupen Circles is here to prove you wrong. Created as a tribute to the iconic Nine Circles style, this fan-favorite has earned its place as the hardest-rated [Nine Circles](https://geometrylite.io/tag/nine-circles) level ever released—a brutal 10-star Extreme Demon that only elite players dare to challenge.

### **Why Sakupen Circles Is So Difficult?**

- Core gameplay style: The conventional Nine Circles wave sequence is enhanced with unpredictable speed shifts, tight gaps, and dramatic gravity flips.
- Fast Transitions: Rapid dual-mode switches and size adjustments allow no space for error.
- Sync-Heavy Design: Every obstacle is timed to the RMX version of Iron God, compelling you to play by rhythm while enduring harsh patterns.
