### **Dash through the Easter Island**

The journey of the tiny egg in Egg Dash is the conquest of the beautiful Easter Island. You can see typical island statues along with other holiday decorations. You will maneuver your character through a series of complex terrain and tricky barriers. Furthermore, in some segments, the egg is also equipped with a spaceship to move in the air. This shape of the character will have different moves compared to the original state. So, how to control it conveniently?

## **Navigate Your Egg**

For the egg, you just need to click or press the spacebar to make the character jump. To jump continuously, hold down the command key. Meanwhile, the spaceship shape will require players to hold down the key for the entity to fly up and release it to fly downward.

Besides the main moves, your egg can also jump with colorful rings and head pads. To activate rings, as soon as the character touches this object, you need to press the key once. As for pads, the egg will automatically jump upon contact.
