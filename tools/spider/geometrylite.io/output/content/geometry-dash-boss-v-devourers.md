## Introducing Geometry Dash Boss V Devourers, a fast-paced action game! 

Geometry Dash Boss V Devourers is a Medium Demon that [Xender Game](https://geometrylite.io/tag/xender-game) created and is known for its menacing figures during the boss fight phase. The level is a component of Xender Game's well-known Hexalogy boss series, which consists of numerous segments with progressively harder difficulty levels. The fifth installment in this series, Boss V Devourers, has action gameplay that moves quickly. Even though a lot of players consider this to be an easy [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon), there are still some challenging parts of the game that need players to focus intensely to finish the level. 

### Interesting related information

- This Medium Demon is 54,800 objects long and averages 1 minute and 28 seconds in length.
- Many gamers adore the level because of its eye-catching elements, which are frequently portrayed as frightening characters during boss battles.
- Robtop Boss Mode, Robtop Boss Mode-2, BOSS 3 Electro, and Boss 4 Laser Eye are the remaining levels in the Hexalogy boss series.
