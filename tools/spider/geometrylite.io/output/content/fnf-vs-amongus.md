## Introduce FNF vs AmongUs

FNF vs AmongUs is a new version of the FNF game in which you will battle well-known characters from the game Among Us. An impostor from the famous series has stepped out of the spacecraft and is ready to participate in this musical war. Start leveling up immediately and conquer three epic tracks in FNF versus AmongUs: Sussus Moogus, Sabotage, and Meltdown. Start this musical trip right now and challenge the impostors!
