**Monster Truck: Drive Me** is an addictive racing game where you must control a truck to overcome a series of obstacles on every road.

## Start a thrilling ride in Monster Truck: Drive Me

Monster Truck: Drive Me is the newest addition to the [Drive Mad](https://geometrylite.io/tag/drive-mad) series, having recently been updated. You will go on another thrilling voyage with a monster truck through a variety of terrains, each with its own unique and tough landscape. Drive your monster truck through a variety of obstacles, ramps, and platforms to reach the finish line. As you go through the stages, you will face greater problems and realize that success is determined by skill rather than speed. 

### Controls Guide

Use the arrow keys to guide your truck through a variety of varied levels full of hazards, ramps, and surprising shocks. 

Hundreds of interesting levels with unexpected difficulties await you; hop in the car and begin your journey today!
