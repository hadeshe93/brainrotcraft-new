Welcome to the racing game **PolyTrack**! Prepare to drive through a variety of terrains and showcase your exceptional speed. 

## About PolyTrack

PolyTrack is a 3D time trial racing game in which you compete against yourself across a variety of terrain to get the best possible times. You can begin your voyage by selecting one of several cars or tracks. Each voyage is intriguing and provides a new experience because each track is distinct and presents different tasks to accomplish. As you go through the game, you will have the opportunity to acquire more cool automobiles with improved performance.

## How to play PolyTrack

Selecting one of the two game modes—Racing Mode or Track Design Mode—is how you begin the game. When your automobile is in Racing Mode, you turn it on and begin racing, where the main goal is speed. In contrast, you can test the races and alter the tracks to your preference in Track Design mode. You can display your boundless inventiveness in this mode.

### Controls

- To drive, use the WASD or arrow keys.
- To restart the race, press R or Enter.
- After completing a track, compete against your ghost.
