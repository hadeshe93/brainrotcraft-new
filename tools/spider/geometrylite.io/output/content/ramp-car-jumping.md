In the driving game **Ramp Car Jumping**, you take control of an insane [car](https://geometrylite.io/tag/car) and navigate difficult vertical ramps while dodging obstacles. It is a novel and difficult gaming experience.

## Gameplay of Ramp Car Jumping

A fun gameplay feature of Ramp Car Jumping is being able to make the highest and craziest jumps with your car. Select your preferred vehicle and terrain first. Initially, the options are restricted, but they can be unlocked gradually. It is your goal to steer your vehicle across difficult vertical ramps, go past obstacles, and cross the finish line without incident. Keep in mind that speed matters too; placing first will enable you to earn more rewards as you go through each level.

### Controls Guide 

- Use Left-click to interact in-game
- Use WASD/ arrow keys to move 
- Use space to jump

### Upgrades and Design your tracks 

There are more than a dozen worlds with different game modes. You can use the coins you earn as you advance in the game to unlock your preferred ramp car, which will allow you to execute amazing jumps and tricks to scale new heights. As you advance through the game, you'll unlock a ton of new features to make it more fun.
