## **Avatar World – Create, Explore, and Live Your Story**

Avatar World is a life simulation game that lets players express their creativity and imagination in a vibrant, open-ended world. Create your own unique avatar and dive into a lively city filled with exciting places to visit, from cozy apartments to stylish boutiques and cafés. With adorable visuals and endless customization, Avatar World offers a sandbox-style experience reminiscent of Toca Boca—where every interaction sparks joy and every corner hides a new surprise.

### **How to play**

Start by designing your dream character, then step out to explore a bustling city full of fun activities. Visit shops, meet other characters, decorate your home, or discover secret areas hidden throughout the world. Each visit brings something new to uncover, encouraging endless play and imagination.

- Mouse / Touch: Tap or click to interact, move objects, and navigate menus.
- Drag and Drop: Customize your character or decorate rooms.
