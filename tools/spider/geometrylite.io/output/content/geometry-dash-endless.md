## Conquer endless gameplay in Geometry Dash Endless!

Geometry Dash Endless is an unofficial level regarded as fairly easy, with a difficulty rating of 5 stars. This is FlappySheepy's [Normal](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal) level and the Force Pack's introduction. Rather than following a fixed set of stages, the game generates an infinite number of randomly generated levels, each more difficult than the last. 

### How to beat this level

Your objective is to navigate a small square through increasingly challenging, obstacle-filled levels. To complete the level, you must leap and avoid many obstacles such as spikes, platforms, and other hazards, with the exception that the level is unlimited.
