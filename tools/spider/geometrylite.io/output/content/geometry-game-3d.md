**Geometry Game 3D** brings you to a fantastic 3D universe where you may immerse yourself in the music world and familiar platforms from the Geometry Dash game.

## Introduce Geometry Game 3D

Prepare to engage in Geometry Game 3D - A 3D version of Geometry Dash take on the [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) series, in which you take control of a cube and set out to conquer numerous thrilling obstacle courses. You are invited to discover a vibrant and captivating universe that seamlessly combines 3D design. It's time to put your reflexes and ability to react to the test once more while listening to thrilling background music. 

## How to **play**

Simply use the spacebar to make your character jump precisely at the proper moment to get over obstacles to play this game.
