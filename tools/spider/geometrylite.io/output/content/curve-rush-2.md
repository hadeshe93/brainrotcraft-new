## **Curve Rush 2 – Dash Through Extreme Terrains** 

Curve Rush 2 brings a whole new layer of excitement with upgraded maps and fresh effects. Players now get to experience icy landscapes, fiery lava zones, and mysterious night trails that test adaptability and lightning-fast reflexes. If you’re a fan of high-energy arcade challenges like Geometry Dash, this game is right up your alley. 

### **Gameplay & features**

- Dynamic maps: Ice, lava, and night effects change how you move and react.
- Simple yet challenging mechanics: Instead of guiding a cube, you now control a ball acing across desert slopes.
- Endless distance run: The further you go, the higher your score—perfect for testing your endurance. 
- Fast arcade action: Every slope, curve, and jump keeps you on edge, demanding precision timing.

### **Controls**

- Hold Left Mouse Button – Build speed and momentum while climbing slopes.
- Release – Stop pushing and prepare for the perfect jump. 

### **Why play Curve Rush 2?**

This sequel takes the thrill of [Curve Rush](https://geometrylite.io/curve-rush) and dials it up with dynamic environments and sharper challenges. The ball mechanic introduces a smoother, more fluid motion that makes every run unique. Whether you’re chasing a new high score or want an adrenaline-packed dash, Curve Rush 2 delivers quick, addictive fun.
