**Labubu Geometry Waves** is a fan-made wave-mode challenge inspired by the Geometry Dash universe. You’ll pilot a tiny plane through 40 increasingly difficult levels, dodging obstacles, collecting power-ups, and enjoying Labubu’s adorable dance animations as you fly.

## **Labubu Geometry Waves** - Master the Rhythm of the Waves

In this [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) mode, precision is everything. Hold to soar, release to dive, and thread through tight spaces at high speed. Each stage tests your reflexes and timing as glowing patterns shift to the rhythm. Power-ups scattered across levels give you temporary boosts, making the flight even more thrilling.

### How to Play

- Hold – Move upward
- Release – Move downward
- Avoid Obstacles: Colliding ends your run
- Collect Power-Ups: Gain boosts to survive longer
- Goal: Reach the finish line and watch Labubu dance!

### Features That Make It Unique

- Wave-Only Gameplay: Focused entirely on Geometry Dash’s most challenging mode.
- 40 Handcrafted Levels: Each stage brings new obstacles and rhythmic flow.
- Adorable Labubu Animation: Watch Labubu cheer you on with every success.
- Simple Controls, High Skill Ceiling: Easy to learn but tough to master.
