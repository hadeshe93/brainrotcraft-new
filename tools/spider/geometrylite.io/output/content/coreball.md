In the arcade game **Coreball**, players must control a bouncing ball and make the correct toss into the center area.

## Discover the Addictive Gameplay of Coreball!

For players of all skill levels searching for a game to sharpen their reflexes and abilities, Coreball is the ideal option. It is your responsibility to precisely and without colliding with any obstacles toss the bouncing ball into the middle area. There are several stages in this game, and each one has its layouts and difficulties. Later stages have increasingly difficult tasks that need fast reflexes to make perfect throws. 

### Controls

To throw the ball, click on your screen!
