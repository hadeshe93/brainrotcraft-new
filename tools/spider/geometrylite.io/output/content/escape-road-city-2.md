**Escape Road City 2** is a challenging driving game in which you must evade police forces while avoiding hazards in the congested city. This game features high-speed chases, strategic driving, and explosive action.

## Overview Gameplay of Escape Road City 2

Escape Road City 2 is the most recently updated game in the [Escape Road series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/escape-road-series). This game immerses you in a chaotic city where survival depends on your driving skills. The gameplay focuses on traversing congested urban locations, avoiding constant police pursuits, and outmaneuvering competing gangs. This enhanced version has numerous new gameplay features and design touches that promise to provide you with an enjoyable chasing experience!

### Controls

- To move the vehicle, use the left and right arrow keys or the /D shortcut.
- Press the Spacebar to exit the vehicle and immediately transfer to another vehicle.
