**Step into the infinite tunnel of Run 3! Run through space tunnels, navigate tricky holes, and defy gravity in this endless runner packed with challenges.**

Recruit new teammates and join Run 3 now to continue exploring the mysteries of the universe. No more vague journeys with unknown destinations, you now have a road map in your hand. Still a small alien, but after his predecessors, this character is more experienced. However, all the most impressive achievements depend on your top improvisational ability. Ready? Join to search for precious treasures of the universe now!

## How to play

You maneuver the character, moving on tunnels, through a series of levels. Unlike games in the same series, Run 3 has larger holes and even loses a piece of moving platform. The difficulty of the game is unbelievable, but as long as you persevere, good results will come! Furthermore, once you reach a certain level, players can completely retry previous rounds.

### **Control the Character**

You control the character by using the left or right arrow keys to navigate to either side. To jump up, you can use the spacebar or the up arrow key. Great achievements will be the key to owning other special characters. Show off your ultimate reflexes and collect a full collection of adorable alien creatures!
