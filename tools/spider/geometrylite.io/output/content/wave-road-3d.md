## **Introduce Dive into Wave Road 3D**

Wave Road 3D is a mesmerizing arcade game that blends the rhythmic intensity of Geometry Dash with sleek 3D visuals inspired by [Geometry Vibes 3D](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-vibes-3d). Glide through glowing tunnels, twist between barriers, and push your focus to the limit. With 20 handcrafted stages and an endless mode where speed keeps rising, only the most precise and patient players can master the wave.

### **How to Play: Precision in Motion**

Avoid spikes, barriers, and traps as you glide through [neon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/neon) corridors. Every movement counts — one wrong tap and it’s back to the start!

Controls:

- Player 1: W / Spacebar / Left Mouse Click
- Player 2 (2P mode): Up Arrow Key

### **Game Modes That Keep You Hooked**

- Classic Mode: 20 handcrafted levels with unique layouts and rhythmic challenges.
- 2 Player Mode: Race head-to-head with a friend to see who can survive the longest.
- Endless Mode: The arrow accelerates endlessly — only the most focused players will go the distance.
