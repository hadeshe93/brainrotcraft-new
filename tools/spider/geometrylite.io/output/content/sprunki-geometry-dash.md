Sprunki Geometry Dash is a creative mod that brings the iconic Geometry Dash character into the chaotic and unpredictable world of [Sprunki](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sprunki), a viral reaction-based game. This mashup combines diverse art styles, unusual movements, and chaotic gameplay to create a fun and unexpected experience.

## **Sprunki Geometry Dash: A Mod Mashup of Chaos and Fun**

Sprunki Geometry Dash is not a typical platformer or rhythm game. Instead, it's a Sprunki mod, a fast-paced, reaction-based game with Geometry Dash as the playable character. This mod combines two legendary characters from distinct game universes. If you appreciate Geometry Dash and meme-style games like Sprunki, this crossover is a fun, light-hearted way to combine the two.

### **How to play**

- Combine Sprunki with skins of musical instruments, Geometry Dash characters, and other items with a simple drag-and-drop interface.
- Every skin represents a distinct sound. Fun mixes can allow you to build a trademark track that is completely your own!
