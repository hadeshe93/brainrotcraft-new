## **INTRODUCE FLAMEWALL WAVE CHALLENGE – THE FAN LEVEL TURNED GAME**

Flamewall Wave Challenge is a fan-made version of Geometry Dash inspired by the iconic [Flamewall](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-dash-flamewall-new-top-1.htm) level. This standalone fan creation focuses purely on wave mode, where precision and rhythm are everything. The screen fills with fiery walls and razor-thin passages, demanding perfect clicks to survive. As the difficulty ramps up, you’ll find yourself in a true endurance test that rewards patience and [skill](https://geometrylite.io/tag/skill).

### **How to play**

- Press Space, Up Arrow, click on the screen, or click with your mouse to control the wave.
- Hold to rise, release to fall—mastering the rhythm is the key to progress.
- Once you die, check your stats (CPS, time, clicks), then press the Retry button to restart instantly.
