In **Merge Fruit**, you can match up identical grapes, lemons, oranges, and more! The well-known Suika Game served as the inspiration for this captivating physics game. 

## About Merge Fruit 

Your goal in Merge Fruit is to combine fruits to create new and larger varieties of fruit. If you enjoy the popular Suika game, Merge Fruit will not be too difficult for you. The gameplay of mixing fruits is identical to the previous game, however new edition includes more colorful and attractive fruits. Are you ready to start right now? 

### How to play

Move your mouse to change the position of the fruit drop to form a combination. When similar fruits are close together, they will naturally match and become larger fruits.
