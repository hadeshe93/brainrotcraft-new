**Pilot your starfighter in X Trench Run! Can you overcome all enemy trenches and engage in epic space battles in this thrilling trench run game?**

X Trench Run is a dramatic 3D arcade action game. You will control a spaceship through tunnels and enemy bases at high speed. The game requires skilled spaceship piloting skills and quick reflexes to dodge obstacles, shoot down targets and survive challenging matches.

### **Experience the Engaging Features**

- X Trench Run Using detailed and vivid 3D graphics, create a realistic and attractive space.
- Vivid sound effects and dramatic background music create a suspenseful and thrilling atmosphere for each match.
- Players will face many different types of enemies and obstacles, requiring quick reflexes and precise maneuvering skills.
- Complete missions and earn achievements to unlock new ships and upgrades.
- You can reset the entire game to reset your progress and record scores.

## **Control the Spaceship**

The flying ship in this game will automatically fly over the surface of a mysterious space station. You can use the WASD or arrow keys to navigate the main entity. Aircraft will only be able to come into contact with the interior space of narrow roads with a height limited by walls. To destroy dangerous laser traps, click the mouse or press the spacebar to fire.
