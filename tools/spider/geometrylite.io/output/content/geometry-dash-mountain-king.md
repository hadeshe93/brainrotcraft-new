## **Introduce Geometry Dash Mountain King**

Geometry Dash Mountain King is a [Medium Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/medium-demon) level rated 10 stars, developed by the famous creator FunnyGame. It uses the energetic soundtrack “Hall Of The Mountain King” by Brunzolaitis, perfectly matching the intense gameplay. First released in the early days of Geometry Dash custom levels, this map became famous for its clever design and creative tricks.

Although Mountain King is already a challenge, it has a sequel called Mountain Queen, which is many times harder and still underrated by many in the Geometry Dash community.

### **Features of the Game**

- Medium Demon Difficulty: Great for players looking to step up their skills.
- Unique Speed Tricks: Includes a rare speed-five ship section.
- Creative Visual Design: Strong contrast between black backgrounds and white blocks.
- Multiple Modes: Switches between cube, ship, and UFO gameplay.
