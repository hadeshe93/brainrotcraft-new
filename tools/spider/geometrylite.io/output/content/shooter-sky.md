In **Shooter Sky**, the skies are filled with diverse opponents beckoning you to join an airborne combat! Can you demonstrate your great shooting abilities by destroying all of your enemies? 

## Dive into the Chaos of Shooter Sky! 

**Shooter Sky** is an action-[pixel](https://geometrylite.io/tag/pixel) game with engaging gameplay and stunning graphics; your goal is to shoot as many adversaries as possible out of the sky. When you first start playing, there will be a short instruction on how to use the game's controls. Your character will automatically move forward; all you have to do is select the adversary you wish to destroy. The reason you can move around is because enemies will also shoot you. You must also evade their attacks or you will die. And be careful; you will not only confront one attacker, but a slew of fighter planes will emerge one after the other, making the battle more stressful and intriguing. 

### Key Features

- Pixel characters: You may unlock and play a wide range of distinctive and colorful characters, each with its charm. 
- Vibrant Visuals: The game has a retro-inspired design, pixelated visuals with modern polish, and bright aesthetics. 
- Power-Ups: The combat becomes more challenging, but don't worry too much; you can acquire power-ups to boost your strength.
