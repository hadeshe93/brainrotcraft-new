**House of Hazards** is a funny skill game that offers many interesting challenges in an apartment. Let's start the game and enjoy multiplayer mode!

## Introduce House of Hazards 

House of Hazards creates a series of challenges and perilous traps in an apartment. Your objective is to intelligently interact with the room's objects to escape the danger. Many seemingly innocuous objects, such as your kitchen cupboard, toaster, or restroom, can be hazardous and attack you at any moment. As you progress through the game, you will be able to unlock new and exciting situations and characters. 

### Controls

**Player 1:**

- Use A/D to Move
- Use W to Jump
- Use S to Crouch or Activate hazard

**Player 2:**

- Use J/L to Move
- Use I to Jump
- Use K to Crouch or Activate hazard
