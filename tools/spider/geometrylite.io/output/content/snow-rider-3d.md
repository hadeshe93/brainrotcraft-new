## Start your snowboarding journey in Snow Rider 3D!

Snow Rider 3D invites you to enter the race with its breathtaking environment and snowy hills! Winter is coming, and there's a festive vibe all around. Now's your chance to participate in a unique snowboarder racing game. The objective is to guide a snowboarder down a snow-covered mountain while dodging obstacles and earning points. It's important to keep in mind that you can unlock new sleighs by collecting gifts along the journey.

### Controls

- Use the Left Arrow key to move the sled left.
- Use the Right Arrow key to move the sled right.
- Press the Up arrow key to jump your sleigh over obstacles like rocks.

### Advice on how to play

Since every track in Snow Rider 3D presents a different set of obstacles, you must practice on every track to become familiar with the layout and spot tough spots. Save the boost for important occasions, such as when you're [jumping](https://geometrylite.io/tag/jumping) to give yourself an advantage over rivals and utilize it wisely.
