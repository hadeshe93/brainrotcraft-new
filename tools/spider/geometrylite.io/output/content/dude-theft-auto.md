## **Step Inside the Crazy Streets of Dude Theft Auto**

In Dude Theft Auto, you roam a hilarious open world powered by ragdoll [physics](https://geometrylite.io/tag/physics) that make even the simplest actions entertaining. From escaping relentless police chases through twisty streets and alleys to pulling off daring bank robberies without getting caught—and even driving recklessly at extreme speeds through crowded roads—this game lets your imagination run wild. Every turn brings a new opportunity, and with physics that send NPCs flying and cars flipping, you'll never know what funny chaos awaits.

### **Controls (PC)**

- Move: Use WASD or the arrow keys
- Jump/Climb: Press Spacebar
- Look/Aim/Shoot: Move the mouse to aim, click to attack or interact
- Interact: Use mouse buttons for picking up and using items
