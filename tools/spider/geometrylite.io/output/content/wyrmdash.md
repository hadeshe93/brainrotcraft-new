## **Wyrmdash: Core Gameplay Overview**

Welcome to the action-packed game Wyrmdash! Control a strong princess and her dragon in a fierce race across treacherous terrain. You must master the hills to gain velocity, make accurate aerial attacks, and use your dragon's flaming breath to destroy foes and barriers ahead. Your [survival](https://geometrylite.io/tag/survival) depends on a combination of speed, ability, and planning.

### **Features**

- Fast and tricky: Expect fast motions and difficult jumps, and flights.
- Strategic Challenge: Use speed, timing, and skill upgrades to dominate each stage and conquer difficult barriers.
- Dynamic Terrain Interaction: Control hills and slopes to gain speed and launch powerful aerial attacks.
