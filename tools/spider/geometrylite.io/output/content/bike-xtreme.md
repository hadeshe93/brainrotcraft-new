**Bike Xtreme** is an exhilarating mountain riding game that tests your abilities on the roughest terrains. Get ready for an exhilarating ride!

## What is Bike Xtreme?

In Bike Xtreme, you'll be racing down steep slopes, performing dangerous stunts, and testing your balance. This is an off-road cycling game with realistic mechanics, dynamic settings, and fast downhill action. You must guide your [bike](https://geometrylite.io/tag/bike) through rocky terrain, steep hills, and difficult obstacles while maintaining speed and balance. The goal is to maintain balance, as even minor errors can lead your automobile to overturn.

### How to play

- To accelerate: Press the W or up arrow keys.
- To move backward: Use the S or down arrow.
- To tilt: Press the AD key or the left/right arrows.
