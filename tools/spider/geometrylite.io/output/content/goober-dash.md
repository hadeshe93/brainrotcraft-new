In **Goober Dash**, you'll play as Goober, a lovable yet agile character tasked with navigating a variety of colorful, obstacle-filled courses.

## Introduce Goober Dash

Goober Dash is a fast-paced, rhythm-based platforming action game similar to Geometry Dash. However, in this version, you will accompany the cute new character Goober. Winterpixel Games produced this game, which quickly gained popularity because of its tough and fast-paced action. Your goal is to immerse yourself in a brilliant and innovative maze, controlling Goober to conquer all obstacles and reach the finish line safely. This is a test of your reflexes and agility! Begin by completing a sequence of increasingly harder stages.

### How to play

- To run, press the arrow keys or the A/D keys.
- To jump, simply press the up arrow. 
- Press the spacebar to activate the booster.
- Run to the finish line! Watch out for spikes!
