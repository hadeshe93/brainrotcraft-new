## **About Golf Hit**

Golf Hit is an exciting sports simulator developed by 1Games. Your mission is to aim and launch the golf ball so high that it might even soar past the clouds and land on Mars. The mix of fun [physics](https://geometrylite.io/tag/physics), easy controls, and exciting upgrades makes it perfect for kids and anyone who enjoys quick, joyful challenges.

### **Swing into Action**

In Golf Hit, one click can change everything. Press the left mouse button to send the golf ball flying through the air, then watch it travel higher and farther with each attempt.

While it soars, collect coins that glitter in the sky. These coins let you upgrade your club and power, helping you achieve even greater distances. Each upgrade brings more excitement as you chase new personal bests.
