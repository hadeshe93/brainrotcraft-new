**Geometry Dash Lunar Ocean** is a fan-favorite [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard)\-rated level designed by PM Gelt that serves as the third and final installment in the Electro Pack. Despite its [4 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/4-stars) rating, many players find it more difficult than anticipated, with some claiming it warrants an Insane (8-star) rating.

## **Overview of Geometry Dash Lunar Ocean**

Geometry Dash Lunar Ocean, available as part of the [Electro Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/electro-pack), brings you a visually fascinating and rhythmically demanding encounter. The level has a range of gameplay aspects, such as cube, ship, UFO, wave, and ball parts, each of which presents a distinct challenge that tests players' timing and reflexes. The level had three user coins at 15%, 65%, and 98% progress points. Remember these locations to gather all three usercoins and complete this level 100%.

### **The Sound of the Sea: Music in Lunar Ocean**

One of Geometry Dash Lunar Ocean's noteworthy features is its ambient music, "Solar Rain" by Geoplex. The track's smooth yet energetic beats complement the pace and tone of the level, heightening the sense of movement and immersion. Geoplex is well-known among the Geometry Dash community for creating melodic, dramatic electronic music that combines emotion and energy. In Lunar Ocean, "Solar Rain" sets the tone for a trip that seems both expansive and rhythmic, like surfing through a digital galaxy.
