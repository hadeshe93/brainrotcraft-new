## About A Small World Cup: A Soccer Game for Two Players 

**A Small World Cup** promises to offer you the best matches from one of the most important events in the world, the FIFA World Cup. This is a game that soccer lovers should not miss. One of the most memorable aspects of A Small World Cup is the surprise that [Ragdoll](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ragdoll) players bring to the pitch. Choose your favorite team and game mode, and start scoring goals right away!

### Gameplay Details

Start this game by selecting one of several teams, each represented by a unique character. Your team is made up of simply a rag-doll, which you must slam against the ball to score a goal. There are numerous game modes, including Practise, World Cup, and Golden Goal, but the main goal is to score goals against your opponent. 

**Controls**

To aim, drag your mouse and left-click, then release to shoot the ragdoll.
