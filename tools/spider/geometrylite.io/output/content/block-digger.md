Welcome to the **Block Digger**! This is a new clicker game with addicting gameplay and progressive mechanisms in which you click on blocks to reveal hidden resources.

## Embark on a Mining Adventure: Overview of Block Digger 

In Block Digger, you play as a miner who uses a digging machine to excavate as deep as possible through layers of colored blocks. Each shattered block produces coins and, on occasion, valuable jewels that can be utilized to improve the digging machine's powers. The primary goal is to delve deeper, discover more valuable resources, and earn higher scores. Upgrade your mining efficiency as you progress through the game!

### Features

- Progressive Mining Gameplay: Dig deeper to find precious resources and rare gems.
- Upgradeable Equipment: Improve your digging machine for greater efficiency and speed. Unlock strong tools and power-ups to get past tougher obstacles. 
- Resource Management: Collect cash and gems to spend on better improvements.
