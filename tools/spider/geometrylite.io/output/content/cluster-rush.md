## Cluster Rush: A Fast-paced Platform Game

Cluster Rush is a 3D platformer that requires you to jump from [truck](https://geometrylite.io/tag/truck) to truck while sliding and hanging on to avoid falling. This game involves exact timing and quick reactions to finish the demanding task. Start the game now and go through 35 difficulty levels that will put your concentration to the test. Always stay focused to react quickly to unanticipated obstacles such as falling crates, startling blasts, and erratic vehicle movements.

### Controls

- To go left or right, use the Left/Right arrow keys or A/D.
- To jump, press the space bar or J.
- To climb, hold down the space bar or press J.
