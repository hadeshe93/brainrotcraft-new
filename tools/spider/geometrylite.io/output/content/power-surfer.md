## Explore a Thrilling Adventure in Power Surfer! 

Power Surfer invites you to embark on an incredible hoverboard adventure! The primary character in this game is a robot who travels on a hoverboard. Your job is to join him, overcome roadblocks, and gain bonus points. You'll race through both levels and limitless challenges, with each level introducing new perils and obstacles to dodge. 

### Tips to play

- To navigate this difficult desert landscape, you must learn to master the controls. Simply click and move your mouse to help our robot overcome hurdles.
- Don't forget to gather batteries, as they are required to proceed through stages.
