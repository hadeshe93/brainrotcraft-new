## **What is FNF (Friday Night Funkin')?**

In the rhythm game [FNF](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fnf), you fight through catchy tunes while coordinating your arrow key movements with the beat. By defeating other characters in rap battles, you, as the boyfriend, aim to win over the girlfriend. Fan-made mods that add more songs, characters, and challenges are becoming more and more popular, and FNF is renowned for its groovy [music](https://geometrylite.io/tag/music) and stunning animations.

FNF Unblocked is an online version of [Friday Night Funkin](https://geometrylite.io/friday-night-funkin)' that may be played in a web browser, even on networks where games are often restricted. It's ideal for brief gaming breaks at school or work without the need to install anything.

### **How to play**

- Choose your week or mod.
- Press the arrow keys (↑ ← ↓ →) at the right time to match the music.
- Don’t miss too many notes or you’ll lose the battle!
