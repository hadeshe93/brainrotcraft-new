**Geometry Dash Jumper** is Geometry Dash's seventh level. Let's start playing right now and experiment with a more advanced rhythm and jump style at a [Harder](https://geometrylite.io/tag/harder) level.

## **Geometry Dash Jumper Overview**

Geometry Dash Jumper is a turning point in the Geometry Dash main levels. It teaches players how to adapt to new mechanics and handle more aggressive jump sequences. You’ll face quicker jumps, tighter spaces, and the introduction of bounce pads, which launch you high into the air and add new timing elements to each run. Explore new obstacles and mechanics that increase the challenge!

### **“Jumper” - The Oldest Soundtrack in Geometry Dash**

The level’s music track, “Jumper” by Waterflame, which has been very popular in the community, is the oldest song featured in Geometry Dash. Released in 2005, it was also used in the popular game Castle Crashers, giving Jumper a nostalgic touch for longtime gamers.
