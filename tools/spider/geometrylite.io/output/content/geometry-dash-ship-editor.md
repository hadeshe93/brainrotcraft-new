## **Geometry Dash: Ship Editor – Create, Fly, and Conquer Your Levels**

Geometry Dash: Ship Editor is about more than just beating levels; you can also create them. This one-of-a-kind mode puts you in command of a ship while also allowing you to create your own obstacles. Whether you want a smooth novice course or an outrageous [obstacle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacle) gauntlet, the options are limitless.

### **Features That Let You Shape the Challenge**

- The Level Editor Tool is the heart of this mode, allowing you to:
- Build levels of any difficulty—easy, tricky, crazy, or downright impossible.
- Arrange obstacles, platforms, and hazards to test precision flying skills.
- Customize visuals to give each stage your personal style.

By experimenting with different layouts, you may construct levels that sharpen your reflexes while keeping the gameplay interesting.
