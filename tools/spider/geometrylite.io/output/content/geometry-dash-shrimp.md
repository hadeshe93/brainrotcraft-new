Welcome to **Geometry Dash Shrimp**! This is a fast-paced rhythm-based platform game in which you navigate a neon-lit underwater landscape filled with many obstacles.

## Introduce the Gameplay of Geometry Dash Shrimp

Geometry Dash Shrimp is a [Harder](https://geometrylite.io/tag/harder) level in the popular rhythm-based platformer Geometry Dash. This level, created by [Schady](https://geometrylite.io/tag/schady), [Adiale](https://geometrylite.io/tag/adiale), and [HighScore47](https://geometrylite.io/tag/highscore47), is noted for its amazing gameplay and mashup style. Immerse yourself in the brilliant neon lights of Geometry Dash Shrimp and begin your quest to master the beat right now!

### More Detailed Information

- Shrimp was published by Schady on November 12th, 2016.
- Shrimp is the fourth level of the [Poison Gauntlet](https://geometrylite.io/tag/poison-gauntlet), following Ground Zero but preceding Generation Retro.
- The game has 11,622 objects and lasts 1 minute and 5 seconds.
