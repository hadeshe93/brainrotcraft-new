In the racing game **Snow Road**, you must navigate your sled across a snow-covered mountain while avoiding obstacles to complete the longest run possible. 

## Introduce the Gameplay of Snow Road 

With this game, you steer a sled down a mountain covered with snow while negotiating hills. It is your task to steer the vehicle deftly around uneven terrain, rocky terrain, and other hazards. You'll need lightning-fast reflexes and complete control over all of your sled's controls to pull it out. As you advance in the game, try to acquire further features and sleds with more striking characteristics and looks.

### Controls 

- To move, use WASD or the arrow keys.
- To leap, use the Spacebar.

## Key Features

- Simple Controls: Simple controls make it easy to ski and jump when skiing, even for beginners.
- Addictive gameplay: Playing a challenging game that puts your driving prowess and lightning-fast reflexes to the test will make it addictive. 
- Dynamic Environment: As you drive, you'll get to see a variety of snowy scenes with hills, trees, and obstacles that shift with your route.
- Many Obstacles: Your run may abruptly come to an end due to obstacles like trees and rocks.
