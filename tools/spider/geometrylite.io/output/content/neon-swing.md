In the action game **Neon Swing**, you take control of a nimble stickman who swings from point to point through a vibrant neon environment.

## Start A Thrilling Arcade Adventure in Neon Swing! 

Enter the vivid neon world of Neon Swing and prepare for an exciting arcade adventure over a series of hard levels. The player's task is to demonstrate his agility and dexterity by swinging from point to point till he reaches the destination. Remember to avoid obstacles and the inexorable red wall that is closing in from behind! As you go through the stages, you will face additional challenges such as faster-moving obstacles and more intricate swinging sequences. Start your trip now!

### Controls

Use your mouse to launch a rope and swing across different platforms.
