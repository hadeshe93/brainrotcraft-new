**Agar.io** is a multiplayer .io game in which you control a cell that consumes smaller cells to grow and escape being devoured by larger ones. 

## Welcome to The Ultimate Cell-Eating Challenge in Agar.io!

Your aim in **Agar.io** is to eat while avoiding being eaten by larger opponents! This is a humorous yet highly competitive multiplayer game in which every move necessitates strategy and calculation to stay alive. Agar.io's popularity can be attributed to its simple rules: Eat smaller circles to expand while avoiding larger players. Let's join hundreds of gamers in the online arena and test your skills right now! 

## How to play

Register to begin the game and select whether to play logged in or not. After you enter the game, you will resemble a tiny dot that is comparable to other opponents. You will grow larger if you consume your opponents, who will be tiny dots. On the other hand, players who are larger than you will try to feed you. If you are too huge, you will burst into numerous time balls, but if you are small enough to fit within, nothing will happen. Keep in mind that this arena contains green, spikey balls.
