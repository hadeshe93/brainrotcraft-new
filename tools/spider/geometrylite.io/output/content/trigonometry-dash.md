## **Trigonometry Dash – A Fan-Made Geometry Dash Experience with a Creative Twist**

Trigonometry Dash is a [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced), fan-made platformer inspired by the iconic Geometry Dash. Created by maji, this game brings back the adrenaline-pumping gameplay you love, along with something extra: a powerful level editor that lets you design your own custom challenges!

### **What is Trigonometry Dash?**

Just like its inspiration, Trigonometry Dash tests your reflexes with nonstop action, tight jumps, and tricky patterns. But it doesn’t stop there. This fan-made version shines by giving players access to a level editor, allowing the community to create and share custom stages packed with unique layouts and difficulty spikes.

### **Master the Controls**

- W / SPACE / UP ARROW – Jump, Boost, Swap, or Flap
- A / LEFT and D / RIGHT – Move horizontally
- S – Dive or move downward (used in [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) mode)
- R – Instantly return to the last checkpoint
- ESC – Pause the game anytime
