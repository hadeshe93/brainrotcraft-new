In the management game Monkey Mart, you take on the role of entertaining monkeys who must learn how to construct and operate each store. 

## Learn how to Manage your shop in Monkey Mart 

Monkey Mart is a simulation game with engaging and innovative gameplay that mixes shop management and resource [management](https://geometrylite.io/tag/management). The game's main protagonists are cheery and hard-working monkeys. Your responsibilities include arranging agricultural items on display shelves, selling goods, and performing cashier duties. Begin the game now and demonstrate your management talents. 

### How to play

Use the arrow keys to direct the monkey through the chores assigned to him in order to operate the store.
