**Cookie Clicker Unblocked** is an addictive [idle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/idle) game in which you bake cookies by clicking on a large cookie and investing in upgrades to automate the process.

## **Overview of Cookie Clicker Unblocked**

Cookie Clicker was created by Orteil (Julien Thiennot) and released in 2013 as a browser game. It immediately became popular because of its curiously rewarding loop: click to collect cookies, spend them to buy structures, and watch your output rise. Cookie Clicker Unblocked allows you to enjoy the full addictive experience of the popular idle game directly from your school or workplace browser, with no downloads or limitations. Click infinite cookies, earn amazing upgrades, and expand your empire without fear of firewalls or filters.

### **Features**

- Click early, automate later. Focus on manual clicking at first, then invest in buildings for automation.
- Use Golden Cookies: Click golden biscuits as often as possible; they provide enormous benefits.
