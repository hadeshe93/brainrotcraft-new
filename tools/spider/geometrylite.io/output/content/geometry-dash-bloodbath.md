## Geometry Dash Bloodbath: Geometry Dash's Most Popular Level

Geometry Dash Bloodbath is a renowned and extremely difficult level that Riot hosts and publishes as a mega-collaboration among the most well-known players in the community. This level is distinguished by its furious pace and complicated patterns, which test your musical abilities to overcome hurdles. Bloodbath makes great use of the [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) game mode, which requires exact control and timing to escape the many hazards.

- Extreme Difficulty Gameplay: This is commonly regarded as one of the most difficult levels in Geometry Dash.
