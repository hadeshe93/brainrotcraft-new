In the thrilling racing game **Drift Road**, you take control of your vehicle and maneuver it across treacherous tracks and twisting roadways.

## Gameplay of Drift Road 

In Drift Road, a Halloween-themed variant of Drift, players take control of an automobile and perform drifts on a narrow elevated road. This is a fantastic chance for you to demonstrate your abilities by doing fluid drifts. Keep your focus since one mistake could send you plummeting into the chasm. As you advance through the game, a variety of cars will become available to you, adding excitement to your travels!

### Controls

- Hold your mouse to keep the car going straight
- Click to drive the right way.
