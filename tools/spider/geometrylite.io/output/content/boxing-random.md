Players can enjoy a fresh experience in **Boxing Random**, a series of random-style sports games. This is a recommendation that you should not miss if you enjoy boxing as a player! 

## About Boxing Random

Boxing Random is a sports game created by RHM Interactive, a well-known game developer that has created other titles in the [Soccer Random](https://geometrylite.io/soccer-random) and [Basket Random](https://geometrylite.io/basket-random) series. Because of its two-player option, easy controls and gameplay, and great entertainment value, this series is well-known and adored. With all of those standard elements, Boxing Random also guarantees you an amazing boxing match experience. The thrill is increased by the fact that every round brings with it new conditions, including boxers and boxing fields.

### Controls

- Player 1 (In Single Player mode): To jump, use the Up arrow key.
- Player 2: To jump, press the W key.
