**Stickman Racing** is a cool action game where you are a stickman racing through many types of terrain and obstacles to become the champion.

## Introduce Gameplay of Stickman Racing

Let's begin the epic race with [Stickman](https://geometrylite.io/tag/stickman) in Stickman Racing, run like the wind, and leave your competitors behind! Your goal is, of course, to be the first to cross the finish line. This game is full of obstacles that require agility and ability to overcome. Prepare to run, wall jump, and roll across a variety of terrains to become the first to cross the finish line! Stickman Racing has dozens of thrilling levels and challenges for you to explore, so don't miss out!

### Tips to get high scores

- Gather Coins and Boosts: Remember that collecting coins, boosters, and other in-game benefits will help you advance more quickly and raise your score.
- Pay Attention to Stunts: Flips and other tricks during leaps will increase your score.
