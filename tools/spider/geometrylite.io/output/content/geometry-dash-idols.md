**Geometry Dash Idols** is one of the most intense and cinematic levels in the game. With rapid gamemode changes and a legendary ending, it's built for true pros. 

## **What Makes Geometry Dash Idols So Legendary?**

Geometry Dash Idols is a 10-star [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) and one of Geometry Dash's most ambitious mega-collaborations. This level, hosted by Herdys, published by Zafkiel, and certified by the well-known player Dorami, highlights the talent of numerous great creators. In this level, players must swiftly switch between cube, robot, wave, ship, spider, ball, and dual modes, sometimes with only seconds to react. It's a continuous test of timing, memory, and control. 

### **Features**

- Constant Mode Switching: Jump between cube, wave, spider, robot, and more—often within seconds. 
- Tight Controls Needed: Precision is key. One wrong tap and it’s over.
- Dual Mode Chaos: Control two icons at once, with mirrored or split paths.
- High-Speed Action: Blazing-fast sections demand quick reflexes and full focus.
