Welcome to Geometry Dash End Of Line — a sleek gray world of rhythm, spikes, and secrets that close the [Beginner Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/beginner-pack) in style.

## **Geometry Dash End Of Line - Overview of the Level**

Geometry Dash End Of Line is a [Normal](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal) 3-star level created by PMK. Check out the closing challenge of the Beginner Pack! Known for its gray color scheme and inspiration from Theory of Everything, it offers a smooth mix of challenge and style while wrapping up the pack on a memorable note. Built with 7,431 objects, it feels polished and detailed without being overly complex, making it a fan-favorite among entry-level challenges.

### **Coin Hunting Secrets**

Each of the three user coins demands timing and attention:

- First coin: Hidden in a subtle path that rewards exploration.
- Second coin: Requires skipping an expected jump and dropping instead.
- Third coin: Found in a wave sequence where precision is key
