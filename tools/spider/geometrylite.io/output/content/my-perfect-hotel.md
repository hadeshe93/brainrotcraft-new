## Become a Hotel Management in My Perfect Hotel!

Create your dream hotel with My Perfect Hotel! In this simulation game, players assume the role of a hotel manager. You own a hotel with unlimited space, and your objective is to keep it running properly so that your customers are satisfied! This game promises to be a terrific entertainment experience that will allow you to demonstrate your originality and strategic thinking in business management!

### How to play

- Designing and operating your luxury hotel, including furniture selection and room layout.
- Serve customers' requirements, collect money, and continue to grow your firm.
- Hire employees, manage resources, and deal with the daily issues that arise as the number of consumers grows.
