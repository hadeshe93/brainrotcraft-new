## Geometry Dash The Lightning Road – Ride the Thunder, Dodge the Spikes!

Geometry Dash The Lightning Road is an Easy Demon, 10-star level created, verified, and published by Timeless Real. With intense music, seamless transitions, and rhythm-based platforming, this level has the intensity of a Demon difficulty while remaining approachable to beginner players. It's widely regarded as one of the most popular levels in the game, and many players think it's one of the simplest Demon-rated levels ever produced.

### More Facts about The Lightning Road

- Easy Demon Difficulty: Ideal for gamers new to Demon challenges.
- Part of Demon Pack 3 is the pack's final and most beginner-friendly entry.
- High Download Count: This is one of the most played and popular fan levels ever created.
