## **Grow a Garden: Relax, Plant, and Enjoy!**

Welcome to Grow a Garden! In this fun agricultural simulator, users may design their ideal garden by planting crops, harvesting them, and selling the output for in-game prizes. The journey from a small patch of earth to a blooming paradise is both relaxing and gratifying, ideal for gamers who value creativity and patience.

### **Simple Controls**

- WASD – Move around your garden
- Spacebar – Jump over paths or garden beds
- Mouse Click – Plant seeds, harvest crops, open your inventory, and more!
