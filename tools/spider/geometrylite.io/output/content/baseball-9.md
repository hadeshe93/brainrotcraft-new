## Introduce Gameplay of Baseball 9

Baseball 9 is a new baseball game that immerses you in the life of a professional baseball player. You will be sent to a stadium filled with cheers from the fans, and your only goal will be to lead your team to victory by shooting extremely accurate shots. To score points for your side, a professional player must be able to shoot extremely accurate shots. It's time to demonstrate your abilities in the arena, with the involvement of all professional players!

### How to play

You aim to make accurate shots, thus precision and timing are critical. Prepare to step onto the field and score points for your squad right now; remember, the goal is to earn as many points as possible.
