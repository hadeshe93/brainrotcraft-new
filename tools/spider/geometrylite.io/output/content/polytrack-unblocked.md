## **PolyTrack Unblocked – Race, Build, and Conquer Your Own Tracks**

PolyTrack Unblocked is a 3D car racing and track-building game inspired by TrackMania. Players can race on pre-made tracks or design their own using a full-featured editor. The game focuses on precision driving and creative level design, letting you test your custom tracks instantly. 

### **Gameplay**

You can switch between Racing Mode and Editor Mode at any time.

- In Racing Mode, drive at top speed, handle sharp curves, and try to complete the lap in record time.
- In Editor Mode, build your own tracks with ramps, loops, and turns. Adjust height, rotate parts, and test your layout until it’s perfect.

### **Controls Guide**

Driving:

- WASD / Arrow Keys – Drive
- Enter / R – Restart

Editor:

- Left Click – Build
- Right Click + Move – Move camera
- Middle Click + Move – Rotate camera
- Mouse Scroll – Zoom
- Shift + Scroll / ZC – Change height
- Q / E – Rotate camera
- R / Space – Rotate item
- X – Delete item
