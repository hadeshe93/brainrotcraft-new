**Geometry Dash Back On Track** is Geometry Dash's second official level, and it signals the beginning of real gameplay. With new mechanics, faster [speed](https://geometrylite.io/tag/speed), and a catchy song, it teaches players how to improve their rhythm and timing.

## **Introduce Geometry Dash Back On Track**

As the second official level in RobTop Games' addictive platformer, "Back On Track" isn't about mind-bending difficulties or elaborate designs. Following the beginning beats of "Stereo Madness," "Back On Track" continues the musical adventure with DJVI's equally catchy and uplifting track of the same title. The level's design is clean and simple, focusing on exposing essential gameplay features without overwhelming inexperienced players.

### **Mastering the Fundamentals: Jump, Fly, and Rhythm**

You'll come across typical obstacles like blocks and spikes, but their placement and timing are purposefully more forgiving, allowing beginners to learn the fundamentals of [jumping](https://geometrylite.io/tag/jumping), flying with the ship, and keeping rhythmic perfection.
