## **Geometry Dash Clubstep: A Demon-Level Leap Into Chaos**

Geometry Dash Clubstep is a famous [original](https://geometrylite.io/tag/original) level that represents a significant turning point in Geometry Dash. As the fourteenth level of the main game—and the first to contain the high Demon difficulty—this stage marks the end of casual gameplay and the beginning of genuine precision. With its crazy design, 38 demon-face obstacles, and mind-bending transitions, Clubstep is a Geometry Dash fan's rite of passage.

### **All About the Coins**

- First coin at 9%, discovered on a tough hidden path.
- Second coin at 71% - Requires flying ability and quick reflexes.
- Third coin at 96% - Hidden in the last moments, a true test under pressure.
