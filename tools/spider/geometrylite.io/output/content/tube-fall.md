## About Tube Fall 

Tube Fall is an infinite runner-style game with a charming, fluffy creature as the main character who navigates a complex network of pipes. This Endless Runner game stands out from other games due to its cute characters and changing difficulty levels. The more coins you win in each level, the more opportunities you will have to unlock a variety of adorable main characters and fascinating levels. 

### How to play

The fluffy creature will go quickly downward. Your objective is to guide the main character along the pipe by passing through holes along the route. If you hit an obstacle, the level will finish.
