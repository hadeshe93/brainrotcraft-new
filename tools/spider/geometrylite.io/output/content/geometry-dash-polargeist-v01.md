## **A Fresh Take on a Classic: Geometry Dash Polargeist v01!**

Get ready to jump into a familiar world with a brand-new look! **Geometry Dash Polargeist v01**, created by the talented Mixroid, is a super cool and updated version of the official level Polargeist. It's the very first level in the [Normal Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal-pack), so it’s the perfect place to start your next adventure! Prepare to experience a fun, fast-paced challenge with fresh visuals and exciting new gameplay!

### **A Hard Level Awaits!**

If you've played the original Polargeist, you'll feel right at home in this version, but with some awesome surprises! Polargeist v01 has been given a new life with updated visuals and gameplay, making it a new and exciting experience even if you've played the original a hundred times. This level is rated as a Hard difficulty with [4 stars](https://geometrylite.io/tag/4-stars), so it's a great test of your rhythm and timing skills. You'll need to master quick taps and perfect jumps to make it all the way to the end!
