**Geometry Vertical** is a rhythm-based platformer in which you control a geometric shape through a succession of vertically scrolling levels full of obstacles.

## Introduce Geometry Vertical

Geometry Vertical is a thrilling arcade game inspired by Geometry Dash that tests your reflexes, timing, and precision! Geometry Vertical is popular among gamers due to its fast-paced vertical movement, rhythmic action, and hard obstacles. This game elevates the classic Geometry Dash formula to new heights. Are you ready to start playing this game right now and climb your way to victory?

### How to play

- To change lanes, use the right or left mouse button. 
- Hold down the left mouse button to move right. 
- Release to move to the left.
- If you are above the hole, click the mouse to the left.
