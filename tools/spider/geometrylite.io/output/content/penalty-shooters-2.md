**Penalty Shooters 2** is a new installment in the Penalty Shooters game series, in which you can train and demonstrate your [penalty](https://geometrylite.io/tag/penalty) shooting abilities to help your team win.

## Introduce Penalty Shooters 2

Penalty Shooters 2 is a new penalty game version that promises to include new leagues, teams, and more excitement. This game becomes more tough and intriguing when you play as both a striker and a goalkeeper. This simple but appealing concept makes Penalty Shooters 2 one of the most popular games in the Soccer Games collection.

### How to play

- Choose your favorite club and strive to win each match as you advance through your league. 
- Aim the ball and kick to score goals, or move your goalkeeper to prevent balls from entering the net.
