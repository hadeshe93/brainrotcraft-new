**Stacky Dash** is a matching adventure arcade game with 3D models where you need to control the character to collect enough bricks and arrive at the final platform. 

## Introduce Stacky Dash

Stacky Dash is a simple but addictive game as you perform the task of moving through a maze of colored blocks. The main character of the game is an agile [stickman](https://geometrylite.io/tag/stickman). You will control the character through obstacles and platforms, collecting bricks along the way. The game has 30 levels with increasing complexity, challenging your wits. Simple gameplay pairs well with its challenging mazes of Stacky Dash that will keep you hooked for hours!

## How to play 

Your task is clear: Collect all the pieces you can to build bridges to get as far as you can. This is a test about controlling your time moves to collect as many bricks as possible. You can choose flawlessly repeated routes across the platforms to ensure a higher score. In case you have an insufficient number of tiles, you can always go back to the previous maze to get more or restart the entire level. Remember that the more bricks you collect, the more diamonds you earn! 

### Controls

Use your mouse or arrow keys to control your character.
