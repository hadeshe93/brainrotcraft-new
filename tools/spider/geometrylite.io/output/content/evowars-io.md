## Introduce EvoWars.io

EvoWars.io is an action game where you fight other players to become the most powerful warrior in the Deathmatch arena. Your goal is to defeat as many foes as can (beginning with little opponents) and collect orbs to grow larger. The combat will become more intense, so swiftly expand your size to obtain an advantage. 

## How to play

Select your username and game mode to begin entering the arena. With a bludgeon, you will begin as a little, agile warrior whose goal is to kill other opponents while surviving. Due to the vast number of opponents who are prepared to destroy you, the struggle will not be easy at first. To rapidly grow in size, the best course of action is to stay away from the bigger players and hit the smaller targets with accuracy.

### Become a bigger warrior in the arena

Bonus points will be awarded to your warrior after every round. Get as many bonus points as you can to assist your warrior develop into a powerful individual in this field. Additionally, you can improve your weaponry to become more up-to-date, which will make you more successful against the competitors.
