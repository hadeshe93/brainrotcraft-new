## Introduce Ragdoll Hit: A chaotic physics-based fighting game

Ragdoll Hit has unique [ragdoll](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ragdoll) dynamics that make every punch, kick, and strike delightfully unpredictable, providing a balance of strategy and pure enjoyment. I will be pleased to assist you with this ragdoll before you realize it. It's time to go forward from here. There are other settings available, as well as additional weaponry that you can utilize. As you progress through the game, you'll obtain more powerful weaponry, giving you an advantage over your opponents.

### Controls

- To hit and move, press A, D, or the left and right arrow keys.
- Use the spacebar to pick up or hold items. 
- Press W or the up arrow key to jump.
- Press S or the down arrow key to kick.
