**Geometry Dash Nemesis** takes the classic rhythm-platforming excitement and flips it on its head. Instead of merely avoiding obstacles, you now have to destroy them while keeping your timing perfect.

## **Geometry Dash Nemesis Overview**

Geometry Dash Nemesis is a fan-created version of the renowned [Geometry Dash series](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games) that takes the rhythm-platforming genre in a new direction. The game was designed to be played immediately in your web browser and is compatible with both PC and mobile devices, allowing players to join at any time and from any location. It combines the usual [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced), beat-synced gameplay with new action mechanics that make each run unexpected.

### **Features**

- Two-Mission Gameplay: Master both jumping and shooting to survive.
- Weapon Mechanics: Blast obstacles out of your path while staying on beat.
- Rhythmic Challenge: Stay in sync with the music while juggling multiple actions.
