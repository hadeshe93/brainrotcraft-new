**Geometry Dash Sonic Wave** is one of Geometry Dash's most famous [Extreme Demon](https://geometrylite.io/tag/extreme-demon) levels. This level, originally built by Cyclic, is a Nine Circles recreation that tested the limits of difficulty when it was first released.

## Geometry Dash Sonic Wave's Gameplay

Geometry Dash Sonic Wave is recognized for its strong [wave](https://geometrylite.io/tag/wave) sequences, which necessitate tremendous precision, rapid reflexes, and impeccable timing. The level's unusual blue color scheme distinguishes it from other Nine Circles levels. To successfully complete the level, you must navigate tight wave corridors, intricate ship parts, and hard timing-based obstacles.
