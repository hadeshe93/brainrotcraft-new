## REPO - The Original: Team Up, Gear Up, and Discover!

REPO - The Original is an online co-op horror game that features immersive physics, horrifying monsters, and real-time team communication. Up to six players can dive into pitch-black, fear-filled landscapes with one purpose in mind: acquire precious items and escape before it's too late. Every round is jam-packed with surprise thrills, teamwork challenges, and unpredictable monster behavior, resulting in a new horror experience each time you play.

### Key Features

- Online Cooperative (Up to 6 Players): Team up with pals to face the horrors together, emphasizing communication and shared dread.
-  Physics-Based Grabbing Tool:  Interact with your surroundings in a realistic and dynamic way to manipulate objects for puzzle solving and extraction.
-  Proximity Voice Chat: Immerse yourself in the terrifying atmosphere by only hearing colleagues who are close by, heightening tension and realism.
