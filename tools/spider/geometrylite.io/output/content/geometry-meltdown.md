## **Introduce Geometry Meltdown**

Geometry Meltdown will ignite your enthusiasm with an addictive adventure game and unprecedented exploration. You will participate in a journey through dark caves with dangerous lava flows. At the same time, hideous monsters are always ready to devour you at any time. A small cube with super-jumping ability will accompany you in this challenge. Be ready for the most timely movements to overcome all dangerous obstacles. Furthermore, gamers need to react quickly when maneuvering each time the character changes form.

### Enjoy eye-catching images and vibrant sounds!

There is nothing to discuss about the eye-catching images and vibrant sounds in Geometry Meltdown. This is also a unique feature that makes your rounds more impressive than ever. There's nothing more exciting than dancing to exciting songs and overcoming dangers spectacularly. Align the most accurate key press timing, and your cube will take care of reaching the finish line. This is definitely a game not to be missed for those who are passionate about the classic gameplay of the famous game Geometry Dash. Get ready to refresh your experiences with terrain that is both familiar and unique!

## **How to play**

There are two ways for gamers to control the cube: click the mouse button or press the spacebar. The two buttons have identical functions, so you can choose according to your habits. As long as it can help you reach the finish line in the safest way. Don't forget to take advantage of the rhythm motion feature for more effective operations.
