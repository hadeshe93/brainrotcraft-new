**Geometry Dash ColorZ** blends a clean design with rhythmic gameplay to create one of the [Force Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/force-pack)'s most visually appealing levels. Created by Xtobe5, it combines brilliant rainbow hues, fluid pace, and a powerful soundtrack to provide players with a unique challenge.

## **Geometry Dash ColorZ - A Force Pack Highlight**

Geometry Dash ColorZ, the second level in the Force Pack, stands out due to its refined design and balanced difficulty. It's rated [Normal](https://geometrylite.io/tag/normal) 3 stars, indicating that it's approachable for players but still requires precise leaps and timing. The contrast gives each area a glowing look, increasing the visibility of obstacles and generating a distinct visual rhythm. With approximately 22,463 items in the design, Xtobe5 made sure that every aspect contributed to the game's seamless, engaging flow.

### **Music and Sync**

The music, Streetlights by Envy/EnV, is a favorite choice among Geometry Dash developers due to its energetic but ambient pulse. The jumps, pads, and portals are in perfect time with the music, giving the impression that the level is more than just a challenge—it's a performance in rhythm and responsiveness.
