**Race Survival: Arena Kin**g puts you in a fast-paced battleground where you must demonstrate your speed in order to become the arena's ruler!

## What is Race Survival: Arena King? 

Race Survival: Arena King is designed for adrenaline seekers and demolition derby lovers alike, combining high-speed driving with cruel survival mechanisms. This is the most recent [Driving Games](https://geometrylite.io/driving.games) update in our collection. Instead of typical laps and finish lines, the goal is straightforward: stay alive and be the last car standing. It's time to drive through large open arenas full of traps, hazards, and other racers willing to do whatever to defeat you.

### How to play

- To drive your car, use the A/D or left/right arrow keys.
- To jump, press W/Spacebar or the Arrow Keys.
