## **Face the Challenge: Make Every Move Count**

Geometry Dash: Dangerous Choices brings you into a frenetic rhythm-based universe where survival requires rapid judgments. With tight obstacles and quick character transformations, this fan-made twist provides pulse-pounding gameplay that tests your reflexes to the limit.

### **Features That Raise the Stakes**

- Multiple characters: Switch instantly between Wave, Spider, and Spaceship forms.
- Dynamic transitions: Choose the right form for upcoming challenges – one wrong pick can end your run.
- Fast-paced levels: Each level is designed to keep players guessing with frequent twists and deadly traps

### **How to play**

Start by selecting a level. Tap the screen or press the spacebar to begin movement. At any point, press 1, 2, or 3 (or use the on-screen icons) to switch between characters.

**Each form has a unique movement:**

- Wave zips in a zigzag line,
- Spider teleports between surfaces,
- Spaceship glides smoothly with gravity-based control.
