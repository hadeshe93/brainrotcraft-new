A full-level, free version of the well-known Geometry Dash game, **Geometry Dash Scratch** was created by Griffpatch specifically for the Scratch platform.

## About Geometry Dash Scratch

Welcome to Geometry Dash, the popular platform game, in its new Scratch version! If you enjoy creating new games, you are probably familiar with the Scratch platform. In this Scratch version, you can guide a square through an intriguing and demanding musical trip. The entire version and completely free levels are ready for you to explore. Prepare to start right away!

### How to play

Like in other [Geometry Games](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-games.games), you control a geometric block to hop across various obstacles to the rhythm. You may create your character before beginning the levels in this game!
