## About Dinosaur Game

Dinosaur Game is a well-known classic with basic gameplay that is ideal for those seeking light enjoyment. This game, sometimes known as Chrome Dino or T-Rex Runner, was released in 2014 to address an annoying issue: the dreaded "No Internet" page. The game became viral as it took players on a thrilling dinosaur escape. This unending adventure promises to provide you with an unforgettable entertainment experience! 

### Why is the Dinosaur Game So Addictive? 

Google's game has become an addictive game for gamers of all ages, with incredibly fascinating gameplay. A dinosaur named T-Rex comes to life and goes on an everlasting trek through a desert riddled with challenges. You can start the game right away with just one simple control. However, getting your name on the leaderboard is not easy; it demands attention and lightning-fast reflexes. This also draws players to Dinosaur Game's gameplay, as they want to prove their abilities and climb the global leaderboard. 

## How to play

The dinosaur's purpose is to avoid obstacles and live as long as possible. Dinosaur will aggressively sprint forward and, therefore make accurate leaping judgments to help him overcome obstacles.
