Triangular Jump is a dramatic and challenging [arcade games](https://geometrylite.io/arcade.games). Lost in the familiar geometric map of the [Geometry Dash](https://geometrylite.io/) universe, what mission will you have? No more breathtaking journeys through complex platforms or dodging obstacles. In this creative game, you will control the sharp spikes in the terrain to puncture the endlessly dancing cubes. Target entities will move in a certain direction. Quickly grasp that direction of movement to create eye-catching collisions.

### **Discover the Novel Features**

- Graphics and features are typical of famous platform games.
- Completely innovative gameplay when players need to move obstacles.
- The new goal is that you are not allowed to ignore over three cubes.
- More levels will open one by one as you complete the mission.
- The lively sound of each impact makes the spirit more excited.

## **How to Play Triangular Jump**

All the triangle blocks in Triangular Jump are your weapons. Quickly observe the location of these shapes on the map to come up with an effective strategy. You just need to click on the pointed block to make it jump up. Because you can only jump to a certain extent, you need to time each shot accurately to destroy the target. The pace of the round will be fast-paced, so you will definitely need many tries to win.
