**Moto X3M Spooky Land** is part of the well-known [Moto X3M series](https://geometrylite.io/tag/moto-x3m), which is recognized for its physics-based motorcycle racing and adrenaline-pumping stunts.

## Moto X3M Spooky Land: A Motocross Madness with a Spooky Twist

Moto X3M Spooky Land takes place against the backdrop of frightening, ghostly landscapes. This is new to the gameplay of this version. This level adds a fresh twist to traditional gameplay by immersing players in a realm full of ghostly images and spine-tingling aural effects. You will have to drive the car through challenging terrain on allegedly ghostly roadways! Enjoy the thrills on each track and begin right now!

### How to play

- Use the W key to accelerate.
- To brake, press S.
- To elevate the front wheel, press A.
- Use the D key to raise the back wheel.
