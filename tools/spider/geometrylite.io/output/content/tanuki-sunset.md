**Tanuki Sunset** is a vivid and relaxing third-person longboarding game in which you play as Tanuki and embark on a skateboarding journey across diverse settings.

## Gameplay of Tanuki Sunset 

Tanuki Sunset immerses you in the beautiful, [colorful](https://geometrylite.io/tag/colorful) world of dusk while also providing high-speed racing opportunities! You will play a cool raccoon who dreams of being on the cover of FISH, Sunset Island's #1 longboarding magazine. In this sleek arcade experience, all you have to do is master your longboard, drift around scenic locations, and do awesome feats. Don't forget to gather "Tanuki Bits" sprinkled along the road, which are essential for keeping your furry friend fed and upgrading your gear at Bob's Skate Shop.

### Controls

- To move, use A/D or the left/right arrow keys.
- To enter the fast stance, use the W or up arrow.
- Use the S or down arrow to do a 180-degree slide.
- Use the space bar to wander.
- Press Esc to pause.
