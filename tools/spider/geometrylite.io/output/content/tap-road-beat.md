## **Tap Road Beat – A Rhythmic Space Rush**

Tap Road Beat is a vibrant, high-energy platform game inspired by the hit [Tap Road](https://geometrylite.io/tap-road) series and spiced up with Geometry Dash-style intensity. It’s a thrilling blend of music and motion where players control a blazing ball across twin-lane tracks filled with fast-moving hazards. As an endless runner, the pace ramps up with every beat, demanding laser focus and quick reaction times. Your goal? Stay alive, keep switching lanes, and vibe your way through dazzling space worlds.

### **How to Play**

- Tap or click to guide your ball through a dual-lane system.
- Obstacles appear in sync with the [music](https://geometrylite.io/tag/music), so timing is everything.
- Anticipate quick switches, gaps, and glowing traps.
- The farther you go, the more intense the music and [speed](https://geometrylite.io/tag/speed) become.
