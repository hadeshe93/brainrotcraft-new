**Geometry Dash Clutterfunk** was created by RobTop Games and included in the initial release of Geometry Dash. As a [Insane](https://geometrylite.io/tag/insane) difficulty (ranked 12 stars), it adds new gameplay mechanics like micro mode and increased portal complexity, signalling a significant departure from previous levels.

## **Dive Into the Chaos of Geometry Dash Clutterfunk**

Geometry Dash Clutterfunk is the 11th level in the core Geometry Dash series, and it represents a significant shift in difficulty and complexity. This level is notable for its fast pace, quick changes, and hard gravity alterations. Clutterfunk requires players to stay sharp and adjust rapidly as the [rhythm](https://geometrylite.io/rhythm.games) picks up and the obstructions become tighter.

### **Designs**

Set to an energetic track by Waterflame, Clutterfunk has a striking, glitchy visual design and incorporates new mechanics such as mini-portals and frequent mode transitions, including cube, spacecraft, and UFO portions. Its tiny spaces and variable rhythm patterns necessitate exact timing and serious muscle memory, making it a fan favourite among players seeking a true challenge.
