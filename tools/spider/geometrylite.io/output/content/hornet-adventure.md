## Hornet Adventure – Step Into the Shadows of Hallownest

Hornet Adventure invites you into a hauntingly beautiful world inspired by the Hollow Knight universe. You’ll play as Hornet, the swift and elegant guardian of Hallownest, battling through dark caverns and ruined kingdoms filled with danger. This fan-made action-adventure game captures the eerie charm and [fast-paced](https://geometrylite.io/tag/fast-paced) combat that made the original beloved — but adds its own unique twists and challenges.

### How to Play

- Move: A / D
- Jump: Spacebar
- Double Jump: Press Space twice
- Attack / Shoot: F

### Why You Should Play This Game?

If you love Hollow Knight’s mix of graceful combat and dark storytelling, Hornet Adventure is a must-play. It offers that same pulse-pounding challenge with fresh mechanics, deeper movement, and new worlds to explore. Whether you’re chasing perfection in battle or simply soaking in the eerie atmosphere, this game is both a tribute and an evolution — a true love letter to fans of action-platformers.
