## Journey to conquer rhythm in Geometry Dash Rocket

Geometry Dash Rocket is a unique version of the Geometry Dash game in which you must use your lightning-fast reflexes to master the beat! The main character in this game is Rocket, and your job is to guide Rocket through obstacles while following the beat to safely reach the finish line. Are you certain that you can complete this difficult journey? 

### Controls

To Jump: Use W, UP ARROW, LEFT-CLICK, or SPACE.
