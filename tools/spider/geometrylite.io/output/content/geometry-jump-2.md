## **Geometry Jump 2 – A Neon Leap into Chaos**

In the latest version of the beloved [Geometry Jump](https://geometrylite.io/geometry-jump) series, Geometry Jump 2 expands the experience with 15 brand-new levels, packed with fast-paced action and tricky sequences. The game sticks to its iconic minimalist geometric design but amplifies it with bright neon visuals and pulse-pounding music. From cube spikes to impossible jumps, this game will test your timing and focus in every second of play. It’s more than just jumping—it's memorizing patterns, reacting instantly, and surviving the madness!

### **How to Play**

- Spacebar or Left Mouse Button to jump
- F key (Plant Emblem) can be used to perform special moves (if available in a level)
- Use the cursor to navigate menus and select levels

Your goal is simple—jump over every obstacle and make it to the end. But with such tight timing and fast transitions, even one mistake means starting over.
