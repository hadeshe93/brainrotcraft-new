Welcome to **Ball Surfer 3D**, an amazing arcade game where you have to control a rolling ball to get past many obstacles in 3D surroundings. 

## Get Ready to Roll in Ball Surfer 3D! 

The [endless runner](https://geometrylite.io/tag/endless-runner) game Ball Surfer 3D takes you to a colorful setting with lively background music. You have to steer a ball down an endless incline. You must have lightning-fast reflexes to avoid gaps, spikes, and shifting platforms in this game. Remember to collect goods that impart transient skills, like speed increases. You can also unlock other ball designs by collecting a lot of awards.

### Tips to play

Although there is an infinite slope ahead, you need to keep an eye on the layout so you can plan your route and anticipate problems. Obstacles in your way shouldn't surprise or frighten you too much. Remaining composed will help you control the ball's pace and direction.

### Controls

Using the left and right arrow keys, move your ball left or right to guide it.
