## **Introduce 8 Ball Pool**

8 Ball Pool is a competitive online billiards game that recreates the classic [sports](https://geometrylite.io/sports.games) game on your screen. 8 Ball Pool online, developed by Miniclip, is one of the world's most popular multiplayer pool games. This game features genuine cue sports gameplay in which players take turns pocketing balls using regular 8-ball regulations. The first player to legally sink all of their balls (solids or stripes), followed by the 8-ball, wins the match.

### **Pro Tips** 

- Use the recommendations to arrange your shots correctly.
- Don't rush—take your time with targeting and plan.
- Learn to use spin to control the location of the cue ball after each shot.
