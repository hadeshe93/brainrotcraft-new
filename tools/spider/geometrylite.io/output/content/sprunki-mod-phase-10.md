**Sprunki Mod Phase 10** is a fan-made version of the renowned music rhythm game Incredibox that allows you to express your creativity by creating your music!

## Sprunki Phase 10: The new fan-made expansion to the Sprunki Incredibox universe

Welcome to Sprunki's Phase 10! This new version of the [Sprunki Incredibox](https://geometrylite.io/sprunki-incredibox) universe claims to allow you to be creative with unique new characters. Many players enjoy Phase 10 because of its diverse characters, each with their unique sound, which will deliver explosive combinations and music that only you can create! In this phase, let us play with sound and come up with fresh musical ideas! 

### How to play

- Drag & drop the 20 various tone icons from the list below onto the characters. 
- You can mute any character and choose which one to play. In this way, your tunes will be unique.
