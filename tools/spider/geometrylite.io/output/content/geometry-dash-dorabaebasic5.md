**Geometry Dash Dorabaebasic5** is a popular [Hard](https://geometrylite.io/tag/hard) level among players due to its gorgeous appearance and hard gameplay. Dorabae designed this level, which is given four stars for difficulty.

## Introduce Geometry Dash Dorabaebasic5

Welcome to Dorabaebasic5's new challenges! This is one of the most popular levels in the [Dorabae-Basic](https://geometrylite.io/tag/dorabae-basic) and [Ruby Pack](https://geometrylite.io/tag/ruby-pack) series. Begin your quest by maneuvering a little cube through Dorabaebasic's five obstacle-filled levels. All of the designs and challenges are linked to the music, thus paying attention to the beat is essential for successfully conquering hurdles. The UFO stage is one of the most difficult sections; to pass, the player must dodge spikes and navigate a tiny passageway. 

### Controls

Press the up arrow key/spacebar or click to jump.
