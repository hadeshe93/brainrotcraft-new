## About Geometry Dash Breeze

Geometry Dash Breeze takes you on an exciting rhythm game adventure. Get ready for unprecedented journeys into a world of breathtaking geometric wonders. You will join a small cube with precise jumps to overcome different terrains. The essential task is that players are not allowed to collide with any [obstacle](https://geometrylite.io/tag/obstacle). Barriers can appear in many different forms, but they are all pointed. Spikes, saw blades, sharp ends of platforms, and something like those all pose deadly dangers.

### Introduce Levels

Geometry Dash Breeze includes many levels with increasing difficulty in the order they appear in the menu. This is an opportunity for gamers to choose a game round that suits their ability to challenge. Besides, you can absolutely participate in practice mode to have checkpoint advantages and fully explore the terrain in less time. However, make sure that the practice rounds will be a favorable premise for the main round. Try to remember the challenges that appear on the map and find ways to overcome them to have a spectacular journey.

## **Control and some tips**

To maneuver the [cube](https://geometrylite.io/tag/cube), you can use the left-click or the spacebar. Note that your character can transform into different states throughout the adventure. Therefore, make sure you can clearly grasp each property of these shapes to have the most accurate command keys.

Here are some tips you can apply to have a more productive round:

- Challenge yourself in practice mode to get familiar with the terrain.
- Refer to the gameplay of previous players.
- Make the most of the rhythmic nature of movement.
