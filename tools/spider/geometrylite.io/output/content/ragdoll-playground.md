## **Ragdoll Playground - Create, Destroy, and Experiment Freely**

Welcome to Ragdoll Playground, a physics-based [sandbox](https://geometrylite.io/tag/sandbox) game that lets your imagination run wild. There are no levels, missions, or limits—only pure experimentation. Drop characters, launch vehicles, detonate explosives, and watch realistic physics unfold in real time. Every reaction feels unique, turning simple ideas into unpredictable mayhem. 

### **The Sandbox Experience**

Ragdoll Playground thrives on freedom. You can build detailed setups or wreak havoc instantly—it’s entirely up to you. Want to test gravity, explosions, or movement mechanics? Place objects, connect them with joints, and see how they interact. The physics system makes every hit, crash, and fall feel satisfyingly real. 

### **Controls**

- Mouse: Select and interact with items
- Arrow Keys: Move around the map
- E / Q: Spawn objects
- F: Activate the selected object
- Hold Left Mouse: Drag objects
- A / D: Rotate objects
- Right Click + Delete / Backspace: Remove objects
