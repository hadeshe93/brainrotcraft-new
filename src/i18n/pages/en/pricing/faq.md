### What are Credits?

Credits are the universal currency for [Bulk Background Remover](/bulk-editor), enabling you to process large batches of images seamlessly. In batch processing, **1 Credit = 1 Image processed**.

Users with Credits can enjoy the following premium features when using [Bulk Background Remover](/bulk-editor):

1. **No Usage Frequency Limits** - Process as many batches as you want within your credit balance, without monthly usage restrictions
2. **Larger Batch Sizes** - Process up to **50 images per batch** (compared to 12 images/batch for free users), with support for even larger batches coming soon
3. **Lightning-Fast Processing** - Complete 50 images in under 1 minute, dramatically boosting your productivity
4. **Keep Free Features** - When your credits run out, you'll still retain all free plan features without losing any functionality

### How long are Credits valid?

Credits purchased are **valid permanently** with no expiration date. Use them anytime at your convenience and plan your usage flexibly.

### Do I need Credits for the Single Image Remover?

**No.** All features in the Single Image Background Remover are **completely free**, including:
- Unlimited usage
- No preview or download restrictions
- No export size limits (supports up to 8K resolution)
- All editing tools and features

Credits are only required for **[Bulk Background Remover (batch processing feature)](/bulk-editor)**.
